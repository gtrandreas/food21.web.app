The ingredients needed to make Easy lemon curry chicken legs with sweet potato and veg :):
  1. Prepare 12 chicken legs
  2. Prepare Half a lemon
  3. Prepare 4 garlic cloves
  4. Get 1 tbsp curry powder
  5. Take 1 tbsp turmeric
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp paprika
  7. Prepare Large Handful cherry tomatoes
  8. Take 1 courgette
  9. Prepare 1 onion
  10. Prepare 2 sweet potatoes
  11. Make ready 2 normal potatoes
  12. Get Salt and pepper
  13. Prepare Oil
  14. Prepare Mayo

Instructions to make Easy lemon curry chicken legs with sweet potato and veg :):
  1. Slash chicken legs
  2. Then add turmeric paprika curry powder garlic oil salt pepper and squeeze the lemon juice in bowl
  3. Put chicken in the bowl and coat
  4. Then put chicken on baking tray and put aside
  5. Cut courgette onion and add tomatoes. Put in oven dish and coat with salt pepper and oil then put aside
  6. Then cut potatoes smaller than roast potatoes size and put in baking tray with little bit of salt and pepper coat in oil
  7. Now put them all in oven at 200c chicken on top then potatoes the veg, should be done in 45 mins until nice and brown. Turn half way through
  8. Serve with a nice dollop of mayo
