The ingredients needed to make Cranberry walnut chicken salad sandwich:
  1. Make ready 1 tsp grill mate chicken seasoning
  2. Prepare 1/2 tsp dales marinade
  3. Get 2 cup diced chicken
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup dried cranberry
  5. Get 1/2 cup walnuts (crushed into small pieces)
  6. Take 1 tsp garlic powder
  7. Get 2/3 cup miracle whip (or your choice)
  8. Prepare 3 tsp white cooking wine
  9. Take 1/2 tsp salt and pepper

Steps to make Cranberry walnut chicken salad sandwich:
  1. Bring chicken and whatever seasonings you like to boil then immediately put on low, cover and simmer for 20min. I used grill mate, dales and 1 tsp salt for mine.
  2. Mix in a medium sized bowl the cranberry, walnuts, garlic powder, wine, salt and pepper and lastly miracle whip.
  3. Add chicken and stir thoroughly, put in fridge and let sit over night. Always better on the second day, lol!
