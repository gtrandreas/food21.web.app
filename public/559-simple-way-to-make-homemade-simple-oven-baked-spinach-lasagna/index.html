The ingredients needed to make Simple Oven Baked Spinach Lasagna:
  1. Get 750 grams. Ricotta Cheese
  2. Take 5 tablespoons Olive Oil
  3. Take 2 Garlic cloves diced
  4. Take 3 cups shredded Mozzarella
  5. Get 400 grams Spinach
  6. Take 1 medium size Egg
  7. Take 2 tablespoons Lemon juice
  8. Take 1 teaspoon Salt
  9. Make ready 1 teaspoon Pepper
  10. Get 2 packaged of Lasagna noodles
  11. Make ready Nutmeg (optional)
  12. Make ready 2 tablespoons Oregano
Instructions to make Simple Oven Baked Spinach Lasagna:
  1. Wash Spinach, drain and set aside.
  2. In a large bowl add the ricotta cheese, salt, pepper, lemon juice, and egg. Combine all ingredients and set aside.
  3. In a large sauce pan, add the olive oil, garlic and oregano, mix together on low heat for 15 minutes and until garlic has soften.
