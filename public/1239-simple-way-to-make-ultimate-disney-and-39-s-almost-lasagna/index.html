Steps to make (Disney's) Almost Lasagna:
  1. Brown meat and drain well. (I normally soak my elbow macaroni during this step and drain them before my meat.)
  2. While meat is cooking, stir together eggs, ricotta, and grated parmesan. (Good time to salt and pepper mixture)
  3. Preheat oven to 350
  4. Mix cooked meat with macaroni, sauce, and optional veggies. Add ricotta mixture and blend well.
  5. Coat a 9x13 baking pan with cooking spray and spread half of macaroni mixture evenly. Top with a cup of mozzarella and a healthy sprinkle of grated parmesan.
  6. Add the remaining macaroni mixture and top with another cup of mozzarella and another sprinkle of parmesan. Cover with foil.
  7. Bake for 20 minutes. Uncover and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes until the cheese on top is lightly browned and bubbly.
