The ingredients needed to make Smoked Whiskey Pickle Grilled Chicken Salad:
  1. Make ready 2 Large boneless skinless chicken breast
  2. Prepare 2 cups baby spinach and Kale mix
  3. Take 4 tomato large wedges
  4. Get 1/4 cup diced cheddar cheese
  5. Take 1/4 cup rough cut white onion
  6. Prepare 4 Spears of Smoked Whiskey Pickles
  7. Prepare Red wine vinegar
  8. Make ready Salad vinegar
  9. Prepare Balsamic vinegar
  10. Take Olive oil
  11. Prepare Pinch spicy salt
  12. Prepare Splash any hot sauce of your liking
