The ingredients needed to make Tomato and Basil Bruschetta:
  1. Get 12 ripe cherry tomatoes
  2. Take 1 medium red onion
  3. Prepare 2 handfuls of basil
  4. Get 1 garlic clove
  5. Prepare 1 Salt to taste
  6. Get 1 French bread stick
  7. Make ready 1/4 tsp thick balsamic vinegar to drizzle over top of each bruschetta
  8. Take 1/4 tsp of extra virgin olive oil or less depending on taste for each bruschetta

Instructions to make Tomato and Basil Bruschetta:
  1. Cut up clove of garlic very finely and add to the bowl .
  2. Cut up cherry tomatoes, onion small dices, and Basil.
  3. Put above ingredients in a bowl together , add a little salt, and a drizzle of olive oil
  4. Mix ingredients together, cover and refrigerate for a few hours.
  5. Now ENJOY!!!
  6. Spoon the bowl of ingredients on to the slices of bread, and drizzle the balsamic vinegar over top.
  8. Heat a griddle pan, slice up stick bread, drizzle each side with some olive oil, when pan is hot put bread in till toasted on each side.
