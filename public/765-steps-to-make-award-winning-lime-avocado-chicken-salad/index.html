The ingredients needed to make Lime Avocado Chicken Salad:
  1. Get 2 cans premium chunk chicken
  2. Prepare 6 ounces sour cream
  3. Get 1 small lime
  4. Take 1 avocado
  5. Get 1/2 bunch cilantro
Instructions to make Lime Avocado Chicken Salad:
  1. Drain chicken and put into medium mixing bowl. Shred
  2. Add sour cream. Mix
  3. Squeeze in juice from lime. Mix
  4. Chop cilantro and mix.
  5. Cut avocado into chunks and mix.
  6. Add salt and pepper as desired.
