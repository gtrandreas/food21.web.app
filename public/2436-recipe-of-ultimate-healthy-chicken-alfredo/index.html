The ingredients needed to make Healthy chicken Alfredo:
  1. Prepare Blackening seasoning
  2. Prepare Boneless chicken breasts
  3. Get on barilla protein pasta (I used angel hair but you can use spaghetti or fettuccine)
  4. Prepare extra vegetables (I used zucchini but you can use some broccoli (just steam or boil them in pasta waster before boiling your pasta)
  5. Take Chopped parsley to garnish
  6. Make ready Low fat Alfredo Sauce
  7. Get chicken broth
  8. Take Lite cream
  9. Take low fat cream cheese (1/2 block)
  10. Prepare garlic minced
  11. Make ready flour
  12. Make ready Salt & pepper
  13. Get adobo seasoning
  14. Get Parmesan cheese

Instructions to make Healthy chicken Alfredo:
  1. Grill or bake chicken at 450° til cooked. Let it slightly cool so you can cut it up
  2. Boil your veggies til almost tender and then add your pasta to cook. Drain and let it sit aside while you make sauce in same pot
  3. Add broth, cream, cheese, garlic, and seasoning to pot. Bring to a simmer and whisk to melt the cream cheese.
  4. Once melted whisk in 2 tbsp of flour to thicken. Let simmer til sauce thickens slightly. Add parsley and Parmesan to finish, enjoy!
