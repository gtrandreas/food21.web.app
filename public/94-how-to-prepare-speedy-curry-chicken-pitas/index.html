The ingredients needed to make Curry Chicken Pitas:
  1. Make ready 2 lb Boneless, Skinless Chicken
  2. Prepare 6 clove Garlic, minced
  3. Prepare 3 tbsp Curry Powder
  4. Make ready 2 tbsp Paprika
  5. Get 2 packages Frozen California Vegetable Blend
  6. Take 1 large Onion, chopped
  7. Get 3 cup Plain Greek Yogurt
  8. Make ready 1 Salt/Pepper seasoning to taste
  9. Make ready 1 Olive Oil
  10. Prepare 1 Sesame Oil
  11. Make ready 1/4 Sesame Seeds
Instructions to make Curry Chicken Pitas:
  1. Set the California mix into a colander and rinse with warm water to defrost, pat dry with paper towel
  2. Start by heating a wok with olive oil and a few drops of sesame oil over medium heat
  3. Season chicken with Garlic, curry, paprika, and salt/pepper in a large bowl
  4. Add the chicken and stir fry about 10 minutes until fully cooked
  5. Add the vegetable mix and onion and cook until veggies are tender but crisp
  6. Add sesame seeds and stir fry about 5 more minutes
  7. Spread yogurt onto pitas
  8. Spoon onto pitas or into pita pockets and serve with rice
