The ingredients needed to make Lemon Yogurt Blueberry Muffins:
  1. Get Ingredients
  2. Make ready 1 3/4 cup flour
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup sugar
  4. Get 2 1/2 tsp baking powder
  5. Prepare 1 egg, beaten
  6. Make ready 3/4 cup milk
  7. Take 1/3 cup cooking oil
  8. Prepare 1/2 cup chopped pecans
  9. Get 1/2 cup blueberries
  10. Prepare 1 cup container lemon yogurt

Steps to make Lemon Yogurt Blueberry Muffins:
  1. mix together dry ingredients in a large bowl
  2. make well in center
  3. mix together milk, egg, oil, yogurt
  4. add to dry ingredients
  5. stir until well blended
  6. mixture should be lumpy
  7. add pecans and blueberries
  8. carefully fold into mixture
  9. fill greased muffin pans.
  10. bake at 400° F for 15- 20 minutes or until golden
  11. NOTE : if using mini muffin pan baking time may be shorter. check after 10 minutes
  12. serve warm and enjoy
