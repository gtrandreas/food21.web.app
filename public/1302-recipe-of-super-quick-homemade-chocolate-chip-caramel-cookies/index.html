The ingredients needed to make Chocolate chip caramel cookies:
  1. Take 2 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  2. Get 1 tsp vanilla extract
  3. Get 1 tsp baking soda
  4. Make ready 1 tsp salt
  5. Get 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  6. Prepare 3/4 cup brown sugar
  7. Take 2 large eggs
  8. Get 2 sticks butter room temp
  9. Get 1 cup semi sweet chocolate chips
  10. Take 1 cup milk chocolate chips
  11. Prepare 1 packages caramel filled chocolate chips toll house
  12. Get If u cant find the toll house caramel use kraft caramel bites

Steps to make Chocolate chip caramel cookies:
  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Cream your butter and sugars together in one bowl, til creamy and combined. Add your eggs, vanilla, to the sugar mixture and combine. Add all your dry in a separate bowl except the chocolate chips and mix. While still beating add the dry a little at a time. Once all combined add your chocolate chips and fold in.
  3. Use two spoons or a cookie scoop and scoop the cookie dough on a parchment lined cookie sheet.
  4. Bake 9-12 minutes. Let cool for a few minutes and once cooled transfer to a cooling rack. This one batch of cookies. It makes 4 doz cookies.
