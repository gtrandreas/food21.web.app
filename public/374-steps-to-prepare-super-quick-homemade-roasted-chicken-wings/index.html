The ingredients needed to make Roasted Chicken Wings ♪:
  1. Make ready 20 Chicken wings
  2. Prepare 150 ml Soy sauce
  3. Prepare 50 ml Sake
  4. Take 1 clove Garlic
  5. Make ready 1 Cherry tomatoes, etc
Instructions to make Roasted Chicken Wings ♪:
  1. Crush the garlic with the flat of a knife. Bring the sake to a boil to evaporate the alcohol. Add the soy sauce, and garlic, then bring to a boil. Let it cool down.
  2. Bring the chicken wings to room temperature. Put the chicken wings in the soy sauce mixture, and massage well. Marinate for 8 minutes while flipping occasionally. Transfer into a sieve.
  3. Preheat the oven to 250℃. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper, and arrange the chicken wings.
  4. Bake in the oven for 5 minutes at 250℃. Turn up the temperature to 300℃, and bake for 3 minutes, then done. Adjust the baking time to suit your oven.
  5. Serve with cold beer!
