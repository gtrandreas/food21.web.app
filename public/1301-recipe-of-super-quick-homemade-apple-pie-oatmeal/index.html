The ingredients needed to make Apple Pie Oatmeal:
  1. Prepare 1/2 cup oatmeal
  2. Prepare 1 cup milk (could be more)
  3. Prepare 1/2 apple (cut into dice)
  4. Get 1/4 cup raisins (I used golden raisins and less than 1/4 cup)
  5. Make ready 1/4 cup pumpkin seeds and sunflower seeds (I used less than it)
  6. Make ready 7 almonds (chopped)
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  8. Take 1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
  9. Get 1/4 tsp salt
  10. Make ready 1 pinch ground cloves (optional)
  11. Take 1/2 tbs honey
  12. Take 1/2 tbs brown sugar
Steps to make Apple Pie Oatmeal:
  1. Boil in medium low heat all the ingredients (except honey), stir them all and add more liquid such as milk or water if needed to reach the consistency that you want.
  2. Simmer around 5 minutes (could be less or more) or until the apple a bit soften.
  3. Garnish with ground cinnamon and a lil bit drizzles of honey (if you use honey for glazing the top, make sure to reduce the brown sugar)
