Instructions to make Spicy penne pasta:
  1. Boil pasta according to package directions.
  2. Heat oil and saute onions and garlic.
  3. Add diced tomatoes and cook for about 7 minutes.
  4. Add salt and green peppers.
  5. Add chicken and cook until tender.
  6. Mix pasta in or pour over it.
  7. Top it off with shredded mozzarella cheese.
