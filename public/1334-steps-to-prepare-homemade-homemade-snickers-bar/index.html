Instructions to make Homemade Snickers bar:
  1. For bottom layer
  2. thoroughly grease 9x13 baking pan
  3. melt ingredients together in sauce pan
  4. pour into baking pan and spread evenly
  5. let cool and harden completely
  6. For second layer
  7. melt butter in sauce pan over medium heat
  8. add in sugar and milk until dissolved and bring to boil
  9. let cook for 5 minutes stirring occasionally
  10. add in fluff, peanut butter, and vanilla, stirring until smooth
  11. turn off heat and fold in peanuts
  12. pour over bottom layer, spread out evenly and cool completely
  13. For third layer
  14. combine ingredients in sauce pan over low heat
  15. let melt, stirring occasionally until smooth
  16. pour over second layer and cool completely
  17. For fourth layer
  18. melt ingredients together in sauce pan
  19. then pour over third layer and cool completely
  20. Finally refridgerate for 1 hour and enjoy!
