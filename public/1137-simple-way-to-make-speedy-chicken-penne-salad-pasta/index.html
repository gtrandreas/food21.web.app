The ingredients needed to make CHICKEN PENNE Salad (pasta):
  1. Get 1 lb Penne (pasta)
  2. Make ready 1/4 cup Olive oil
  3. Take 2 (12 1/2 ounce) cans of chicken
  4. Prepare 1 cup Chopped Celery
  5. Take 1 cup small grape tomatoes or cherry tomatoes cut in halves
  6. Prepare 1 (10 ounce) can of olives
  7. Get 1/2 cup crisp bacon bits
  8. Get 1/4 cup whole raw almonds
  9. Make ready 1 3/4 cup Mayonaise
  10. Make ready 1 tbsp Lemon pepper

Instructions to make CHICKEN PENNE Salad (pasta):
  1. Cook pasta according to al dente instructions. Pasta should be full bodied. Stir in olive oil.
  2. Drain juice from canned chicken and add chicken to pasta.
  3. Add bacon bits and chopped celery.
  4. Add tomatoes, sliced olives, and almonds to salad.
  5. Season salad with lemon pepper.
  6. Stir in mayonaise.
  7. Store finished salad in a covered container in refrigerator to chill before serving.
