The ingredients needed to make YMUSTUHATE my Fried Apple Pies:
  1. Take 1 can Grands flakey biscuits
  2. Get 4 apples
  3. Get 1/2 cup sugar
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  5. Take 1 tsp lemon juice
  6. Make ready 2 tsp butter
  7. Take 1 dash nutmeg
Steps to make YMUSTUHATE my Fried Apple Pies:
  1. Dice apples
  2. add butter & diced apples in a pot and stew 15-20 minutes(until soft)
  3. add dash of nutmeg
  4. mix in: sugar, cinnamon and lemon juice allow it to cook down
  5. roll out Grands flakey biscuits using flour (prevents sticking)
  6. fill dough with Apple filling
  7. rub water on edge of dough
  8. fold over to close, do fork on water to pass and seal
  9. fry on both sides 1-2 mins
