Steps to make cauliflower Alfredo sauce:
  1. cook noodles as directed on box,drain.
  2. in a large skillet saute minced garlic with the butter,cover low heat. cook until garlic is soft,and fragrant, but not browned.remove from heat,set aside.
  3. bring water to a boil,add cauliflower and cook for 7- 10 min or until fork tender.
  4. using a slotted spoon transfer cauliflower to a blender,add broth and milk.puree until sauce is smooth.
  5. add your sauce to the skillet with the garlic.blend well.season with salt n pepper.on low heat cook for 3 min.
  6. serve over fettuccine noodles.garnish with parsley.
