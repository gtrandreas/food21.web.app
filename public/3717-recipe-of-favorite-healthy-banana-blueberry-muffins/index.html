The ingredients needed to make Healthy Banana-Blueberry Muffins:
  1. Prepare 1 cup oats quick - cooking rolled (not instant)
  2. Get ½ cup all-purpose flour
  3. Take ½ cup whole wheat flour
  4. Prepare ½ cup sugar granulated
  5. Get ¼ cup flax seed ground
  6. Prepare 1½ teaspoons baking powder
  7. Take 1 teaspoon baking soda
  8. Take ½ teaspoon salt
  9. Get Optional : 1 tablespoon flax seeds whole
  10. Prepare 1½ cups bananas , mashed (3 large)
  11. Prepare ¼ cup butter , melted
  12. Take 1 egg large
  13. Make ready 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  14. Take 1 cup blueberries fresh or frozen
  15. Take Handful Raspberries

Instructions to make Healthy Banana-Blueberry Muffins:
  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F/ 180°C
  2. Prepare a 12 cup muffin tray by spraying with some cooking spray. Set aside.
  3. Combine dry ingredients in a large bowl. Mix well and set aside.
  4. In another medium sized bowl, mix all the wet ingredients i.e mashed bananas, butter, one egg and Vanilla Extract
  5. Fold in the wet banana mixture into the dry ingredients and mix until all the dry ingredients have been well moistened.
  6. Now gently fold in the blueberries. I prefer the fresh blueberries, but frozen ones work just fine.
  7. Equally divide the batter among 12 muffin cups.
  8. Bake them for approximately 20minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean. (well, we know the drill right). Once done cool them on a wire rack.
