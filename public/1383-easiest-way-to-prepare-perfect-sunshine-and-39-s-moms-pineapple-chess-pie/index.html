The ingredients needed to make sunshine's moms pineapple chess pie:
  1. Prepare 1/2 cup butter
  2. Take 1 1/2 cup sugar
  3. Make ready 4 large eggs
  4. Prepare 3 tbsp all purpose flour
  5. Make ready 1 can 8.oz pineapple crushed
  6. Make ready 1 tsp vanilli extract
  7. Make ready 1 9"unbaked pie shell deep dish
