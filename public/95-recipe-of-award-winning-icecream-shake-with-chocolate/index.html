The ingredients needed to make Icecream shake with chocolate:
  1. Prepare 2 scoops vanilla icecream
  2. Get 2 cup milk
  3. Take 1/2 cup melted chocolate
  4. Get As needed Crushed ice
  5. Get As needed Sprinkles
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp chocolate sauce
  7. Make ready 2-3 marshmallow
  8. Prepare as needed Whipped cream
  9. Get as needed Wafers biscuit/chocolate roll
  10. Prepare 4-5 teaspoon Chico chips
  11. Prepare as needed Cherry

Steps to make Icecream shake with chocolate:
  1. Take chocolate sauce in a mug and make pattern by turning it
  2. For making rim take sprinkles in plate
  3. Apply butter cream icing or whip cream on the mug rim
  4. Roll it in sprinkles
  5. Take icecream,ice,milk and chocolate in mixi and make shake
  6. Pour it in mug and add marshmallow with light hand
  7. Pipe whipped cream upon it
  8. Garnish with sprinkles,cherry,chocolate roll,and chocolate chips
  9. Serve cold this yummy shake
