The ingredients needed to make Coffee Brownie Ice Cream Bars:
  1. Make ready For Brownie Layer
  2. Get 4 ounces unsalted butter (1 stick)
  3. Make ready 4 ounces unsweetened chocolate, chopped
  4. Make ready 4 latge eggs
  5. Prepare 2 cups granulated sugar
  6. Take 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  7. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon salt
  8. Make ready 1 cup all purpose flour
  9. Take For Ice Cream Layer
  10. Get 1/2 gallon chocolate chip ice cream
  11. Take 1 cup heavy cream
  12. Get 2 tablespoons instant coffee granules
  13. Take 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  14. Get For Chocolate Layer
  15. Make ready 1 cup heavy cream
  16. Get 7 ounces Lindt Exellence Extra Creamy Milk Chocolate, chopped
  17. Prepare For Topping
  18. Make ready 1 1/2 cups cold heavy whipping Cream
  19. Get 1/4 cup confectioner's sugar

Instructions to make Coffee Brownie Ice Cream Bars:
  1. Make Brownies
  2. Spray a 9 by 13 inch pan with bakers spray. Line the pan with foil with ends extending over sides for easy removal of cooled brownies. Spray foil with bakers spray
  3. Melt chocolate and butter in a microwave safe bowl just until melted and smooth. Cool to room temperature.
  4. In a bowl beat eggs, vanilla and sugar until blended
  5. Add flour and salt and stir in
  6. Add chicolate mixture and stir iust until uniform in color
  7. Spread into prepared pan and bake 20 to 25 minutes just until a toothpick comes out with moist crumbs. Cool completely on a rack
  8. Make Ice cream Layer
  9. Heat cream to a boil, add sugar and coffee and stir to dissolve, refrigerate until cpld
  10. Soften ice cream about 15 minutes at room temperature, transfer to a large bowl
  12. Working quickly beat ice cream with cold coffee cream
  13. Imediately spread evenly over cooled brownies and freeze until hard
  14. Make Chocolate Layer
  15. Heat cream until just under a boil and pour over chocolate, let stand 1 minute then stir smooth. Let rest 10 minutes
  16. Now spread over frozen ice cream layer. Return to freezer to harden. Then cover with whipped cream toppimg
  17. Make Whipped Cream Toppong
  18. Beat cream until soft peaks form
  19. Add sugar and vanilla and beat until it holds its shape
  20. Spread evenly over chocolate layer and freeze until hard
  21. Garnish with Cholate Coffee Toffee Bark
  22. Remove entire dessert from pan by lifting out with foil
  23. Cut into bars
