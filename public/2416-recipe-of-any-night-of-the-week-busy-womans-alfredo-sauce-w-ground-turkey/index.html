Steps to make BUSY WOMANS ALFREDO SAUCE w/ ground turkey:
  1. *** put ground beef on first and break apart… then add turkey, celery, & onion…
  2. put ground meat and spices. When meat is no longer pink. drain in colander for about 15 minutes … Return to pot after rinsing.
  3. put pot on with water, oil, salt in the water. when it begins cooking add pasta. let pasta boil until al's dente… add broccoli to noodles the last 10 minutes of cooking noodles. turn off and drain. run under cold water to stop the cooking process.
  4. while pasta cooks put Alfredo sauce, 1/2 cup cream, butter in a sauce pan… when it begins bubbling add room tempature, cream cheese, butter, milk, sour cream, and spices… let the new ingredients melt to form a thick creamy sauce..
  5. add another 1/2 cup heavy cream add parmesan cheese… and allow to simmer on low. add more as needed to thin…
  6. mix meat, sauce and noodles together evenly coated… add a little sauce at a time to coat. if there is sauce I serve it with my bread as a dipping sauce.
  7. - I usually make a meatless salad and toasted Italian bread with herbal butter and melted parmesan …I'll post that recipe also..
