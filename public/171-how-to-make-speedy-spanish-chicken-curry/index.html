The ingredients needed to make Spanish chicken curry:
  1. Make ready 500 grm chicken
  2. Make ready 2 tbs Chilli powder
  3. Take 1 tbs Dry oregano
  4. Prepare 1 tsp cumin powder
  5. Prepare 1 Onion (thinly sliced)
  6. Get 2 tbs Tomato puree
  7. Make ready 1 Yellow bell pepper (cut into cube)
  8. Take 1 tbs Apple cider vinegar
  9. Make ready 1/2 cup Coconut milk
  10. Get 1 tsp Turmeric powder
  11. Prepare to taste Salt
  12. Make ready 4 tbs Olive oil
  13. Get Knob butter
  14. Get 1 tsp Sugar

Steps to make Spanish chicken curry:
  1. Wash meat, add turmeric powder, chilli powder, salt and marinate for 30 min
  2. In a deep pan pour 4tbs oil, add onion and fry until golden brown
  3. Now add chicken and cook in a medium flame for 15 min. Stir often. Add tomato puree, oregano, cumin powder, bell pepper and cook for 5 min,
  4. Add 1 cup of water, salt and cook in low flame until chicken become soft
  5. Now add coconut milk, and vinegar, and cook for 5 min, and remove the pan
  6. Add knob of butter and sugar and your chicken curry is ready to serve
