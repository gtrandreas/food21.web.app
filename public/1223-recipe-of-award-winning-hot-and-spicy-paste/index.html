The ingredients needed to make Hot and Spicy Paste:
  1. Take 1 cup hearts of zucchini.(from the stuffed vegetables recipe)
  2. Prepare 1 large onion
  3. Prepare 1/3 cup chopped chillis.
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup sweet soy souse.
  5. Take 1 pinch salt
  6. Get 5 tbsp oil
  7. Make ready 1/2 cup water
