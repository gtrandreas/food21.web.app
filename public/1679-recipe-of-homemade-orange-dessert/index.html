Instructions to make Orange dessert:
  1. At first,take orange and cut the surface.
  2. Then, remove the orange pulp clearly without tear the peel.
  3. Keep it in mixer and grind.
  4. After that,ready the orange juice.
  5. Strain the seeds from pulp.
  6. Then,take it in pan
  7. Then,add cornflour and sugar in the pulp.
  8. Whisk them together during 5 mins.
  9. Then,put it in gas and stir well
  10. When the mixture will thick and sticky.
  11. Next,turn off the gas.
  12. Keep the pan aside.
  13. Pour the mixture in the empty orange peel.
  14. After pouring,keep the dessert for 10 hrs in freeze.
  15. Lastly,after freezing,serve the dessert.
