The ingredients needed to make Roasted chicken leg tikka:

  2. Prepare 1 small bowl hung curd
  3. Take 1 tbsp cornflour
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp lime juice

Instructions to make Roasted chicken leg tikka:
  1. First in a bowl put chicken pieces now properly wash it then add all given ingredients and spice and 1 tbsp mustard oil, salt as per taste mix it well.
  2. Now cover and keep it in the fridge for 2 hours.
  3. After 2 hours take out from the fridge and set a room temperature.
  4. Now take a microwave-safe baking tray then put marinate pieces and brush with remaining oil
  5. Now keep it in preheated microwave (convection mode) for20 min at 200 c now grill it for 5 mins.
  6. Now take out from microwave and remove the plates and serve with onion salad.
  7. Now it is ready.
