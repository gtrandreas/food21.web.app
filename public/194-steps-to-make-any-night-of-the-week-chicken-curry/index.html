The ingredients needed to make Chicken Curry:
  1. Get 500 gm Chicken (washed)
  2. Make ready 2 Onions (diced)
  3. Get 1 Big Tomato (grated)
  4. Prepare 2 Tbsp Oil
  5. Make ready 3 Tbsp Coriander Powder
  6. Get 1 Tbsp red chilli powder
  7. Get 1 Tsp Turmeric Powder
  8. Get 1 Tsp Garam Masala
  9. Take 1 Tbsp Ginger Garlic Paste
  10. Get To taste Salt
  11. Make ready As needed Water
Instructions to make Chicken Curry:
  1. In a pan add oil fry ginger garlic paste,after few mins add onions and cook until translucent,add tomato let it cook
  2. Add chicken and cook until it releases water,add all dry ingredients,cover n cook
  3. Add water if necessary cook on slow for 10 minutes and off fire
