The ingredients needed to make Chicken Pasta with Cucumber Salad:
  1. Prepare 2 each cucumber
  2. Get 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  3. Make ready 1/2 packages pasta
  4. Make ready 4 each boneless skinless chicken breast
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp fresh dill
  6. Prepare 3 each green onion
  7. Prepare 2 dash garlic salt
  8. Take 1/2 tbsp lemon juice
  9. Take 1 dash sage
  10. Make ready 1 dash onion powder
  11. Get 1 dash celery salt
  12. Make ready 1 dash salt
  13. Take 2 dash ground black pepper
  14. Prepare 2 dash ground ginger
  15. Prepare 1 tbsp sugar
  16. Make ready 3 can 10 3/4 cans of cream of chicken soup
  17. Prepare 2 each olive oil, extra virgin
  18. Take 1 olive oil, extra virgin
  19. Make ready 1 boiling water
Instructions to make Chicken Pasta with Cucumber Salad:
  1. Cut fat off chicken
  2. Dice chicken
  3. Wash hands twice.
  4. Boil two pots of water half full
  5. Add pasta to one pot
  6. Add chicken to the other pot
  7. Peel and slice cucumbers
  8. Place cucumbers in bowl
  9. Stir both pots on stove
  10. Add all spices , mayo, herbs, lemon juice, sugar, salt, pepper to cucumbers
  11. Slice thinly green onions
  12. Add green onions to cucumbers
  13. Stir til all mixed well cucumbers bowl
  14. Stir pots on stove
  15. Strain excess dressing from cucumbers and put bowl in fridge
  16. If chicken is all white, even when a piecce is cut removed POT from burner and turn off that burner
  17. When pasta is done strain it
  18. Put pasta back in pot
  19. Put POT back on stove
  20. Add cream of chicken soup to pasta
  21. Stir pasta welk
  22. Drain chicken
  23. Add chicken to pasta
  24. Mix well
  25. Cook pasta on low til it looks like just a pasta
  26. Serve pasta and cucumber salad
  27. Season pasta to taste! Will be bland!
