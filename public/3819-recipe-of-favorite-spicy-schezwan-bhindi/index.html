Steps to make Spicy schezwan bhindi:
  1. Wash and dry ladyfingers, cut both tips. Chop onion finely, cut tomato and capsicum into dices. Cut chillies and ginger into small pieces.
  2. Heat oil in pan add onion and saute for 2 minutes, add chilli and ginger saute for 1 minute. Add ladyfinger saute for 1 minute, cover lid and cook for 8-10 minutes on low medium flame. Stir occasionally.
  3. Now add tomato & capsicum saute for 1 minute, add turmeric powder, coriander cumin powder, chilli flakes & salt. Stir well. Now add schezwan fried rice masala mix and cook for 2 minutes. Spicy schezwan bhindi is ready to serve.
