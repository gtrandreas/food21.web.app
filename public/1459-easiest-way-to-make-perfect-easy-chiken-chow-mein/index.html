The ingredients needed to make Easy_CHIKEN_Chow_Mein:
  1. Prepare 1CUP CHICKEN CUBES 12 ounces cooked chow mein noodles
  2. Make ready 2 cups thinly sliced green cabbage
  3. Take 1 cup thinly sliced capsicum
  4. Make ready 1 cup thinly sliced white onion
  5. Make ready 3 tablespoon s oil
  6. Get sauce
  7. Get 1/4 cup soy sauce
  8. Get 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  9. Take 2 teaspoon garlic paste
  10. Prepare 1/8 teaspoon ginger paste
  11. Prepare ⅛ teaspoon black pepper
  12. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon Salt

Steps to make Easy_CHIKEN_Chow_Mein:
  2. Heat oil over medium heat in large skillet or pan. - * Add onions and capsicum and sauté 3-4 minutes until onions become clear.
  3. Add Chicken SAUTE 5 MINUTES
  4. Add ginger garlic paste
  5. In a medium bowl whisk together sauce ingredients. - Add sauce to pan
  6. ADD cabbage and sauté 1-2 minutes longer til cabbage is tender
  7. Add noodles and stir to coat noodles in the sauce.
  8. Serve warm. Enjoy
