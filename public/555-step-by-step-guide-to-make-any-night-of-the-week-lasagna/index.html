Steps to make Lasagna:
  2. Brown ground beef, chop some onion and garlic add to meat.
  3. Mix cooked meat into a bowl add pasta sauce. *Save a little sauce to add to the top layer of lasagna. *Put a layer of sauce on bottom of baking dish.
  4. Combine Ricotta and eggs in bowl and mix until smooth
  5. Add cheese mixture to top of noodles, *Next spread on meat sauce, sprinkle layer of mozzarella cheese, *Lay next layer of noodles and repeat cheese spread meat sauce mixture until dish full.
  6. Add remaining sauce to top. Noodle and sauce only for final top layer.
  7. Cover with foil Bake 350° for about 45 min to an hour.
  8. Let cool, Enjoy
