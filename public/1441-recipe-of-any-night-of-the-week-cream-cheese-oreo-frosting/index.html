The ingredients needed to make Cream cheese oreo frosting:
  1. Get 1 cup powdered sugar
  2. Take 1 cup cream cheese ( room temp )
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup butter ( room temp )
  4. Get 1 tsp pure vanilla extract
  5. Take 2 packages regular sized oreos
  6. Make ready 2 packages mini oreos

Instructions to make Cream cheese oreo frosting:
  1. Cream together cream cheese and butter until combined…sift powdered suger and add a little at a time to cream and butter …..add vanilla and mix well
  2. Crush oreos ( the best way is to put it in a food processor or mixer )
  3. Add the crushed oreos to the mix and blend well …if u like your frosting creamy rather than sugery put as much suger as u like ….but if u like it sugery u could add more.
  4. This frosting works best with thd double chocolate chip muffins i made….or any kind of muffin or cupcake
  5. For decoration…before baking the cupcake or muffins you are going to put frosting on , put an oreo in the bottom of each muffin …and when ready to decorate pipe the frosting and make sure that there aren't any big chunks if oreos in it or it with get cloged.
