The ingredients needed to make Blueberry Muffins:
  1. Prepare 8 Tbsps butter , soft . (1 stick)
  2. Take 1 cup sugar
  3. Prepare 1/2 Tsp salt .
  4. Take 2 eggs
  5. Take 2 Tsps baking powder .
  6. Get 2 cups all-purpose flour (8.5 oz.)
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup milk
  8. Make ready 1 Tsp vanilla extract .
  9. Get 2 1/2 cups blueberries fresh , frozen or dried
  10. Prepare 2 Tsps sugar . for topping

Instructions to make Blueberry Muffins:
  1. Preheat the oven to 375F. Generously grease a muffin pan, or prepare muffin cups. Grease the top of the pan as well to keep the muffin tops from sticking.
  2. Cream together the butter, sugar and salt. Add the eggs and mix well. Add the vanilla and mix.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour and baking powder.
  4. Alternately add some of the flour and some of the milk to the butter/sugar mix, mixing after each addition. Batter should be thicker than pancake batter, but thinner than cookie dough.
  5. Fold in the blueberries and stir until just combined.
  6. Divide the batter evenly between 12 muffin cups. Sprinkle muffin tops with sugar.
  7. Bake for 30 minutes. Cool muffins in the pan for 5 minutes, then invert the pan and slap the muffins out onto a clean counter.
  8. Transfer muffins to a cooling rack.
