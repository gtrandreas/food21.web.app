The ingredients needed to make Gluten free/ corn free Pumpkin pie cake:
  1. Prepare 2 1/4 cups gluten free flour (doesn't have xanthan gum)
  2. Take 1 1/2 cups raw sugar
  3. Make ready 2 teaspoons cream of tarter
  4. Take 1 teaspoon sea salt
  5. Get 1 teaspoon baking soda
  6. Get 1 stick salted butter
  7. Take 4 eggs
  8. Make ready 30 oz canned pumpkin pie mix
  9. Prepare 2 teaspoons pumpkin pie spice
