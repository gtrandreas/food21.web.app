The ingredients needed to make Spaghetti & Meat Sauce:
  1. Get 1 lb spaghetti
  2. Get 1 1/2 lb ground beef
  3. Prepare 1 large can whole tomatoes, crushed
  4. Get 1 small can tomato paste
  5. Get 2 teaspoons basil
  6. Make ready 1 teaspoons thyme
  7. Prepare to taste kosher salt
  8. Prepare to taste course black pepper
  9. Get 1 Tbsp brown sugar
  10. Make ready 2 Tbsp olive oil
  11. Get 1 cup dry parmesan cheese
  12. Take 1 lb shredded cheese
