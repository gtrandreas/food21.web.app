The ingredients needed to make Tres leche (Milk cake):
  1. Get 1 cup all purpose flour
  2. Make ready 1 and 1/2 tsp baking powder
  3. Make ready 1/4 tsp salt
  4. Prepare 5 large eggs
  5. Prepare 1 cup sugar
  6. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla extract
  7. Take For the cake syrup filling(milk mixture)
  8. Make ready 1 cup evaporated milk
  9. Get 1/2 cup condensed milk
  10. Get 2 cup whole milk
  11. Prepare For the frosting
  12. Prepare 1 cup whipping cream powder
  13. Prepare 1/2 cup cold milk

Steps to make Tres leche (Milk cake):
  1. Mix your dry ingredients together and keep aside.
  2. In a bowl add your eggs and sugar and whisk until fluffy. Then add vanilla extract and mix. Add ur flour mixture and then mix gently. Then put in your baking pan and put into your pre heated oven and bake for 15 mins.
  3. For the milk mixture, put your whole milk, condensed milk and evaporated milk in a bowl and mix.
  4. To make the whipped cream frosting, pour your whipped cream powder in a bowl and 1/2 cup cold milk and mix until it is stiff.
  5. Once cake is done, allow to cook for 5 mins. Then use a fork and make holes all over the cake(so that the milk mixture soaked into cake) then pour over your milk mixture. Then use ur frosting and decorate the top of your cake. Serve and enjoy. Decorate with strawberries, chocolate syrup, cinnamon sugar and any other things.
