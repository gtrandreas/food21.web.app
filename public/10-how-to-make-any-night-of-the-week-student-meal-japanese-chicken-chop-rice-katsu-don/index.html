The ingredients needed to make Student Meal: Japanese Chicken Chop Rice (Katsu Don):
  1. Take 1 pc Chicken Thigh
  2. Make ready 1 cup Cooked Rice
  3. Make ready 1 cup Cooking Oil (shallow frying)
  4. Get 1/2 cup Panko breadcrumbs
  5. Make ready Marinade (mix in a bowl);
  6. Get 1 pc Egg
  7. Prepare 2 Tbsp Corn Flour
  8. Make ready 1-2 g Salt (1 or 2 pinch)
  9. Prepare Sauce
  10. Get 1/3 cup Water
  11. Prepare 1 Tbsp Kikkoman Soy Sauce
  12. Prepare 1 g Dashi (1 pinch)
  13. Make ready 70 g Onions (slice or chopped)
  14. Make ready 30 g Spring Onions (chopped finely, garnish)
  15. Prepare Optional;
  16. Make ready 2 tsp Vinegar
  17. Get 1 tsp Sugar
Instructions to make Student Meal: Japanese Chicken Chop Rice (Katsu Don):
  1. CHICKEN; Marinate the chicken with the egg, flour, salt and pepper for 30 minutes minimum. Prepare 1 cup of cooking oil on medium heat for shallow frying. When ready, coat the chicken in Panko and fry it 2-3minutes each side or until golden brown. Remove and set aside.
  2. SAUCE; In a saucepan, add water, soy sauce, dashi, (vinegar and sugar if you want), as well as onions. Bring it to a boil, then cover with a lid and cook for 1 minute.
  3. ASSEMBLY; Next, lay the chicken flat on top of the onions and pour in the egg. Cover with a lid and cook for 10 seconds. Turn off the heat. Prepare a bowl of rice and lay the chicken and the sauce on top of the rice to serve. Garnish with spring onions.
