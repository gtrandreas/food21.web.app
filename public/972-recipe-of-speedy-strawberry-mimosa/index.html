The ingredients needed to make Strawberry mimosa:
  1. Make ready 1 tbsp Raspberries,Frozen
  2. Prepare 1 tsp Strawberries
  3. Make ready 1/2 tsp white sugar to taste
  4. Prepare 1-2 drop vanilla essence or vanilla extract
  5. Get 1 glass soda or sprite/ Champagne
  6. Make ready Strawberry for garnish
  7. Take 4-5 ice cubes

Instructions to make Strawberry mimosa:
  1. In a medium sauce pan combine raspberries and strawberries diced.
  2. Heat on medium heat until soft.
  3. Add sugar cook for 5-6 minutes.
  4. Cool for 5 minutes, Blend or puree, Strain.
  5. Pour into the glass add soda or champagne & ice cubes.
  6. Garnish with strawberry serve chilled.
