Instructions to make Oatmeal blueberry muffins:
  1. Preheat oven to 375
  2. Add rolled oats to a bowl.
  3. Add milk and stir.
  4. In another bowl sift together flour,baking powder,salt and sugar.
  5. Add egg and oil to milk mixture and stir well.
  6. Pour into dry ingrediants and add blueberries.
  7. Optional : coat top of muffins with a pinch of brownsugar .
  8. Optional : mix 2 tbs of jam into mixture for extra fruity flavor.
