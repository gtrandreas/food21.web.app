The ingredients needed to make Roasted Chicken Simple Delicious:
  1. Get 3 1/2 lb Whole chicken
  2. Get 1 tbsp Minced Garlic
  3. Take 1 medium Onion
  4. Make ready 2 stick Stalk scallion
  5. Make ready 2 tsp Salt
  6. Get 1 tsp Garlic Powder
  7. Take 1 tsp Season Salt
  8. Prepare 1/2 bunch Black Pepper
  9. Make ready 1 tsp Cayenne Pepper
  10. Make ready 1 tbsp Olive oil
  11. Prepare 2 clove Garlic
Instructions to make Roasted Chicken Simple Delicious:
  1. Clean chicken, removing extra fast and rinse then pat dry
  2. Make slices into the onion but not let it not break apart. Cut the scallions in to half
  3. Sprinkle 1 tsp of salt into the cavity of the chicken and stuff it with the onion, garlic and scallion
  4. Sprinkle the remaining season on the chicken along with the garlic. You can lift the skin at the breast and get some of the season and garlic under there.
  5. Place chicken in a baking pan breast side down, cover and let it bake for 45 mins at 400f, then you uncover your chicken and gently flip it on its back, brush with olive oil and let roast for another 1hr 15 min at 375°F
  6. To make gravy, you can pour a little warn water in the pan after removing the chicken, use a plastic spoon to get all those dripping into a sauce pan, add enough corn start to thicken and let simmer.
