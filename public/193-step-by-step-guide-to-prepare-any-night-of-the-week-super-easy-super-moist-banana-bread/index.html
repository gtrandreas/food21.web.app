The ingredients needed to make super easy, super moist banana bread:
  1. Prepare 3 ripe bananas
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup melted butter
  3. Take 1 1/2 cup flour
  4. Prepare 1 cup sugar
  5. Make ready 2 eggs
  6. Prepare 1 tsp baking soda
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp banana extract
