The ingredients needed to make Cast Iron Dark Chocolate And Coffee Brownies:
  1. Make ready 1 cup brown sugar
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  3. Make ready 3 tsp instant nescafe granules
  4. Take 2 eggs
  5. Get 1 tsp vanilla extract
  6. Get 1/2 cup all purpose flour
  7. Prepare 1/4 tsp baking powder
  8. Take 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  9. Prepare 1/4 tsp cinnamon powder
  10. Get 1/4 tsp salt
  11. Take 1/2 cup pecans chopped
  12. Get 1/2 cup + 1/3 cup semi sweet chocolate chips
Instructions to make Cast Iron Dark Chocolate And Coffee Brownies:
  1. On low heat in a heavy bottomed pan melt butter along with sugar and nescafe granules.Turn heat off add chocolates chips(1/2 cup) and mix until the chocolates melt completely.
  2. Add eggs along with vanilla extract and whisk along with a balloon whisk
  3. Shift together flour, cocoa powder, baking powder,cinnamon powder and salt.Stir in the chocolate chips (1/3 cup)and the pecans
  4. Add the flour in the chocolate - butter mixture and gently fold in.
  5. Grease a 9" skillet with.Pour the brownie batter in and bake in a preheated oven at 180 degree for 15 - 20 until just set minutes
  6. Remove to a cooling rack and let col before slicing and serving
  7. Best had with a cup of tea.
