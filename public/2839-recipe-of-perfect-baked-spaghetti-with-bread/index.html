The ingredients needed to make Baked Spaghetti with Bread:
  1. Make ready Bread
  2. Make ready store baked Italian bread
  3. Get butter divided
  4. Take granulated garlic powder
  5. Prepare kosher salt to sprinkle
  6. Prepare Spaghetti
  7. Make ready cooked spaghetti pasta
  8. Take ground beef
  9. Prepare canned diced tomatoes
  10. Take canned tomato sauce
  11. Make ready flame roasted peppers
  12. Take sliced kalamata olives
  13. Get capers
  14. Take sugar
  15. Prepare salt
  16. Get ground white pepper
  17. Prepare granulated garlic powder
  18. Make ready tomato paste
  19. Take diced onion
  20. Make ready Topping
  21. Make ready shredded Parmesan cheese
  22. Prepare Gouda cheese
Steps to make Baked Spaghetti with Bread:
  1. Preheat oven 400°Fahrenheit. Add ground beef to a deep oven safe pan. Add the onion, peppers, olives, capers, and spices. Add tomato sauce, paste, and diced tomatoes. Simmer 10 minutes stir in the pasta.
  2. Get the gouda and slice. Add all over the top of the spaghetti. Sprinkle the parmesan over the top. Put into oven for 1 hour.
  3. Slice the bread melt the butter. Add the sliced bread and fry the bread in the butter.
  4. Turn and add the garlic. Toast the back side till browned and crispy. Sprinkle with kosher salt and set aside. Take the spaghetti out of the oven and let rest 15 minutes.
  5. Serve, I hope you enjoy!!!
