The ingredients needed to make Easy Chicken Alfredo Lasagna:
  1. Get Chicken Alfredo Ingredients
  2. Make ready Butter
  3. Take onion, chopped
  4. Get (12.5 oz.) Chunk chicken breast, drained
  5. Take 14.5oz. Fire Roasted Tomatos, drained
  6. Make ready mozzerella
  7. Prepare 15 oz. jar of Four cheese (or your preferrence) Alfredo sauce
  8. Make ready whole wheat (or regular) uncooked lasagna noodles
  9. Prepare Frozen chopped spinach, rinsed in colander, squeeze out water.
Instructions to make Easy Chicken Alfredo Lasagna:
  1. Set your oven to 350¬∞. While it is warming up, chop your onions and fry them in the 2 T. butter until thay are a little browned.
  2. Add to the onions, everything except for the mozzarella cheese and lasagna noodles. Incorporate everything well. Continue to cook for a couple of minutes.
  3. Grease a 10√ó13 pan, then spoon a little of the Alfredo mixture onto the bottom of the pan. Lay three pieces of lasagna noodles in the pan, spoon a layer of Alfredo sauce, add a layer of mozzarella, then noodles again. keep layering with noodles, sauce, cheese. Make sure you stop with a layer of sauce and cheese on top. :)
  4. Top your Alfredo Lasagna dish with aluminum foil and bake for 35 minutes. Remove foil and bake another 5 minutes.
