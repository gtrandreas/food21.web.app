Instructions to make Healthy chocolate jam brownie:
  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour, baking powder, cocoa, flax, salt and cinnamon. Whisk until light and fluffy: very much the consistency of sifted powdered sugar.
  2. In a smaller bowl, mix together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Add beaten eggs and soy milk.
  3. Create a well in the center of the flour mixture and pour in the egg mixture. Stir until it just comes together and fold in the chocolate chips.
  4. Butter a 9x13" baking pan and dust it with flour. Once sufficiently covered, pour in the brownie batter.
  5. Heat the jam in the microwave for about a minute until its consistency turns runny. Spoon some of the jam atop the brownie batter. Use a knife to swirl the batter and the jam together, taking care not to mix the two together completely.
  6. Bake in a 350 degrees oven for about 25-30 minutes, or until browned and a toothpick inserted into the center of the pan comes out clear. Enjoy warm with no-sugar-added ice cream or with a cold glass of milk.
