The ingredients needed to make Bhindi nu shak lady finger sabji:
  1. Take 1/2 kg lady finger
  2. Prepare 2 tablespoon Oil
  3. Prepare 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  4. Get 2 green chillies
  5. Prepare 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds
  6. Get 3-4 garlic cloves
  7. Make ready to taste Salt
  8. Take 1/2 teaspoon Coriander powder
  9. Take 1/2 teaspoon Garam masala
  10. Get 2 teaspoon Kashmiri chilli powder
  11. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon Curry powder

Steps to make Bhindi nu shak lady finger sabji:
  1. Heat oil and add cumin seeds and crushed chillies and garlic.
  2. Add chopped lady finger and saut for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add salt, coriander and garam masala powder.
  4. Mix and saute again, cover and cook until vegetable becomes little soft.
  5. Add chilli and curry powder. Saute for 2 minutes and it's ready to go.
  6. Serve hot with dal-rice or roti.
