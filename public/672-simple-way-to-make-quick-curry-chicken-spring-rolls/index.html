The ingredients needed to make Curry chicken spring rolls:
  1. Get 0.5 pound chicken breast
  2. Prepare 20 sheet spring rolls paper
  3. Take 1 onion
  4. Prepare 1 potato
  5. Make ready 1/2 carrot
  6. Make ready 2 tsp curry powder
  7. Take 1 tsp salt
  8. Take 1 tsp black pepper powder
  9. Make ready 2 tbsp oil
  10. Take 2 tbsp milk

Steps to make Curry chicken spring rolls:
  1. Ingredients
  2. Diced onion, carrot,potato and chicken in small cube
  3. Add 2 tbsp oil over medium heat then add onion
  4. Add chicken and leave over heat until cooked
  5. Add carrot and potato
  6. Add curry powder, salt, pepper powder and milk. Then mix it for about 2 mins
  7. Put curry chicken on rolls paper and rolls
  8. Roll.
  9. Rolls.. Done
  10. Add oil to low medium heat until hot then fry spring rolls until golden yellow. Done
  11. Dip with sweet chilli sauce! Yummy!
