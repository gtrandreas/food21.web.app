The ingredients needed to make Double Chocolate Chip cookies:
  1. Take 2 cups all purpose flour
  2. Get 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  3. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon salt
  4. Prepare 1 cup packed brown sugar
  5. Get 1/2 stick melted butter
  6. Take 1 egg
  7. Take 1 egg yolk
  8. Get 1 tablespoon vanilla extract or powder
  9. Take 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  10. Prepare 3 tablespoons condensed milk
  11. Make ready 1/4 cup milk
  12. Prepare 1 cup white or chocolate chips

Steps to make Double Chocolate Chip cookies:
  1. Mix all the dry ingredients in a medium bowl and the chocolate chips
  2. In a separate small bowl mix the brown sugar, white sugar, melted butter, egg, egg yolk, vanilla powder or extract, condensed milk, and milk
  3. Mix all the ingredients together in a large mixing bowl
  4. Scoop the mixture into a baking tray and bake for 8 minutes
  5. Take your chocolate chip cookies out and let them cool for 10 minutes
  6. After they cool place them on a plate and ✨Enjoy!✨
