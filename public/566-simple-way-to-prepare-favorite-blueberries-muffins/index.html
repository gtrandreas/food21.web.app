The ingredients needed to make Blueberries Muffins:
  1. Get 1 1/4 cup Bizquit Mixing
  2. Make ready 1/4 cup Sugar
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup Milk
  4. Take 1 tsp Vegetable oil
  5. Take 1 Egg
  6. Take 1 1/2 dozen Blueberries
Instructions to make Blueberries Muffins:
  1. Preheat oven on 350°F
  2. Mix the egg, sugar, milk, oil and bizquit mixing all together
  3. When all the ingredients are mixed together put a dozen of the blueberries till they are very shattered and mixed with the other ingredients.
  4. Grease a muffins pan, with a litle of vegetable oil or with a non stick spray.
  5. Put your mixing in to the pan and add a couple of blueberries in each of the holes .
  6. Put it inside the oven for 15 to 20 mins
