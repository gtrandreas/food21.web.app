The ingredients needed to make Soy Spaghetti Meatballs,& Peppers:
  1. Prepare 2 lbs ground beef
  2. Take 2 lbs spaghetti
  3. Make ready 1 lg. yellow onion (chopped)
  4. Make ready 6 sm Portobello mushrooms (chopped)
  5. Prepare 1 halves each yellow & red bell peppers (chopped)
  6. Get 2 garlic cloves (chopped)
  7. Make ready Kikkmon soy sauce
  8. Take kosher salt
  9. Make ready course black pepper
  10. Make ready olive oil

Steps to make Soy Spaghetti Meatballs,& Peppers:
  1. In a lg pot add, water and, kosher salt then boil add pasta give a quick stir cover and, let boil.
  2. In a lg skillet add, olive oil, onions, garlic, peppers,soy sauce mushrooms let cook down
  3. Season beef with salt and, pepper then, add them to skillet cook them through drain and, add them to the soy mixture
  4. Saving some of the pasta water drain spaghetti then, gradually add spaghetti mix well spaghetti should turn brown from soy sauce, enjoy.
