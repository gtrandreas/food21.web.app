Steps to make Fried fish restaurant style with salad,mash potato and dips:
  1. First of all wash the fish and marinate with turmeric powder and ginger garlic paste for half hour at least
  2. Then after marination add red chilli flakes,black pepper,salt
  3. Then coat in egg and then in bread crums and deep fry in medium flame
  4. For the dip :- mix all the ingredients together in a bowl
  5. For salad:- shred all the vegetables add lil bit salt and pepper and mayonnaise
  6. For mash potato:-. Boil potatoes then mash it add butter salt and black pepper mix it all together
  7. Serve hot and enjoy
