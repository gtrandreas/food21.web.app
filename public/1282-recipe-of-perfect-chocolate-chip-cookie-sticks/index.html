The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Chip Cookie Sticks:
  1. Make ready 1/2 cup canola oil
  2. Make ready 1/4 cup creamy peanut butter
  3. Take 1/2 cup sugar
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup light brown sugar
  5. Get 1 large egg
  6. Get 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  7. Take 1 & 1/2 cups flour
  8. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  9. Get 1/2 teaspoon salt
  10. Get 1/2 cup peanut butter chips
  11. Get 1 cup semi sweet chocolate chips
Instructions to make Chocolate Chip Cookie Sticks:
  1. Preheat oven to 375º. Line a bakung sheet with parchment paper. Set aside.
  2. In a large mixing bowl. Combine the oil, peanut butter, sugars, egg, vanilla. Cream together. In another bowl Combine dry ingredients. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the sugar mixture and mix until well combined. Fold in the peanut butter chips. Divide dough in half .
  3. On preared baking sheet. Shape each portion into a 15"x3" rectangle .About 3" apart. Sprinkle with chocolate chips. Pressing them lightly into the dough.
  4. Bake in preheated oven 6-7 minutes. (bake 8-9 for a crispier cookie). Mine took 14 minutes. Cool for about 10 minutes. Then cut them with a serrated knife into 1" strips. Remove to wire rack to coil completely. Enjoy. … :-)
