The ingredients needed to make Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies:
  1. Get 2 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  2. Take 1 tsp baking soda
  3. Make ready 1/4 tsp salt
  4. Make ready 1 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  5. Make ready 2 eggs
  6. Take 1 cup unsalted butter (softened at room temp.)
  7. Take 3/4 cup brown sugar
  8. Make ready 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  9. Prepare 2 cup chocolate chips
Instructions to make Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies:
  1. Sift together all purpose flour, salt, baking soda, and cinnamon. Set aside.
  2. Cream together unsalted butter along with both kinds of sugar until smooth, then add in eggs one by one.
  3. Combine the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients, and mix just until combined.
  4. Mix in chocolate chips, and mix again just until combined.
  5. Preheat your oven to 350°F, and line 2 baking sheets with parchment paper.
  6. Shape your cookie dough either with an ice cream scoop, or 2 spoons and drop equal sized dollops on to your baking sheet.
  7. Refrigirate your cookie dough for 15 minutes while your oven is preheating.
  8. Bake in your preheated oven for 12 to 15 minutes, until lightly golden brown. The center should be a little soft, but they will harden while cooling down.
