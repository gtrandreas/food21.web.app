The ingredients needed to make Fresh Tomato Bruschetta:
  1. Take 1/8 cup olive oil
  2. Prepare 1/3 cup fresh basil chopped
  3. Make ready 3 garlic cloves, minced
  4. Take 5 plum tomatoes, seeded, diced
  5. Make ready 1/4 cup red onion, diced
  6. Get 2 tbs grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
  7. Make ready 2 tsp balsamic vinegar
  8. Get 1/4 tsp salt & pepper
  9. Get 1 loaf long baguette roll (bread)

Steps to make Fresh Tomato Bruschetta:
  1. In a large bowl, combine all of the ingredients (except the bread) and toss gently. Refrigerate at least 15 minutes to allow flavors to blend.
  2. Pre-heat the broiler (low setting). Place oven rack about 6” from the top. If no low setting is available, place rack about 8” from the top.
  3. Cut bread at a slight angle into 1/2" to 3/4” slices and place on a baking sheet. Drizzle the top of each slice with olive oil and toast under low-heat broiler until lightly browned. Check at 1 minute intervals.
  4. Remove the sheet from the oven and top each slice with the tomato mixture that’s now near room temperature. Place the individual slices on a serving platter and serve promptly.
