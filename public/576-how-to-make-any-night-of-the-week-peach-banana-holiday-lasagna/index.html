The ingredients needed to make Peach Banana Holiday Lasagna:
  1. Take 6 sheets IDEAL GOURMET LASAGNA, cooked and halved
  2. Prepare 1 egg, lightly beaten
  3. Take 1 cup cheddar cheese, grated
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup cream cheese
  5. Get 1/4 cup white sugar
  6. Prepare 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  7. Take 1 can peach halves, drained and cubed
  8. Prepare 4 pieces bananas, sliced
  9. Take 1/4 syrup from canned peaches
  10. Get for the topping:
  11. Make ready 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  12. Get 1/3 cup brown sugar
  13. Make ready 1/4 cup cashew nuts, toasted and chopped
  14. Get 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  15. Get 1 dash ground nutmeg
  16. Prepare 3 tablespoons butter
  17. Make ready for sour cream:
  18. Get 3/4 cup Alaska Crema
  19. Get 3 tablespoons maple syrup
  20. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon lemon juice
Steps to make Peach Banana Holiday Lasagna:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Mix cheddar cheese, cream cheese, egg, white sugar, and almond extract in a medium-sized bowl. Blend well. In another bowl, combine sliced banana, peaches and honey.
  3. Put together the cheese mixture and mixed fruits as final filling. Spread evenly on halved Ideal Gourmet Lasagna sheet. Roll to finish.
  4. To make the Topping, combine flour, 1/3 cup brown sugar, cashew nuts, cinnamon, butter and nutmeg in a small bowl. Whip until crumbly. Sprinkle on top of lasagna rolls.
  5. Line lasagna rolls in a pan. Bake in preheated oven for 45 minutes. Cool for 15 minutes.
  6. Top with blended Sour Cream. Serve warm
