The ingredients needed to make Roasted Veggies:
  1. Take Broccoli
  2. Make ready Golden yellow mini potatoes/ big red potatoes
  3. Make ready Carrots
  4. Make ready Pepper
  5. Prepare Sea salt
  6. Take Oregano
  7. Take Thyme
  8. Take Canola/ olive oil
  9. Make ready Onion
  10. Get Peppers
  11. Get Asparagus
Steps to make Roasted Veggies:
  1. Cut potatoes into bite size (1/4- 1/2 depending on size)
  2. Cut carrots in half and make slices of pepper.
  3. Mix in oil to coat
  4. Season to desired cut onion and add to season
  5. Bake at 425 for 20-25 min.
  6. While baking cut broccoli then oil and season.
  7. Add broccoli and asparagus when potatoes are close to soft.
  8. Bake about 15 min watching and possibly turning heat down.
  9. I baked it in a cast iron skillet
