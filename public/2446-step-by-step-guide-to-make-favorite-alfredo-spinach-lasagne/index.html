The ingredients needed to make Alfredo Spinach Lasagne:
  1. Make ready 2 boxes (9 ounce) oven- ready lasagne noodles
  2. Get 2 jars (15 ounce) Creamy Basil Alfredo Sauce
  3. Get 1 (15 ounce) container ricotta cheese
  4. Take 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  5. Make ready 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  6. Prepare 2 large packs fresh baby spinach
  7. Make ready 1 cup Parmesan cheese
  8. Make ready Canola oil cooking spray

Instructions to make Alfredo Spinach Lasagne:
  1. In a large baking pan spread 1 jar Alfredo sauce on bottom of pan that has been lightly sprayed with canola oil cooking spray…..
  2. Layer several layers of your oven ready lasagna noodles on top of basil Alfredo sauce…..
  3. In a large bowl mix together your ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese and black pepper……
  4. Spread half of your ricotta cheese mixture over noodles in pan….
  5. Next layer 1 bag of your fresh baby spinach over the ricotta cheese mixture…….
  6. Next add several more layers of your oven ready lasagna noodles over spinach……

  9. Now add the rest of your oven ready lasagna noodles on top of spinach, pressing down gently on top of spinach……
  10. Add your remaining jar of Alfredo pasta sauce over noodles…..spread sauce evenly over noodles…..
  11. Cover and bake for 1 hour in a preheated 375 degree oven…..
