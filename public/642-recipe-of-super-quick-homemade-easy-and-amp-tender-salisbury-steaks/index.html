The ingredients needed to make Easy & Tender Salisbury Steaks:
  1. Get 1 1/2 lb ground beef
  2. Get 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  3. Make ready 1 can french onion soup
  4. Make ready 1 egg
  5. Get 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  6. Get 1/4 tsp salt
  7. Prepare 1 small red onion, chopped
  8. Prepare 1/4 cup red pepper
  9. Get 1/4 cup water
  10. Prepare 1/4 cup ketchup
  11. Prepare 1/4 tbsp mustard
  12. Take 1 tbsp worcestershire sauce
  13. Prepare 1 tbsp all-purpose flour
Steps to make Easy & Tender Salisbury Steaks:
  1. In a large bowl, mix together the beef, 1/3 cup of the french onion soup, bread crumbs, onion, red pepper, eggs, salt and ground pepper.
  2. Shape into round or oval patties.
  3. In a frying pan, brown both sides of the patties.
  4. On a small mixing bowl, mix the flour, the remaining soup, ketchup, worcestershire sauce, water and the mustard. Mix well and pour over meat cover and cook for 20 min.
  5. Serve with any side dish you like and enjoy!
