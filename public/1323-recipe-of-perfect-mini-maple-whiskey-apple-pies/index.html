The ingredients needed to make Mini Maple Whiskey Apple Pies:
  1. Make ready 1 can honey butter biscuits
  2. Get 2 medium diced red apples
  3. Prepare 2 medium diced green apples
  4. Get 1/2 oz maple whiskey
  5. Make ready 4 tbsp brown sugar
  6. Take apple pie spice
  7. Prepare 1/2 stick butter
  8. Prepare all-purpose flour
Instructions to make Mini Maple Whiskey Apple Pies:
  1. Place apples,butter,whiskey,apple pie spice, brown sugar in med pot
  2. Take biscuits and roll them out to fill a muffin tin.
  3. Bake biscuits for 4 mins in a 400°F oven
  4. When apples are soft turn them off and add flour if they are to to juicy.
  5. Pull biscuits out of oven and take a spoon and spread the dough out to make a well. Dough is going to be almost done a puffy
  6. Add apple pie filling into biscuits and bake for 15 mins.
  7. Drizzle with carmel and add your favorite ice cream
