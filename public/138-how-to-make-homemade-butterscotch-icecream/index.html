The ingredients needed to make Butterscotch Icecream:
  1. Make ready For Icecream-
  2. Take 200 ml full fat milk (Amul Gold Homogenised Standard Milk)
  3. Prepare 2 tbsp cornflour
  4. Get 1/4 cup sugar
  5. Take 1 cup fresh cream
  6. Get 1/2 tsp Yellow food colour
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp butterscotch essence
  8. Prepare 1/2 cup butterscotch granules
  9. Prepare For making granules-
  10. Get 1/4 cup cashews
  11. Prepare 1 tbsp butter
  12. Prepare 1/2 cup sugar
  13. Prepare For garnishing-
  14. Make ready as required Dairy milk chocolate
  15. Take Few Few roughly chopped Almond & cashews
  16. Take as required Chocolate syrup

Steps to make Butterscotch Icecream:
  1. First take 1/4 cup milk aside and boil rest of the milk.
  2. Flame should be medium.
  3. Now in 1/4 cup milk add cornflour and mix it well.
  4. Make sure it is lump free mixture.
  5. After one boil add sugar and cornflour mixture.
  6. Stir it continuously.
  7. When milk turns thick in consistency, turn off the flame.
  8. Let the mixture cool down on room temperature.
  9. After that set this mixture into fridge.
  10. Meanwhile to make butterscotch granules, take a pan and add sugar into it.
  11. Let sugar caramelized.
  12. Now add butter and mix it well.
  13. Add roughly chopped cashews and mix it well.
  14. Pour the mixture on kitchen surface and flatten it.
  15. Let it cool down.
  16. Now break the butterscotch granules and keep aside.
  17. You can also use ready made butterscotch granules to make this recipe very easy.
  18. Now take a large pot and add cream into it.
  19. Beat it until it turns frothy and doubles in quantity.
  20. Make sure you beat it in one direction.
  21. Beating for 7-9 minutes cream would be ready.
  22. Now remove the mixture from fridge.
  23. Add condensed milk and whipped cream into it and again beat it for 2 minutes.
  24. After that add yellow food colour and butterscotch essence and mix it well.
  25. Also add butterscotch granules and mix it.
  26. Pour the mixture into setting mold and set this Icecream into refrigerator for 6-7 hours.
  27. After that garnish it with dairy milk chocolate, almond cashews and with chocolate syrup.
  28. Butterscotch Icecream is ready to be serve.
