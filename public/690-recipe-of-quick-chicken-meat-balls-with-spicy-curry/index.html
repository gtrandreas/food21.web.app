The ingredients needed to make Chicken Meat Balls With Spicy Curry..:
  1. Take 1 kg Mince Chicken meat
  2. Take 1 bunch Fresh Corriander
  3. Take 2 large Green Chillies
  4. Take 2 tbsp Gram Flour or Garbanzo beans powder.
  5. Take 1/2 tsp Salt (to taste)
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp Red Ground Pepper (to taste)
  7. Prepare 1 tsp Ginger Garlic Powder or Paste.
  8. Take 1/2 cup Oil
  9. Get 3 large Onions (Chopped)
  10. Make ready 1 Salt and Red Ground Pepper (to taste)
  11. Get 1/2 tsp turmeric powder.
  12. Get 1/2 tsp Garam masala Powder.
  13. Take 1 tbsp Ginger Garlic Powder or Paste.
  14. Take 1 1/2 cup Water

Instructions to make Chicken Meat Balls With Spicy Curry..:
  1. For Meat Balls, Chop Fresh Corriander and green Chillies. Add it with all the other powdered ingredients. Set aside.
  2. Heat up a Wok. Add oil and Chopped Onions. Stir till pinkish in color.
  3. Add Ginger Garlic Powder or Paste, Salt, Pepper, Turmeric & Garam Masala Powder. Add Water and leave it on slow flame for 10 minutes.
  4. Make balls of meat mixture and start adding it in curry.
  5. Leave it on slow flame for 20 to 30 minutes till they r fully cooked.
  6. Serve With Chapati or Boiled Rice.
