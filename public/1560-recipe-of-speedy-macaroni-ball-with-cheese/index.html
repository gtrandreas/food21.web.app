The ingredients needed to make Macaroni ball with cheese:
  1. Make ready 250 gr macaroni
  2. Take 100 gr meat or chiken, choped
  3. Get 100 ml fresh milk
  4. Prepare 150 gr chedar cheese, slice
  5. Prepare 1 onion chopped
  6. Take 3 garlic chopped
  7. Get 1 tsp black paper
  8. Get 1 tbsp salt
  9. Prepare 2 tbsp powder
  10. Make ready 1 egg
  11. Get 100 gr bread crumbs
