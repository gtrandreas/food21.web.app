The ingredients needed to make Salisbury steak and gravy:
  1. Make ready 1 lb ground beef
  2. Get 1/3 cup bread crumbs
  3. Make ready 1 egg
  4. Take 1 packages onion soup mix
  5. Make ready 2/3 tsp pepper or to taste
  6. Prepare 2/3 tsp garlic or to taste
  7. Prepare 3 tbsp bacon grease or shortening
  8. Take 3 tbsp flour
  9. Get 3 cubes of beef bullion
  10. Get 3 or more cups of hot water and put the bullion cubes in it i say 3 or more cups depending on how thin or thick you want your gravy

Instructions to make Salisbury steak and gravy:
  1. Mix ground beef onion soup mix pepper garlic bread crumbs and egg in a large bowl
  2. Take about a large meatball size and roll it in your hands into a log like shape and Smoosh it so it looks like a steak shape instead of a hamburger patty
  3. Put your steaks in a large pan with a lid and cook until meat is thoroughly cooked flipping once.
  4. Remove your steaks from pan onto a platter or plate while you make your gravy
  5. Add in your bacon grease or shortening cook until it's liquified
  6. Add in your flour
  7. Stir be sure to get out all clumps it will look kind of pasty.. keep cooking it until it's a dark brown gravy kind of color but not enough to burn it
  8. Quickly add in your water with bullion cubes
