The ingredients needed to make Lady's finger stir fry:
  1. Prepare 200 gram lady's finger cubed
  2. Get 1/4 pumpkin cut into cubes
  3. Make ready 1 teasle gourd sliced
  4. Take 1 onion chopped
  5. Make ready 2-3 green chillies chopped
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  7. Take To taste salt and sugar
  8. Take As needed oil

Instructions to make Lady's finger stir fry:
  1. Heat oil and add onion and green chillies
  2. Add the lady's finger stir fry well till the moisture dries up
  3. Add gourd and pumpkin stir fry well and cover till it become soft
  4. Add salt and turmeric powder and mix
  5. Finally add sugar and mix well
  6. Serve it with rice or chapati
