The ingredients needed to make Chicken with Rich Oyster Sauce and Mayonnaise Sauce:
  1. Prepare 1 Chicken breast
  2. Prepare 1 ◎Sake/soy sauce (for dipping the chicken)
  3. Take 1 ◎Katakuriko (for coating the chiken)
  4. Take 1 tbsp each ★Oyster sauce/ Sugar
  5. Take 2 tbsp ★Mayonnaise
  6. Prepare 1 small amount ★Ichimi chili pepper powder
Instructions to make Chicken with Rich Oyster Sauce and Mayonnaise Sauce:
  1. Cut the chicken into bite-size pieces. Marinate them in sake and soy sauce at a 4:1 ratio.
  2. Coat with katakuriko.
  3. Heat a generous amount of oil in a pan and cook the chicken until crispy.
  4. Prepare another bowl to make the sauce with the ★ ingredients. When the chicken has cooled down a little, marinate in the sauce. Note: do not pour the sauce into the pan!
