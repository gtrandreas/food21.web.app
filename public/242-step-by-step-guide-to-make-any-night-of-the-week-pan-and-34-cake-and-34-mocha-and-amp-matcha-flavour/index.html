The ingredients needed to make Pan"cake" (mocha & matcha flavour):
  1. Get 1 1/2 tbs milk powder (mocha-matcha flavour)
  2. Prepare 2 tbs high protein flour
  3. Take 1/4 tsp baking powder
  4. Make ready 1/4 tsp cinnamon powder
  5. Get 1 pinch salt
  6. Make ready 1 whole egg
  7. Make ready 2-3 tbs water
  8. Make ready 1 tbs honey
  9. Make ready 1 tbs oil (coconut oil)

Instructions to make Pan"cake" (mocha & matcha flavour):
  1. Prepare all the ingredients
  2. Mix dry ingredients (milk powder, flour, cinnamon, salt, and baking powder)
  3. Add egg first and wishk the batter then add water (in accordance to u're preference) 2 tbs and whisk
  4. Add honey and oil then mix all up together
  5. Preheated the pan on medium low heat and start to cook the pancake
  6. If the bubbles/ holes start appear then your pancake is ready to flip and wait 30 second or so then check if its done. Repeat for the rest of the batter
  7. Serve with any topping you like. My topping are blueberry jam (on each top layer) raisin, strawberry, and dried coconut
