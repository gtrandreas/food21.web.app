The ingredients needed to make Crack Chicken Spaghetti Bake:
  1. Get 4 cups cooked chicken, shredded or cubed
  2. Prepare 1 lb spaghetti noodles
  3. Take 2 (10.75 oz) cans of "Heart Healthy" cream of chicken soup
  4. Prepare 16 oz pack of Velveeta, cubed (I used 10 oz)
  5. Get 1 & 1/2 cup low sodium chicken stock or broth
  6. Get 1 teaspoon each of black​ pepper, onion powder, & garlic powder
  7. Make ready 2 teaspoons Parsley flakes (optional)
  8. Take 1 (1.5 oz.) package dry ranch mix
  9. Take 3 tablespoons chopped bacon or bacon bits
  10. Take 1 cup Italian, cheddar, or Parmesan cheese, grated

Steps to make Crack Chicken Spaghetti Bake:
  1. Gather chicken or use rotisserie chicken (deboned & chopped) and set aside.
  2. Bring a large pot of water to boil and cook pasta Al Dente. Drain and set aside. Preheat oven to 350º F and lightly grease a 9×13-inch baking dish.
  3. Combine chicken soup, velveeta, broth, and seasoning in a medium saucepan over medium heat, stirring until melted and smooth.
  4. Whisk in dry ranch. Mix well.
  5. Place drained pasta and chicken in a large bowl. Pour cheese sauce over the chicken and pasta and toss until everything is covered
  6. Pour into baking dish and top with shredded cheese and bacon pieces.
  7. Bake, uncovered, for 30-35 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly, and dish is heated through.
