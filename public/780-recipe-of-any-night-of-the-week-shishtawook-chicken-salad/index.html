The ingredients needed to make Shishtawook chicken salad:
  1. Get 1 boneless chicken breast, washed and cut to bite size pieces
  2. Make ready Half a ripe pawpaw
  3. Get 1 bunch salad greens
  4. Take Some olives (optional)
  5. Make ready 1 handful peanuts
  6. Get 2 tbsp crumbled cheese of any variety (optional)
  7. Get Half an onion sliced thin
  8. Make ready 1-2 tbsp oil to grill the chicken and pawpaw
  9. Make ready For the marinade
  10. Get 4-5 tbsp plain yoghurt
  11. Take 1 tbsp garlic paste
  12. Prepare 1-2 tbsp tomato paste
  13. Take 1 tsp thyme(can use coriander powder)
  14. Make ready 1-2 tsp red paprika (or black pepper)
  15. Take 4-5 tbsp lemon juice (1 lemon)
  16. Take to taste Salt
  17. Make ready For Pear balsamic vinaigrette
  18. Get 1 ripe pear, peeled,cored and chopped (substitute an apple)
  19. Prepare 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar( or any dark vinegar-1 tbsp,if synthetic
  20. Get 2 tbsp olive oil
  21. Prepare 2 tbsp lemon juice
  22. Take to taste Salt and pepper

Instructions to make Shishtawook chicken salad:
  1. Marinate the chicken with all the spices, yogurt and tomato paste for minimum 30 minutes. For best results, marinate overnight.
  2. Wash and dry the salad greens, toss in onions, olives, cheese if using, peanuts and sprinkle some salt and pepper. Keep aside.
  3. Blend the pear or apple with vinegar, oil, salt, pepper and lemon until smooth. Add some water if you prefer a thinner dressing.
  4. In a heavy bottomed pan, heat a tbsp oil and grill the pawpaw for about a minute, regularly tossing it. Use the same pan to grill the chicken on skewers until done and having reddish brown char marks. You can directly grill without skewers too.
  5. To serve, toss in the greens, followed by the pawpaw and the chicken skewers. Drizzle the vinaigrette. Relish immediately. Enjoy !!
