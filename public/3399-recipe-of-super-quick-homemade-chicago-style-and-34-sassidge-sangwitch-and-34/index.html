The ingredients needed to make Chicago-style "Sassidge Sangwitch":
  1. Get Quality Italian Sausage
  2. Prepare Red, Yellow and Green Bell Pepper, cut into vertical strips
  3. Take Italian dressing
  4. Take Sweet Onions, sliced
  5. Get Extra Virgin Olive oil
  6. Make ready Garlic cloves, minced
  7. Take Crushed Red Pepper & Oregano, dried
  8. Prepare Salt & Fresh Cracked Pepper
  9. Make ready Italian bread loaves, sliced widthwise
  10. Take Mild or Hot Giardiniera, per sandwich (Mezzetta Chicago-style Italian Sandwich Mix)
Steps to make Chicago-style "Sassidge Sangwitch":
  1. In a medium bowl | Add Onions, Peppers, Italian dressing, Garlic, Fresh Cracked Pepper, Crushed Red Pepper, and Oregano | tossing them around until coated
  2. Poke each Sausage with a Fork 4-5 times | Add everything into a zip lock bag and marinate overnight or 2-4 hours
  3. Preheat oven to 350¬∞ | Add Everything to a 9 x13 Glass Baking pan | Bake for 40-60mins until Sausage is cooked | Add Peppers in the last 30 minutes | Flip & Toss everything around few times during cooking time
  4. Building your "Sassidge Sangwitch": Place a sausage on a bun, slather in good yellow mustard, top with an ample amount of caramelized onions, peppers, and Giardiniera
