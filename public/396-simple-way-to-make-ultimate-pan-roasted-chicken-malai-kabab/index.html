The ingredients needed to make Pan roasted Chicken Malai Kabab:
  1. Prepare 300 gms chicken mince
  2. Prepare 1 1/2 medium onion finely chopped
  3. Make ready 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  4. Prepare 1 tsp tandoori masala
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp chaat masala
