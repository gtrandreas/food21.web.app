The ingredients needed to make Lemon & Garlic Chicken Caesar Salad:
  1. Take 2 medium boneless skinless chicken breasts
  2. Make ready 2 head chopped romaine lettuce
  3. Get 1 1/2 cup seasoned whole grain croutons
  4. Make ready 3 minced garlic cloves
  5. Take 1/3 cup Renee's Mighty Caesar Dressing
  6. Take 1 parmigiano-reggiano for garnish
  7. Make ready marinade
  8. Get 4 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  9. Prepare 4 tbsp lemon juice
  10. Take 3 tbsp garlic salt OR garlic bread seasoning
  11. Make ready 1 tsp unpasteurized liquid honey
  12. Take 1 dash paprika
  13. Get 1 dash ground oregano
  14. Take 1/4 tsp lemon pepper or citrus seasoning
  15. Get 1 salt and pepper to your liking

Instructions to make Lemon & Garlic Chicken Caesar Salad:
  1. Sear whole breasts OR cut into strips for full flavour and put into a resealable plastic bag.
  2. Combine marinade ingredients and pour into bag with chicken, turning to coat. Marinate in fridge for 1-2 hours, turning occasionally.
  3. When chicken is close to being fully marinated, wash the romaine heads thoroughly and chop. Add croutons and chopped romaine to a large salad bowl, set aside.
  4. Mince the garlic cloves and set aside. Transfer chicken to a non-stick cast iron skillet on medium high heat. Add garlic. Continue to cook until chicken is no longer pink and garlic is nearly dissolved, about 8 minutes.
  5. Add dressing to salad and toss. Add cooked chicken. Garnish servings with curls of parmigiano-reggiano cheese, and lemon wedges (optional).
