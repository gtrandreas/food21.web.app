The ingredients needed to make My Mexican Salsa on a jacket Potato. ðŸ˜š:
  1. Take 350 g Good minced Beef
  2. Take 3/4 pint passata mash 6 Tomatoe in it mix.or 1 tin tomatoes
  3. Prepare 1/2 Large Onion
  4. Get 1 tbls Olive oil
  5. Prepare 1 tbls mexican herbs
  6. Make ready 2 Garlic cloves mashed
  7. Make ready 2 Chillies chopped
  8. Prepare 1 dried Chicken stock cube
  9. Prepare 1 Red Pepper chopped
  10. Prepare 4 tbls Loose Corn
  11. Make ready slices Jalapeno
  12. Prepare 1 tsp red hot pepper sauce (Optional)
  13. Prepare 200 g Mexican cheese grated. If too hot just use grated chedder
  14. Take 4 tbls Sour Cream i used Sour cream and mix in 1 tbls fresh chive
  15. Make ready 2 Medium Potatoes or Rice with lemon flavour
Instructions to make My Mexican Salsa on a jacket Potato. ðŸ˜š:
  1. Heat up the oil add onions fry for 1 minute add garlic, mexican herbs stir in then add the meat. Cook on medium until the meat is all coloured.
  2. Next add chillies, corn and then the passata and tomatoes also red peppersmixed in. Carry on cooking for 5 minutes.
  3. Next add your potatoes washed and pierce holes in the microwave to cook.
  4. Add the chicken stock to the mix if its dry you can add more passata or an extra tin crushed Tomatoes.
  5. Grate the cheese ready and make the Sour cream or bought sour cream add chives if you want. Add 1 tbls jalapenos to the mix stir in.
  6. Get the potatoe on the serving plate then spoon over the beef salsa. Next add the cheese on top of the salsa and add back to microwave for it to melt.
  7. Next add two tbls of the sour cream on each plate then add a couple of jalapenos on top.
  8. If your having tortillas add them around the outside.serve hot.
