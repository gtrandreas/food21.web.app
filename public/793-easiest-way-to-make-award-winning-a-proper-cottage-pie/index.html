The ingredients needed to make A Proper Cottage Pie:
  1. Make ready Filling
  2. Make ready 1-1/3 pound ground beef
  3. Take 2 cups beef broth
  4. Make ready 1-1/2 cups carrots
  5. Prepare 1-1/4 cup celery
  6. Take 1-1/3 cup onion
  7. Make ready 1/2 cup red wine
  8. Prepare 1/3 cup all purpose flour
  9. Prepare 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  10. Make ready 1-1/2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  11. Take 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  12. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  13. Make ready 1 teaspoon salt
  14. Prepare 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  15. Prepare 4 tablespoons butter
  16. Take Mash
  17. Make ready 4 tablespoons butter
  18. Make ready To taste salt
  19. Make ready 1 cup sharp cheddar cheese shredded
  20. Take 1-2/3 pound potatoes
  21. Get As needed water
  22. Prepare 2 tablespoons chopped parsley flakes
  23. Prepare 1 cup evaporated milk
  24. Get Topping
  25. Prepare 1 cup sharp cheddar cheese shredded
Steps to make A Proper Cottage Pie:
  1. Peel wash and cube the potatoes. Cover with water and boil till fork tender. Drain the water. Add the butter, and milk. Shred the cheese and add to the potatoes. Add the parsley and salt.
  2. Whip the potatoes well till creamy. Let rest. Chop the vegetables and melt the butter.
  3. Sauté the vegetables for 12 minutes in the butter with the salt, pepper, garlic, and thyme. Add the beef and when browned add the wine and allow to reduce. Simmer 15 minutes. This can be done as you are doing the potatoes.
  4. When the liquids are reduced add the flour and tomato paste. Stir and incorporate well. Let the flour cout and cook for 7 minutes. Add the broth.
  5. Preheat the oven 400°Fahrenheit. Stir well stirring constantly this will thicken. Transfer to an oven safe pan I used an 8×12 pan about 2-1/2 inch deep. Spread it out evenly. Then add the mashed potatoes I used a spoon, but you can pipe it on.
  6. Take a big fork and plow the field, put grooves on top. Then top off with 1 cup sharp cheddar cheese shredded. Put onto another pan. I put parchment paper on the bottom pan for easy cleanup. It's gonna bubble over!!!
  7. Bake 30 minutes. Take out of oven and allow to rest 12-15 minutes before serving. I hope you enjoy!!!!
