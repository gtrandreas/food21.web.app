Steps to make Bhindi ghousht:
  1. At fist wash meat and put aside then chop onions and then pour oil in a pan
  2. Then add onion in the pan then broil until golden brown then add g.g paste
  3. Now add chopped tomatoes in it and broil it well
  4. Then put all spices and salt shown in pic on sill add water and crushed and add water and make a paste
  5. Then add it in a pan then add sabut garam masala then broil it well then beat the yougert
  6. Now add yougert and broil it well then add almost 3 glass water and cover until done
  7. Then add ladyfingers in the meat when meat half tenderize then add garam masala and broil then add water and cover then when ladyfingers tenderize
  8. Then serve…..
