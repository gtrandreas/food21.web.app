Instructions to make Breakfast lasagna:
  1. Cook bacon until crispy
  2. Pan fry left over potatoes in bacon grease
  3. Chop up mushrooms, red onion & cherry tomatoes and saute slightly
  4. Layer potatoes, cheese x2, spinach x2, veggie mixture, bacon then pour eggs on top with more cheese
  5. Cook at 400' for 40 mins or until eggs are fluffy & set
