The ingredients needed to make Glazed salmon with thai style mango and strawberry salsa:
  1. Prepare 2 pieces salmon
  2. Make ready 10-12 asparagus
  3. Prepare Sauce
  4. Take 1 small cup small diced mango
  5. Take 1 small cup small diced strawberry
  6. Make ready 1 shallot thinly sliced
  7. Prepare Half cup of small diced cucumber
  8. Take Half cup of diced red pepper
  9. Make ready 1 tsp fish sauce
  10. Get 1 tsp soy sauce
  11. Make ready 1 tbsp brown sugar
  12. Take 1 tsp chilli flakes
  13. Get salt and pepper

Instructions to make Glazed salmon with thai style mango and strawberry salsa:
  1. Mix all the sauce base together
  2. Use 2 tbsp of sauce base glazed all over the salmon and leave it to marinate for 10 mins.
  3. Grill asparagus on a griddle pan or on the barbie for 3-5 min. Take them out and leave it aside.
  4. Grill salmon skin on first to the same pan or barbie. Leave it for 8-10 minutes then turn over and leave it to cook for another 5-6 min or until fish just cooked.
  5. Plate up time, put asparagus on as the base and add salmon and thai mango and strawberry salsa on top.
