Instructions to make Pan- roasted chicken:
  1. Mix all the marinade ingredients well.
  2. Marinate the chicken inside and outside well
  3. Keep in refrigerator for atleast an hour. Can leave it overnight as well
  4. In a pan add oil and whole garam masala. When it heats add chicken n fry on high flames until both sides get color. Now cover and let cook in low flame for almost an hour or until the chicken is tender.
  5. Dry the excess water and serve
