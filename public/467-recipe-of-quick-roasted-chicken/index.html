The ingredients needed to make Roasted chicken:
  1. Take 500 gm Chicken
  2. Take 50 gm Butter
  3. Prepare 1 finely chopped onion
  4. Prepare 4-5 dry red chilli
  5. Take 1 tsp whole coriander seeds
  6. Get 2 tsp fennel seeds
  7. Take 2 tsp cumin seeds
  8. Prepare 1 tbsp pepper
  9. Prepare 2 cloves
  10. Get 1/2 tsp Turmeric powder
  11. Take 1/2 tsp Red chilli powder
  12. Make ready 4 tbsp curd
  13. Prepare 6-7 chopped garlic cloves
  14. Prepare 1 inch ginger
  15. Take 2 tsp lemon juice
  16. Make ready to taste Salt

Steps to make Roasted chicken:
  1. Take a pan heat it put dry red chilli, coriander seed, fennel seeds, pepper and cumin seeds and roast it and keep aside
  2. In a grinder jar put all roasted spices and Ginger garlic and lemon juice and grind till smooth paste is formed
  3. Now wash chicken properly
  4. Take a mixing bowl put chicken and put spices mixture, curd and turmeric powder add red chilli powder,salt and mix well and marinate for 1 hours
  5. Take a pan heat it and put butter and chopped onion,Ginger garlic paste soute it and add little amount of salt strie well
  6. Now add chicken and saute it Now cover with lead and cook for 7-8 minutes
  7. Now open the lead and stire well cook for 8-10 minutes on low flame
  8. Now roasted chicken is ready
  9. Garnish with chopped fresh coriander
