The ingredients needed to make Hot chocolate chocolate cake w/ cream cheese frosting:
  1. Get 2 cups flour
  2. Get 1 cup sugar
  3. Make ready 1 1/2 tablespoon baking soda
  4. Take 1 1/2 tablespoon baking powder
  5. Take 1/2 teaspoon salt
  6. Prepare 9 packets hot chocolate mix
  7. Make ready 1 tablespoon vanilla
  8. Make ready 3 large eggs
  9. Make ready 3/4 cups oil
  10. Get 1 cup milk
  11. Make ready 1 cup boiling water
  12. Make ready 1 package cream cheese
  13. Get 3/4 cup butter unsalted
  14. Take 1 cup powdered sugar
