The ingredients needed to make Maple-nut Oatmeal Brownie Cookies:
  1. Get 1 (18.03 oz) box fudge brownie mix
  2. Get 3 packets maple brown sugar oatmeal. (1 heaping cup)
  3. Make ready 2 eggs beaten
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup oil
  5. Take 1 1/2 tablespoons water *or coffee
  6. Make ready 3/4 cup chopped roasted almonds
Instructions to make Maple-nut Oatmeal Brownie Cookies:
  1. Preheat oven to 350.
  2. Stir together brownie mix, oatmeal packets, almonds, egg, oil, and water in a large bowl until well incorporated
  3. Batter should be thick enough to cling in ball to an upside down spoon. If too loose, add a little more quick oats until thickened
  4. Drop heaping tablespoons of batter onto UNGREASED baking sheet leaving 2" space between. * these cooking can spread!
  5. Bake 10-12 minutes until edge are crisply firm andd center is just set
  6. Cool for 2 minutes on pan then transfer to a rack to cool completely
