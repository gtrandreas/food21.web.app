The ingredients needed to make Easy Cheesy Lasagna Bake:
  1. Take 1 box radiatore pasta, cooked and drained
  2. Prepare 1 spaghetti sauce
  3. Make ready 8 oz tomato sauce
  4. Prepare 1 lb hamburger, cooked and drained
  5. Take 1 tbsp minced garlic
  6. Take 1 salt and pepper to taste
  7. Prepare 16 oz cottage cheese
  8. Prepare 1/2 lb cheddar cheese, shredded
  9. Make ready 1/4 lb Monterey cheese, shredded
Steps to make Easy Cheesy Lasagna Bake:
  1. Add pasta, hamburger, spaghetti sauce, tomato sauce, garlic, salt and pepper to a 9X13 dish and mix until well combined.
  2. Spoon cottage cheese over to top of mixture and spread into the top layer.
  3. Spread cheddar cheese over the top and top with Monterey cheese
  4. Bake at 450°F for 30 minutes or until cheese is bubbling
