The ingredients needed to make Strawberry icecream:
  1. Take 600 ml almond milk see recipe opposite
  2. Make ready 310 ml whipping cream my tub said 300ml
  3. Get 300 grams strawberries
  4. Get 25 grams sweet freedom natural or your choice sweetner
  5. Make ready 5 ml Strawberry extract flavouring
  6. Take 5 egg yolks

Steps to make Strawberry icecream:
  1. Make almond milk or buy
  2. Add almond milk, whipping cream & extract to a pan and bring almost to the boil
  3. Put yolks and sweetner in a jug and whisk until slightly creamy
  4. Pour some of milk and cream mixture into egg mixture stirring then pour back into pan and on low to medium heat keep stirring until it coats the back if a spoon
  5. Put strawberries in a blender and blend
  6. Pour milk cream mix into big mixing bowl and stir in strawberry mix
  7. Leave to cool then cover with foil and pop in fridge, preferably overnight
  8. When ready stir mixture and put icecream machine on paddles turning pour mixture in and let run. Mine took 45 minutes
  9. Serve or put in container and freeze.
  10. Enjoy.
