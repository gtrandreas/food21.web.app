Simple Way to Prepare Speedy Chicken Curry

The ingredients needed to make Chicken Curry:
  1. Get For Marination
  2. Make ready 500 gm chicken
  3. Prepare 1 tsp turmeric powder
  4. Take 1 tsp chilli powder
  5. Prepare 2 tsp ginger garlic paste
  6. Get To taste salt
  7. Get 2 tsp fresh curd
  8. Take 2 tsp mustard oil
  9. Prepare For Curry
  10. Take 2-3 tbsp mustard oil
  11. Make ready 1 tsp sugar
  12. Make ready 1 bay leaf
  13. Get 1 inch cinnamon
  14. Prepare 2-3 green cardamoms
  15. Prepare 3-4 cloves
  16. Prepare 2 onions chopped
  17. Make ready 1 tomato chopped
  18. Take 2 green chillies slit
  19. Make ready 1 tsp red chilli powder
  20. Make ready 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  21. Prepare 2 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  22. Make ready 1 tbsp onion paste
  23. Get To taste salt
  24. Make ready 2 tsp meat masala powder
  25. Get 1 tsp garam masala powder
  26. Make ready 2 tsp chopped coriander leaves
Instructions to make Chicken Curry:
  1. Wash chicken pieces properly and marinate with salt, turmeric powder, chilli powder, ginger garlic paste, mustard oil and curd. Set aside for one hour.
  2. Heat oil in a cooking pot and add sugar. When sugar starts to caramelize add bay leaf, cinnamon, green cardamom, cloves and saute for a minute.
  3. Add in chopped onion and fry till golden brown. Add red chilli powder, turmeric powder, onion paste, ginger garlic paste and salt. Saute well for a couple of minutes. Add meat masala powder and cook till oil starts to leave the sides.
  4. Add chopped tomato and green chillies. Cook till tomato turns soft.
  5. Now add the marinated chicken pieces and mix properly so that the chicken pieces are well coated with the masala mix. Cover and cook for 10 minutes. Give it an occasional stir in between to avoid the masala sticking to the bottom of the pot.
  6. Next add water (preferably hot water) as required and stir once. Cover and cook on a medium flame for about 20 minutes or until chicken is tender.
  7. Open the lid and stir. Add garam masala powder and coriander leaves. Cover and switch off the flame.
  8. Serve chicken curry hot with fluffy white rice.
