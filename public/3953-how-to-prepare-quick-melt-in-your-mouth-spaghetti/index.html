  1. Make ready Spaghetti
  2. Prepare 1 lb ground beef

Steps to make Melt in your mouth Spaghetti:
  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°
  2. Sautee onion, garlic, & mushrooms until soft. Add ground beef, seasons and cook thoroughly. Drain grease and add pasta sauce.
  3. Boil spaghetti noodles per instructions. Drain.
  4. Mix beef and noodles to make your spaghetti
  5. In a separate bowl mix cheese mixture together thoroughly
  6. Layer cheese mixture on bottom of baking dish
  7. Layer spaghetti on top of cheese mixture
  8. Repeat layers and top with mozzarella cheese
  9. Bake for 20 mins
  10. There ya have it! Melt in your mouth Spaghetti!
