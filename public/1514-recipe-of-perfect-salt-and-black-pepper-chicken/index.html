The ingredients needed to make 🌶🍜Salt and Black Pepper Chicken🍜🌶:
  1. Prepare Oil for deep frying
  2. Take For Chiken Marination
  3. Take 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  4. Take 1 tsp light soy sauce
  5. Get 1/2 tsp sugar
  6. Prepare 2 tbls cornflour
  7. Make ready 1 tbls oil
  8. Take 2 tbls water
  9. Make ready For the Coating
  10. Make ready 120 g plain flour
  11. Take 60 g cornflour
  12. Prepare 1/2 tsp freshly ground black pepper(to taste)
  13. Take 1/4 tsp salt (to taste)
  14. Make ready Dry Fry Seasonings
  15. Take 1 tblsp oil
  16. Make ready 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper(to taste)
  17. Prepare 1/8 tsp salt (to taste)
  18. Make ready 1 bell pepper, sliced
  19. Get 2 cloves garlic, chooped
  20. Take 1 medium sized onions, thinly sliced
  21. Make ready Few leaves of Parsely (optional)
Steps to make 🌶🍜Salt and Black Pepper Chicken🍜🌶:
  1. Combine the cubed chicken and marinade ingredients in a bowl. Leave to marinate for a 1hr.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the coating ingredients. Roll each cube of chicken in the coating mix, ensuring the entire surface is covered in flour.
  3. In a batches, deep fry the chicken for 3-4 minutes until golden brown. Drain on kitchen pepper towels.
  4. Heat the oil in a wok. Fry the black pepper and salt, followed by the bell pepper, garlic and then onions sute 2 minute.
  5. Add the fried chicken to the wok, toss everything together, and serve. - - Serve with egg fried rice, noodles or simple boiled rice.
