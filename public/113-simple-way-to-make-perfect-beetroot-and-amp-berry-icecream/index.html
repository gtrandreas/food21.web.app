The ingredients needed to make Beetroot & Berry Icecream:
  1. Get 1 cup grated Beetroot
  2. Take 1/2 cup berries
  3. Prepare 1/3 cup sugar
  4. Get 1 tsp Raisins
  5. Get 2 tbsp roasted nuts
  6. Make ready 1/2 cup thick milk
