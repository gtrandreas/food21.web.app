Instructions to make One Pot Spaghetti and Meatballs:
  1. Brown meat balls in olive oil on each side. Remove and set aside.
  2. Saute onions in same pan, once softened, add garlic. Cook another 30 seconds til garlic gets fragrant. Add all 4 cans of tomatoes.
  3. Bring to a boil. Add in meatballs. Lid on, low heat, cook for 1 hour and 20 minutes.
  4. Add 2 1/2 - 3 cups water and spaghetti. Cook med. heat, covered, another 15 - 20 minutes til pasta is cooked.
