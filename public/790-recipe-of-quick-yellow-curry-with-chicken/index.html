The ingredients needed to make Yellow curry with chicken:
  1. Get 4 potatoes
  2. Make ready 2 onions
  3. Get 1 package yellow curry paste
  4. Make ready 1 can coconut milk
  5. Take 1 cup water
  6. Prepare 8 chicken fillets
  7. Prepare 2 tbs salt
  8. Take 4 tbs palm sugar
  9. Make ready 4 tbs tamarind juice
  10. Prepare 2 tbs vegetable oil
  11. Prepare 2 tbs peanut butter
