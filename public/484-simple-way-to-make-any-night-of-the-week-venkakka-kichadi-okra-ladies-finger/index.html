The ingredients needed to make Venkakka Kichadi (Okra/Ladies Finger):
  1. Take 1 Cup Yogurt Curd /
  2. Take 1/2 Kilogram Okra Lady ' s Finger / / Vendakka
  3. Get 1 1/4 Teaspoons Turmeric powder
  4. Prepare 1 1/2 Teaspoons chilly Red powder
  5. Make ready 1 Pinch Cumin Seeds Jeera /
  6. Take 1 Pinch Asafoetida (Kaayam)
  7. Take Salt
  8. Prepare 2 Tablespoons Oil
Instructions to make Venkakka Kichadi (Okra/Ladies Finger):
  1. Wash and pat dry Lady's Finger /Okra /Vendakka. Cut the top end of each one and slice into uniformly round pieces.
  2. In a bowl mix turmeric, red chilly powder and salt with a teaspoon of water and make a paste of it.
  3. Add the cut lady's finger /okra /vendakka pieces into the bowl and mix well with a spoon, so that the masala gets well coated.
  4. Heat a non-stick frying pan/ kadai, with oil. Fry the masala coated okra/vendakka on a medium flame.(Do not burn it).
  5. When the okra/vendakka is fried, lower the flame and add jeera/cumin seeds. Off the flame and add asafoetida to it. Mix well. Keep aside to cool.
  6. Take yogurt/curd in a serving bowl and mix with enough salt. When the okra/vendakka is cooled, add this to curd.
  7. Serve with hot steamed rice.
