The ingredients needed to make Malaysian Curry Chicken:
  1. Get 2 Chicken meat

  4. Get 3 potatoes

  9. Make ready Option Cumin powder
  10. Get To taste chicken powder
  11. Make ready 1 tea spoon sugar
  12. Take 2 bay leaves
  13. Make ready 1 lemong grass
  14. Get Water

Steps to make Malaysian Curry Chicken:
  1. Prepare all ingredients, cut cube potato, onion, tomato and chicken
  2. Heat olive oil in a pot, add onion and fry until fragrant, add lemong grass, bay leave and fry for few minutes, add curry pasta and fry until you get nice smell of curry pasta.
  3. Add chicken meat and cook until chicken looks dry. Add boiled water and cook over high heat
  4. Add potatoes, garlic powder and bring it to boil again, then turn into lower heat and cook untill potatoes get tender.
  5. Put in tomato coconut milk and bring it to boil again then turn into lower heat again and add chicken powder and sugar to taste.
