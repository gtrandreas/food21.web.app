The ingredients needed to make Wheat Banana Bread (Eggless):
  1. Prepare 2 Cups Whole wheat flour
  2. Prepare 2 Big bananas
  3. Get 1 Pinch nutmeg
  4. Get 1 Pinch cinnamon
  5. Make ready 3/4 th cup brown sugar

  9. Make ready 1 cup milk+ 3 Tbsp milk
  10. Take 1 Pinch salt
  11. Take coconut oil for greasing
  12. Prepare 1 Tsp Vanilla essence

Instructions to make Wheat Banana Bread (Eggless):
  1. Peel bananas and mash in a bowl. Keep aside.
  2. Take a big mixing bowl and sieve wheat flour into it. To it, add all the dry ingredients- sugar, cinnamon, flaxseed, baking soda, baking powder, salt and nutmeg. Dry mix.
  3. Add mashed bananas and curd to dry mix and gradually add milk to the same for smooth consistency batter. Whisk a bit to mix everything nicely.
  4. Grease the bread tin with coconut oil and pour the batter into it.
  5. Bake at 180 degree for 30 to 40 minutes in a preheated oven. Check with fork from middle to check whether it's cooked properly. The fork should come out clean.
  6. Enjoy with green tea !
