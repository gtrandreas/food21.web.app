The ingredients needed to make Christmas Fudge:
  1. Get 300 grams Marshmallows
  2. Prepare 100 grams Unsalted Butter
  3. Get 1 tbsp Water
  4. Get 200 grams Chocolate
  5. Prepare 1 cup Pecan nuts, chopped
  6. Get 1 tsp Vanilla

Steps to make Christmas Fudge:
  1. Line an 18cm square tin with tinfoil, grease lightly.
  2. In a saucepan, add marshmallows, butter and water. Stir occasionally over low heat until melted and combined.
  3. Add chocolate, pecans and vanilla to pan, stirring until chocolate has melted.
  4. Pour into prepared pan and refrigerate until set.
