The ingredients needed to make BLT BRUSCHETTA:
  1. Prepare 1 bunch spring mix (chopped)
  2. Make ready 1 medium tomato (sliced/chopped)
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp olive oil
  4. Prepare 3 slice bacon or bacon bites
  5. Prepare 2 slice Italian bread
  6. Get 1 pinch black pepper
  7. Make ready 2 tbsp shredded cheese (prefer mozzarella cheese or Cheddar cheese)
  8. Take 1 tbsp pesto
  9. Make ready 1 oz pesto
