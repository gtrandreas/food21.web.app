The ingredients needed to make Oven roasted chicken cacciatore:
  1. Prepare 4 chicken quarters
  2. Prepare 2 tbsp olive oil
  3. Take 2 tbsp flour
  4. Prepare 1 1/4 tsp salt
  5. Prepare 1 tsp oregano
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp ground pepper
  7. Get 12 oz fresh mushrooms, sliced
  8. Make ready 1 large pepper, chopped
  9. Make ready 3/4 cup chopped onions
  10. Prepare 14 1/2 oz canned tomatoes
  11. Take 1/2 cup white wine
  12. Get 1 tbsp chipped fresh flat-leaf parsley

Steps to make Oven roasted chicken cacciatore:
  1. Remove skin from chicken and trim visible fat.
  2. Heat oil in a large pan in the oven for 2 minutes.
  3. Combine flour, 1 tsp salt, oregano and pepper in a large Ziploc bag. Add chicken pieces, tossing to coat well with the mixture.
  4. Arrange chicken on hot oil in the pan and bake for 20 minutes.
  5. Stir mushrooms, pepper and onions into the pan with chicken. Bake for 15 more minutes, stirring once.
  6. Add tomatoes with their liquid, wine and remaining 1/4 tsp salt, breaking up tomatoes with a fork.
  7. Broil for 10 minutes.
  8. Sprinkle with chopped parsley.
