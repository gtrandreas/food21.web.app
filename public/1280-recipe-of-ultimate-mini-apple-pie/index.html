The ingredients needed to make Mini apple pie:
  1. Make ready 8 pack crescent rolls
  2. Make ready 1/4 cup packed brown sugar
  3. Prepare 1 tsp apple pie spice
  4. Get 3 tbsp melted butter
  5. Prepare 1 apple

Instructions to make Mini apple pie:
  1. Place rolls on a baking sheet covered in parchment paper
  2. Mix brown sugar and apple pie spice and spread on top of each roll
  3. Core and peel an apple. Slice it into 8 pieces. Melt butter and dip apple slices in it
  4. Place a piece of apple on the large end of crescent roll and roll up drizzling the tops with butter
  5. Bake at 375 degrees for 10 minutes or until brown
