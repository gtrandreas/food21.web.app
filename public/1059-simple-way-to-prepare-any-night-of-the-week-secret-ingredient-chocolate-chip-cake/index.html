The ingredients needed to make Secret Ingredient Chocolate Chip Cake:
  1. Take 2 cans white beans, rinsed & drained (500g)
  2. Take 100 g soy yogurt
  3. Get 1/4 cup soy milk
  4. Take 3 tbsp oil
  5. Take 1 cup quick oats
  6. Take 1 packet baking powder
  7. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  8. Make ready 1 cup brown sugar
  9. Take 1/4 cup white sugar
  10. Get 1 packet vanilla sugar (or 1 tsp vanilla extract)
  11. Make ready 3 tbsp AP flour
  12. Prepare 180 g baking chocolate, chopped
Instructions to make Secret Ingredient Chocolate Chip Cake:
  1. Blend the beans until creamy and no chunks remain. Put in a large bowl.
  2. Add all other ingredients (mix in chocolate chunks last). Do not overmix.
  3. Pour in a parchment-lined springform pan. Bake at 350°F/180°C for about 40 min. The inside should still be gooey. Let cool & serve. Enjoy :)
