The ingredients needed to make good old collard greens®:
  1. Make ready bunches of collard greens
  2. Get of garlic powder
  3. Prepare onion diced
  4. Make ready brown sugar
  5. Make ready smoked turkey necks
  6. Take of chicken broth (small box) or can
  7. Get salt
  8. Make ready cayenne pepper ( optional) if u like spice.
  9. Get onion diced

Instructions to make good old collard greens®:
  1. place turkey necks in a pot with enough water just to cover meat
  2. boil for 35 to 40 minutes don't let water boil out
  3. add onion and sugar and seasonings and chicken broth
  4. wash greens and cut up into bite size pieces then add to the pot. cover and continue to cook for a while on simmer until greens reach ur preferences. add more salt if u like.
  5. let stand. serve and enjoy.
