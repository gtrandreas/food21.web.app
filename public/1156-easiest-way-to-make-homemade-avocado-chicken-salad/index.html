  6. Make ready 3 fried chicken tenderloion

Instructions to make AVOCADO CHICKEN SALAD:
  1. Fry up the chicken tenderloin seasoned and covered in flour
  2. Boil the eggs
  3. Grab a container and put avocado and mayo in it. Stir it up. Mix it until Well blended. More mayo than avocado.
  4. Add seasonings. Seasoned salt, garlic powder, and pepper
  5. Chopped up chicken, tomatoes, onions, cucumber, lettuce and eggs add it to the avocado and mayo
  6. Add Bacon bits
  7. Mix well. Adding more seasoning if necessary.
  8. Cover container and place it on the refrigerator for 30 minutes.
  9. Enjoy
