The ingredients needed to make Chocolate peanut butter brownie bars:
  1. Make ready 1 Cup All-purpose Flour
  2. Take 1/2 cup butter
  3. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla essence
  4. Make ready 150 gm 55 % dark chocolate
  5. Get 3/4 cup sugar
  6. Make ready 2 Eggs
  7. Take 1/2 tsp salt
  8. Make ready 2 tbsp peanut butter
  9. Make ready 2 tbsp Milk

But peanut butter, chocolate AND brownies? This treat just went next level friends. BUT it is still so easy to throw together. The brownie is a simple mix The perfect match.

Instructions to make Chocolate peanut butter brownie bars:
  1. Preheat the oven to 180c. Melt the butter and dark chocolate in microwave or using double boiler method.
  2. Whisk together the eggs,sugar,vanilla, salt until they turn frothy and pale
  3. Add the premelted chocolate mixture to the egg mixture
  4. Sieve in the all-purpose flour. And fold it together to form a single homogeneous mixture.And spread into the baking pan.
  5. Add milk to adjust the consistency
  6. In the same bowl of chocolate mixture put the peanut butter and microwave it with little milk for 30 seconds
  7. Now top this mixture to the top of earlier poured batter…and form patterns.
  8. Bake it at 180 c for 25 to 30 min.Cool completely and cut into squares.
