The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Fudge Brownie Sundae:
  1. Prepare all-purpose flour
  2. Get oil
  3. Prepare Coco powder
  4. Take eggs
  5. Take baking powder
  6. Get caster sugar
  7. Take vanilla essenan
  8. Take warm water
  9. Get salt
  10. Prepare Walnut for garnishing
  11. Get Topping
  12. Make ready small packet milk chocolate (melted)
  13. Make ready Coco powder
  14. Take milk
Steps to make Chocolate Fudge Brownie Sundae:
  1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees.
  2. Line your pan with parchment paper.
  3. In a bowl, shift flour, baking powder,salt and Coco powder mix together.
  4. Add eggs and vanilla essenan.
  5. Add warm water and oil. Mix all brownie ingredients in a mixing bowl by using hand whisk or beater.
  6. Pour in a greased pan baked for 25 to 30 minutes. Sprinkle the Walnuts.
  7. Cool in the pan for 10 minutes.Then remove from the tin and allow to cool on a rack,cut into square shape.
  8. Mix the chocolate,Coco powder and milk. Pour the ice cream.
  9. Enjoy the delicious Brownie with vanilla ice cream 😋
