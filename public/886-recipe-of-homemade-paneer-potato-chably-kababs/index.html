The ingredients needed to make Paneer potato chably kababs:
  1. Get 250 gm paneer
  2. Prepare 2 potatoes boil and mash
  3. Get 1 tsp aanardana(dry mango seeds)
  4. Prepare 1 tsp coriander seeds
  5. Get 1 tsp cumin seeds
  6. Get 10 black pepper
  7. Prepare 4 dry red chilli
  8. Prepare 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  9. Prepare 1/2 tsp green chilli paste
  10. Get 1 tsp salt
  11. Get 1 onion chopped omelet cut
  12. Get 1/2 tsp grind garam masala
  13. Get 4 tbsp oil
