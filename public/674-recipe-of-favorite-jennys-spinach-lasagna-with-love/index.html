The ingredients needed to make Jennys spinach lasagna with love:
  1. Prepare 1 box uncooked lasagna
  2. Make ready 4 8 ounce cans of tomatoe sauce
  3. Prepare 1 packages spinach
  4. Take 6 tbsp garlic powder
  5. Take 3 tablespoons onion powder
  6. Prepare 2 tablespoons white sugar
  7. Prepare 6 tablespoons Italian seasoning
  8. Make ready 1 cup parmesan cheese
  9. Take 2 cups ricotta cheese
  10. Make ready 4 cups mozzarella cheese shredded
  11. Prepare 6 tablespoons salt
  12. Take 2 tbsp chilipowder
  13. Prepare 4 eggs

Instructions to make Jennys spinach lasagna with love:
  1. Boil lasagna as directed on box
  2. While your lasagna is boiling start mixing together 2 cups of ricotta cheese 1 cup of parmesan cheese ,1 cup mozzarella cheese and 4 eggs in a bowl .
  3. Your going to season that mixture with 3 tablespoons garlic powder, 3 tablespoons of Italian seasoning and 3 tablespoons of salt .
  4. After that you are going to make your sauce , start to boil the 4 cans of tomatoe sauce then add 3 tablespoons of onion powder ,3 tablespoons of garlic powder ,and 3 tablespoons Italian seasoning ,along with 2 tablespoons sugar 3 tablespoons salt and 2 tablespoons chilipowder
  5. Grease pan then layer bottom with lasagna spread evenly half the cheese mixture then spread half on the sauce . Repeat. Then add remaining mozzarella cheese on top .
