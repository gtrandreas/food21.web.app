To get started with this particular recipe, we have to first prepare a few components. You can cook quick alfredo chicken pizza using 8 ingredients and 8 steps. Here is how you can achieve that.

The ingredients needed to make Quick Alfredo Chicken Pizza:
  1. Take Chicken strips (cooked)
  2. Take creamy Alfredo sauce
  3. Get creamer
  4. Get garlic powder
  5. Take mozzarella cheese
  6. Take Ready to bake pizza crust (I used mini)
  7. Get Pizza sauce
  8. Take Parmesan cheese
Instructions to make Quick Alfredo Chicken Pizza:
  1. Cook chicken strips per directions on box……
  2. Let chicken strips cool down for a bit after baking, then shred strips and place shredded chicken into a large bowl…..
  3. Pour your jar of Alfredo sauce over chicken and your 1/4 cup of creamer, garlic powder and mix all well……
  4. Next stir in your mozzarella cheese……
  5. Spread some pizza sauce over your crust, then place several teaspoons of Alfredo chicken on your pizza crust…..
  6. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese over all…..
  7. Bake in a preheated 450 degree oven for 8-10 minutes or until cheese is fully melted……
