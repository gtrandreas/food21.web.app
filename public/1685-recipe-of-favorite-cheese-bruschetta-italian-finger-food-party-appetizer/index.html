The ingredients needed to make Cheese Bruschetta Italian finger food party appetizer:
  1. Make ready 1 packet Bruschetta or hard crust bread
  2. Get 1 Capsicum (small)
  3. Get 1/2 piece Carrot shredded
  4. Take As needed Cheese regular
  5. Make ready As needed Cheese mozzarella for topping
  6. Prepare 1 Onion
  7. Prepare 1 Tomato
  8. Take 1/2 spoon Refined flour
  9. Take to taste Salt
  10. Make ready 1 pinch Black pepper crushed
  11. Take As needed Chilli flakes
  12. Get 2 tsp Organo or mixed herbs
  13. Make ready 2 tsp Melted butter
  14. Get As needed Olive oil
  15. Prepare Garlic optional
  16. Prepare As needed Mint leaves or basil leaves
Instructions to make Cheese Bruschetta Italian finger food party appetizer:
  1. First wash, peel and cut the veggie in different shapes as shown in the pic (beca'se cooking time of different veggies is n't same)
  2. Take a frying pan.. Put some butter,put the veggies one by one (onions first, tomatoes in the last)and toss them..add spices along with salt (salt should be light) and mix..don't cook much as to maintain the crunch of veggies..add refined flour in the mixture..add regular cheese
  3. Take yo'r bruschettas and spread little melted butter (if u r making with garlic then mix 2-3 cloves of crushed garlic in the melted butter) then spread
  4. Spread veggies mixture on bruschettas and shredd mozerella cheese on top and microwave 30-30 seconds or(roast on non stick tawa for 2 minutes till mozzarella cheese is melted) 2 times and take it out
  5. Yo'r Italian bruschetta r ready..garnish with a drop of tomato ketchup and a (mint,coriander or basil) leaf as u have available
  6. Serve immediately
  7. Instruction ; if u r taking hard bread then cut it in to slices and roast on medium to slow flame till get crunchy
