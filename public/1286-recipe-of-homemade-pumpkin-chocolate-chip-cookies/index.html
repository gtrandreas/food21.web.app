The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin Chocolate Chip Cookies:
  1. Take 3 cups flour
  2. Get 3 tsp baking powder
  3. Get 2 tsp cinnamon
  4. Prepare 1 tsp nutmeg
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp ground ginger
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp ground cloves
  7. Prepare 3 cups chocolate chips
  8. Get 2 tsp salt
  9. Take 1 tsp baking soda
  10. Prepare 1/2 cup molasses (not black strap)
  11. Get 2 tsp milk (I used cashew)
  12. Prepare 1 can (15 oz) pumpkin
  13. Make ready 1 cup brown sugar
  14. Take 3/4 cup canola oil (or refined coconut oil, melted)
  15. Make ready 2 eggs
  16. Get 2 tsp vanilla

Instructions to make Pumpkin Chocolate Chip Cookies:
  1. Preheat oven 375°F
  2. In large bowl add flour, sugar, powder, spices, salt, and chocolate chips. Mix well. Add pumpkin.
  3. Measure out molasses in a large measuring cup, add soda, vanilla, and milk. Combine well and add to bowl.
  4. Whisk eggs in measuring cup and add to bowl. Combine contents of bowl thoroughly
  5. Using #20 scoop, scoop it cookies onto parchment lined baking sheets leaving a little space between each (they will not spread).
  6. Bake 8 minutes and rotate, bake an additional 4 or until cookies are done.
  7. Cool for several minutes in baking sheet before transferring to wire rack to cool completely.
