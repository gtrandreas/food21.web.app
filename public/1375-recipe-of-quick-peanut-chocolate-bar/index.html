The ingredients needed to make Peanut Chocolate Bar:
  1. Make ready 1/2 cup Peanut
  2. Make ready 1/4 cup Jaggery
  3. Get 2 tbsp Butter
  4. Get 1/4 cup Chocolate Horlicks
  5. Take 1/4 cup powdered Marie Biscuits

Steps to make Peanut Chocolate Bar:
  1. Roast Peanuts on tava.
  2. Remove skin and grind coarsely.
  3. Melt butter in a bowl.
  4. Add grated jaggery and keep on heat till it melts.
  5. Remove from heat and add coarsely grounded peanuts.
  6. Add chocolate horlicks and marrie biscuit powder and mix well.
  7. Transfer this mixture to aluminium foil covered pan and press with spoon.
  8. Cover and refrigerate for 2 hours.
  9. Cut in pieces and enjoy.
