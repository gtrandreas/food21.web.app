The ingredients needed to make TL's No Bake After School Granola Bars:
  1. Prepare 1 cup sweetened coconut
  2. Make ready 2 cup quick rolled oats
  3. Make ready 2 cup dry cereal like cheerios or one of your choice
  4. Make ready 1 cup peanut butter
  5. Make ready 1/2 cup honey
  6. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla

Steps to make TL's No Bake After School Granola Bars:
  1. Line a 8x8 pan with aluminum foil. In bowl add cereal, oats and coconut.
  2. In a microwave safe bowl add peanut butter, honey and vanilla and heat for a minute or until mixture will stir smooth.
  3. Pour over the cereal mixture and combine well. Press into prepared pan and refrigerate for an hour. Cut into bars. Keep in airtight container. Enjoy!
