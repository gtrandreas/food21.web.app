The ingredients needed to make Spicy Lady finger with besan:
  1. Get 300 Gm lady fingers
  2. Take 1/2 Cup gram flour
  3. Make ready 1 Tbsp mustard oil
  4. Take 1/2 Tsp cumin seeds
  5. Take 1/2 Tsp carom seeds
  6. Make ready 1 Tbsp Ginger garlic chilli paste
  7. Make ready 2 Chopped green chillies
  8. Get 1/2 Tsp turmeric powder
  9. Prepare 1 Chopped onion
  10. Make ready as per your taste Salt
  11. Prepare 1 Tbsp coriander powder
  12. Make ready 1/2 Tsp chilli powder
  13. Take 1 Tsp dry mango powder
  14. Make ready 1/4 Tsp garam masala
  15. Get 1 Tbsp chopped coriander leaves
Steps to make Spicy Lady finger with besan:
  1. Take the ladyfingers and cut them in to the big pieces. Make sure lady fingers should be dry. Keep them aside. Now take the gram flour,add little salt now add water and make thick and smooth batter,whip for 2-3 minutes. Batter is ready, keep it aside.
  2. In a wide pan,add mustard, now add cumin seeds, carom seeds, ginger garlic paste add chilli paste and mix well. Now add turmeric powder,chopped onion and mix it well. Now add chopped lady fingers and salt. Mix it well and make flame to lowest and cook it for 10 minutes.
  3. In between stir sabji for even cooking. Now bhindi is soft. Move all the bhindi in to the center. Now pour gram flour batter around the bhindi (In a circle direction). Cover and cook for 3-4 minutes or till the gram flour is fully cooked.
  4. After 4 minutes gram flour has been cooked.Now break the gram flour in a small pieces and mix. Now add coriander powder, chilli powder, dry, dry mango powder, garam masala and mix it well and cook it for 2 more minutes. Now garnish with the chopped coriander leaves
