The ingredients needed to make Curry Chicken:
  1. Take 1 chicken thigh
  2. Get 1 onion
  3. Prepare 1 potato
  4. Make ready 1 carrot
  5. Prepare 60 g currycubes
  6. Make ready 15 g curry powder
  7. Make ready 15 g coriander

Instructions to make Curry Chicken:
  1. Cut chicken thigh into 4cm cubes, onion into 1 inch pieces, carrot and potato into 1 inch cubes.
  2. Blanch carrot and potato for 5min.
  3. Heat 2tbsp cooking oil, saute onion for 1min. then add chicken cubes, stir-fry for 2min.
  4. Add 1tbsp curry powder, stir-fry for 1min.
  5. Pour 500ml water in the pot, then put carrot and potato in.
  6. Until the water boil, cover with the lid, cook on medium heat for 15min.
  7. Add curry cubes, stirring till curry cubes are melted. cover and cook on low heat for 10min.
  8. Add coriander before serving.
