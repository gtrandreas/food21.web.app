The ingredients needed to make Breakfast Sausage Spaghetti in Tomato Sauce:
  1. Get 3 Breakfast sausages
  2. Get 1 pints of parsley
  3. Get 100 grams Spaghetti
  4. Make ready 1 Salt and pepper
  5. Prepare 1 cup Tomato sauce for spaghetti
  6. Make ready 2 clove garlic
  7. Take 2 tsp olive oil
  8. Get 500 ml boiling water

Instructions to make Breakfast Sausage Spaghetti in Tomato Sauce:
  1. Mince garlic
  2. Put spaghetti in boiling water.
  3. Cook for 5 mins.
  4. Drain spaghetti in strainer.
  5. Add oil in pan.
  6. Fry breakfast sausages.
  7. Remove from pan after cooked. Cut into pieces.
  8. Add oil in pan with minced garlic.
  9. Put spaghetti, tomato sauce in pan.
  10. Mix and cook for 2 mins.
  11. Mix breakfast sausage with spaghetti in pan.
  12. Add parsley, salt and pepper before serving.
