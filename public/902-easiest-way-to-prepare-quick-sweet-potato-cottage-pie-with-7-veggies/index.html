The ingredients needed to make Sweet potato cottage pie with 7 veggies:
  1. Make ready 800 g lean beef mince
  2. Prepare 3 onions
  3. Prepare Diced garlic
  4. Take 3 carrots
  5. Take 1 courgette
  6. Make ready 1 leek
  7. Prepare 100 g tomato purée (approx half a tube)
  8. Prepare 6 sweet potatoes
  9. Take 3/4 white potatoes
  10. Make ready Cheddar cheese for sprinkling over the top
  11. Make ready 2 Beef stock cubes
  12. Prepare 3 cups water
  13. Take Cornflour
  14. Get Squeeze honey (just because I’m obsessed)
  15. Make ready Black pepper
  16. Prepare Butter & milk for mash

Steps to make Sweet potato cottage pie with 7 veggies:
  1. Peel and cut all potatoes into cubes and cover in water. Boil and simmer till soft. Meanwhile, Fry onions and mince until golden brown. Then add all the veggies, tomatoes purée and beef stock cubes. Mix the flour into a small bit of water slowly to make a paste, then add the rest of the water. Make sure everything is now together except the potatoes.
  2. Ladle into oven proof dish, and set aside. Mash potatoes together with a knob of butter and a dash of milk until soft smooth mash. Layer over the top then sprinkle with mash. I cook mine in a warm oven (180 fan for approx 25/30 mins until bubbling).
