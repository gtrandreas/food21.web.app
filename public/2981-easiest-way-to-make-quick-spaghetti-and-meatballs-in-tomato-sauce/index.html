Steps to make Spaghetti And Meatballs In Tomato Sauce:
  1. Boil your spaghetti in salted water.
  2. Drain your spaghetti and add half tablespoon of olive oil (I love adding olive oil to noodles it gives a nice taste !) , then put aside.
  3. Mix your beef with salt ,pepper, and spices. Then shape in to meatballs.
  4. Fry your meatballs in oil.
  5. Prepare your sauce pan and add abit of olive oil.
  6. Add the tomato sauce, tomato paste ,a bit of salt and half a cup of water. Then cover for 10 minutes.
  7. Add then your meatballs. And abit of Parmesan cheese and let it cook for another 15-20 minutes or until done.
  8. Serve your spaghetti with your meatball sauce topped with some Parmesan cheese ! Happy meal☺️☺️☺️
