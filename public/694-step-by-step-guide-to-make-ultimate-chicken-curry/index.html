The ingredients needed to make Chicken Curry:
  1. Prepare 2 kg Chicken
  2. Take 10 medium Chopped Onion
  3. Take 3 Green Chillies
  4. Prepare 1 Tomato
  5. Take 1 1/2 tbsp Kashmiri Chilli Powder
  6. Take 3 tbsp Coriander Powder
  7. Get 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  8. Take 1 1/2 tsp Black Pepper Powder
  9. Make ready 1/2 tsp Garam Masala
  10. Prepare 2 Ginger
  11. Get 8 Garlic
  12. Take 2 tsp Lemon Juice
  13. Make ready 2 Curry Leaves
  14. Make ready 3 Coriander Leaves
  15. Make ready 250 ml Hot Water
  16. Get Oil
  17. Get Salt

Instructions to make Chicken Curry:
  1. Mix coriander powder, chilli powder, turmeric powder, pepper, garam masala, ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice & salt into a paste. Marinate the chicken in it.
  2. Heat some oil. Add the chopped onions & chillies. After three minutes, add the curry leaves. Cook till onions turn brown.
  3. Chop the tomatoes & add it to the onions. Cook till the tomatoes are mushy.
  4. Add the marinated chicken into this & mix well.
  5. If you want dry chicken curry, do not bother with this step. If you want gravy add 250 ml hot water. Cook well.
  6. One minute before turning off the stove, add the chopped coriander leaves. Add salt if needed. Curry is ready…!!
