Instructions to make Professional cinnamon rolls topped with cream cheese icing:
  1. Scale all the ingredients
  2. Cream the butter , sugar and salt for 3 min
  3. Add the eggs gradually and continue to mix
  4. Add the rest of the dry ingredients such as powder milk, bf, cf, yeast and give it a mix for less than a min
  5. Add milk , continue to mix
  6. Round the dough and place it in a greasy bowl don't forget to cover the bowl , let the dough relax and double in size foe about 1 hour and 15 min
  7. Roll the dough after it doubled in size
  8. Roll roll roll
  9. Mix all the filling ingredients together
  10. Add a little bit of water on the surface of the dough , then add all the amount of filling on the dough
  11. Roll the dough again
  12. Cut the dough
  13. Place the dough in a cake pan , don't forget to butter the pan before placing your cinnamon rolls or you could use parchment paper
  14. The cinnamon rolls at this point has to rest and double in size for about 30 min
