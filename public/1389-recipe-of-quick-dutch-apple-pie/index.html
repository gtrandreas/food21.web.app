Steps to make Dutch apple pie:
  1. preheat oven to 350°F
  2. combine all ingredients for crust in the pie plate and form a ball
  3. press ball of dough out evenly to form the pie crust
  4. put apple pie filling into the pie crust (I used canned but you can make fresh pie filling)
  5. combine topping ingredients (grating frozen butter works well)
  6. use a fork or hands to work topping into a crumble
  7. sprinkle topping over pie
  8. bake 40-50 minutes
