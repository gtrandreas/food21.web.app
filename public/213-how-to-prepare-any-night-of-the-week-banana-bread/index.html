The ingredients needed to make Banana Bread:
  1. Make ready 3 banana
  2. Make ready 1 1/2 flour
  3. Prepare 3/4 brown sugar
  4. Take 2 eggs
  5. Get 1/4 tbsp bakung soda
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp baking powder
  7. Get 1/2 tbsp cinnamon
  8. Make ready 1/4 cup oil

Steps to make Banana Bread:
  1. crush all the bananas.
  2. Add the eggs and the oil and mix.
  3. Add the other ingredients in proportion, mix for 10 minutes or until it looks mixed.
  4. grease pan and cover with a light layer of flour.
  5. preheat oven to 300°f
  6. place in oven for 30 mins or until top looks golden brown.
