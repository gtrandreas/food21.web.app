The ingredients needed to make Blueberry muffin with yoghurt:
  1. Take 35 dkg flour
  2. Make ready 15 dkg sugar
  3. Take 15 dkg yoghurt
  4. Take 1 lemon
  5. Get salt
  6. Get 6 g baking soda
  7. Make ready 3 eggs
  8. Make ready 5 tbs olive oil
  9. Make ready 300 g blueberry
  10. Make ready 100 g chocolate chips

Instructions to make Blueberry muffin with yoghurt:
  1. Prepare for cooking.
  2. Combine the whole wet ingredients. Salt, eggs, lemon, yoghurt, olive oil.
  3. Mix them together.
  4. Combine the whole dry components. Flour, chocholate chips, baking soda, sugar.
  5. Combine the dry and the wet ingedients and mix them.
  6. Preheat your oven to 180 C.
  7. Divide the mix among 12 muffin cases.
  8. Bake 20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean.
