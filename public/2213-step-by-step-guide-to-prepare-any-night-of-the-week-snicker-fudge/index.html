Steps to make Snicker fudge:
  1. FIRST LAYER
  2. Lightly spray 9x13 baking pan with spray
  3. Over medium heat melt milk choc chips, p.b. and butterscotch in small pan
  4. Stir until completely melted pour into lightly greased pan, even out and refrigerate until set
  5. SECOND LAYER
  6. Melt butter over medium-high heat in sauce pan
  7. Add sugar and evaporated milk, bring to boil then cook for 5 minutes, stir constantly
  8. Remove from heat and quickly mix in marshmallow, p.b. and vanilla
  9. Add peanuts to mix, and spread evenly over choc mix
  10. THIRD LAYER
  11. Melt caramels and heavy cream over low heat until smooth
  12. Pour over peanut mix refrigerate until set
  13. FOURTH LAYER
  14. Repeat step one
  15. Melt choc, butterscotch, and p.b. over low heat until smooth
  16. Pour over caramel layer refrigerate for at least 1 hour
