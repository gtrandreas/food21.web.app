The ingredients needed to make Cashew Chicken:
  1. Make ready garlic
  2. Make ready cashews
  3. Take teriyaki sauce
  4. Take collard greens
  5. Get garlic
  6. Get Chicken
  7. Prepare chicken
  8. Get CHIEF Green seasoning
  9. Prepare Adobo seasoning
  10. Get soy sauce
  11. Prepare pepper
  12. Get salt
  13. Get green onions
  14. Make ready onion
  15. Get ginger
Instructions to make Cashew Chicken:
  1. Cut the chicken into slices and place in mixing bowl. Add green seasoning and Adobo. Shake to coat. Place chicken in refrigerator to marinate for 30 minutes.
  2. Cut the white tips off of green onions and discard. Flip onions, cut any brown frayed edges off and discard.
  3. Heat pan on stove.
  4. Dice the onion.
  5. Sauté the green and white onions together with a dash of salt and pepper. Cook down.
  6. Add chicken.
  7. Using a garlic press, slowly add in garlic.
  8. Add in the ginger.
  9. Transfer to pot and add teriyaki sauce. Coat evenly. Begin adding greens and douse with soy sauce. Stir and cover.
  10. Continue adding greens and soy sauce. Cook down (until greens shrivel down)
  11. Add cashews and cover for about 10-15 minutes. Once cashews pop but aren't fully crunchy serve with rice.
