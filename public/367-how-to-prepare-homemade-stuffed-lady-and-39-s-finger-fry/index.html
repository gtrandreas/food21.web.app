The ingredients needed to make Stuffed Lady's Finger Fry:
  1. Prepare 500 gms Lady's Finger
  2. Make ready as required oil to fry
  3. Take Ingredients to grind
  4. Get 1/2 cup coconut
  5. Get 1/2 tbsp cumin seeds
  6. Get Masala powder ingredients
  7. Make ready 1/4 tsp Turmeric powder
  8. Get 1 tsp Salt
  9. Prepare 1 tsp Red chilli powder
  10. Take 1/4 tsp Asafoetida
  11. Take 1/2 tbsp Garam masala powder
  12. Get 1 tbsp Besan flour
Instructions to make Stuffed Lady's Finger Fry:
  1. Grind the ingredients into a fine paste.
  2. Add the masala powders to the grinded paste.
  3. Wash and make lady's finger to dry. Cut into long pieces.
  4. Cut the lady's finger in the middle and keep the stuffing inside.
  5. Heat kadai. Pour oil. Add the stuffed Lady's Finger one by one.
  6. Close the lid and allow to cook on all sides.
  7. Once all sides are fried. Stuffed Lady's Finger Fry is ready.
