The ingredients needed to make Healthy double chocolate cookies no sugar no flour!!:
  1. Make ready 1 cup almond flour
  2. Prepare 1 egg
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup melted no sugar chocolate I use Lily’s dark one!
  4. Take 1 spoon melted coconut oil or ghee
  5. Make ready 1 tsp baking powder
  6. Take 1 spoon vanilla
  7. Make ready 3 Splenda little bags or 3 spoons of monkfruit or stevia it’s up to you!
  8. Take 1/2 cup no sugar chocolate chips! I use Lily’s one again this time I chopped a Lily’s bar for big chunks
  9. Get to taste Walnuts

  4. When your have the dough add the chocolate chips or chunks and walnuts and mix them together, you have to make the cookie form with your hand I make 4 like in the picture and place them in the tray with oven paper so the don’t stick.
