The ingredients needed to make Quick Lasagna:
  1. Get 45 oz Ragu spaghetti sauce
  2. Take 2 lb Hamburger
  3. Make ready 12 oz Oven ready lasagna noodles
  4. Make ready 16 oz Shredded cheese of your choice
  5. Get 1/2 cup Water
Steps to make Quick Lasagna:
  1. Preheat oven to 350?F
  2. In another pan cook noodles until almost done.
  3. In a 13x9 inch greased baking dish start layers of lasagna, hamburger, sauce and cheese
  4. Add a layer of cheese on the top
  5. Bake until done ness that you like. I like the crispy burnt cheese so I baked 90 min
