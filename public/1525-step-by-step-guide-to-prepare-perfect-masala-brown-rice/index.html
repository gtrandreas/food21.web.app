The ingredients needed to make Masala brown rice:
  1. Get 2 cups pre cooked long grain rice
  2. Prepare 1 cup shredded chiken
  3. Prepare 100 grams cooking butter
  4. Take 3 spoon olive oil
  5. Get 1/2 cup cauliflower
  6. Get 1/2 cup green and red capsicum
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup finely chopped onions
  8. Prepare 1 spoon grated ginger and garlic
  9. Make ready 4 spoon gram masala
  10. Get 2 spoon cayenne pepper
  11. Make ready 4 spoon dark soy sauce
  12. Make ready to taste Salt and seasoning

Steps to make Masala brown rice:
  1. Heat the cooking butter on a non stick pot, add the oil to help it melt in time
  2. Add the onions to saute
  3. Add the shredded chicken, ginger and garlic, cook for 3 minutes
  4. Add cauliflower, chili pepper, salt and seasoning to taste
  5. Cook until tender
  6. Place the pre cooked rice on a bowl
  7. Add the gram masala, and cayenne pepper, mix throughly
  8. Pour the rice mix to the chicken, and stir fry for 2 minutes
  9. Add the soy sauce and the capsicum peppers
  10. Ad salt and seasoning to taste
  11. Stir fry for 1 more minute and turn of the heat
  12. Plate and serve
