The ingredients needed to make My Taco seasoning:
  1. Take 2 TBSP hot chili powder
  2. Prepare 1/2 TSP cayenne pepper
  3. Prepare Pinch kosher salt
  4. Make ready 1 1/2 TSP smoked paprika
  5. Take 1 TBSP ground cumin
  6. Prepare 1 TSP ground coriander
  7. Make ready 2 TSP corn starch
  8. Get 2 TSP garlic powder
  9. Make ready 1 cup water

Steps to make My Taco seasoning:
  1. Add all ingredients except water and meat in a jar or small bowl that has a lid.
  2. Cover with lid and shake. (You may store in a air tight container for 4 weeks)
  3. Brown one pound of meat, drain grease and add mixture with the water. Stir untill thickened.
  4. Enjoy!!
