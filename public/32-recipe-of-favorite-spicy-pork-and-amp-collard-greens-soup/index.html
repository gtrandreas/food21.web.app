The ingredients needed to make Spicy Pork & Collard Greens Soup:
  1. Make ready 1 lb Ground Pork
  2. Take 5 Garlic cloves - finely chopped
  3. Prepare 4 tsp Ginger - Finely grated
  4. Prepare 1 tsp Crushed red pepper flakes
  5. Get 1 tsp Cumin seeds - lightly crushed
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp Sunflower oil
  7. Prepare 4 cup Vegetable broth
  8. Make ready 1 bunch Collard Greens
  9. Get 4 Scallions - finely diced
  10. Prepare 2 tbsp Braggs amino acids or Soy sauce
  11. Make ready 4 oz Rice Noodles
Instructions to make Spicy Pork & Collard Greens Soup:
  1. Mix pork, garlic, ginger, red pepper flakes, and cumin in medium bowl
  2. Heat oil in large pot over med heat
  3. Add pork mixture, stir until browned 8-10 min
  4. Add broth, bring to boil. Reduce heat and simmer 8-10min
  5. Add collard greens, scallions, and soy sauce, stirring occasionally until greens are tender 5-8minhg
  6. Meanwhile, cook noodles & drain
  7. Divide noodles among bowls and ladle soup over
