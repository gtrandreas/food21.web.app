The ingredients needed to make Whole wheat banana bread:
  1. Make ready 4 ripe bananas
  2. Take 2 eggs
  3. Get 1 tsp baking soda
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup butter
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  6. Take 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  7. Take 1/2 cup sugar
  8. Prepare 1 1/2 cup whole wheat flour
  9. Take 1/2 cup brown sugar
  10. Make ready 1 1/2 cup milk

Steps to make Whole wheat banana bread:
  1. Preheat oven at 350°
  2. Mix flour, sugars, salt, baking soda
  3. In a separate bowl mash bananas into a purée as finely as possible
  4. Mix wet ingredients (eggs, milk, butter, and vanilla) into its own mixture.
  5. Combine dry and wet mixtures and add to greased and lightly floured bread pan
  6. Bake for 80 mins
