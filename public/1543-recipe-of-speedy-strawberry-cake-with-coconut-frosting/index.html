The ingredients needed to make Strawberry cake with coconut frosting:
  1. Prepare 1/2 cup Strawberry puree (strained)
  2. Get 1/2 cup strawberry Fresh cut pieces
  3. Take 2 cups All purpose flour
  4. Prepare 2 cups Sugar Confectionery
  5. Make ready 3 Eggs
  6. Prepare 1 1/2 tsps Baking soda
  7. Take 1 cup Butter (2 sticks)
  8. Make ready 1/2 cup Buttermilk
  9. Make ready 1/4 cup milk/ water
  10. Take 1 tsp vanilla extract
  11. Make ready 2-9 " inch cake tins
  12. Get 1 cup Coconut milk (drain the excess water in the can)
  13. Get 1 cup Heavy cream
  14. Make ready 1/2 cup sugar Confectionery
  15. Prepare 1 tsp Coconut extract

Steps to make Strawberry cake with coconut frosting:
  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degree F
  2. Strawberry puree is made with 5-6 strawberries and 3 tbsp sugar. Keep it aside.
  3. Cream together butter,sugar and vanilla using hand mixer or beater.
  4. Then add the strawberry puree and mix well.
  5. Add in the eggs one by one and the buttermilk.
  6. Now add the dry ingredients slowly and if the batter is too thick add the milk.
  7. Make sure the flour is well incorporated in the batter.
  8. Lastly mix the strawberry pieces into the cake batter slowly.
  9. "THE CAKE BATTER WHEN POURED INTO PAN SHOULD HAVE A RIBBON LIKE CONSISTENCY". Bake the cake!
  10. FROSTING- Whip the thick coconut milk and heavy cream at medium speed till it forms stiff peaks.
  11. Add in the sugar and vanilla and refrigerate this frosting for about half an hour.
  12. Once cake is cooled..frost it and enjoy!
