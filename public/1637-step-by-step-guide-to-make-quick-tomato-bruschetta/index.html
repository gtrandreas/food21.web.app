The ingredients needed to make Tomato Bruschetta:
  1. Prepare 1 long Baguette
  2. Prepare 2 finely chopped tomatoes
  3. Make ready 1 onion chopped
  4. Make ready 8-10 black olives
  5. Take A few basil leaves
  6. Make ready As required Dried Oregano
  7. Prepare As per taste Finely chopped garlic
  8. Take As required Extra virgin olive
  9. Take 1 tbsp vinegar
  10. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  11. Prepare 1/4 tsp black pepper
  12. Take 1/2 cup grated cheese
  13. Take For garnishing:
  14. Take 2 tbsp melted butter and finely chopped garlic
  15. Take As required Butter paper or parchment paper

Instructions to make Tomato Bruschetta:
  1. Take a bowl, add finely chopped tomato, onion,basil, olives and mix them. Now add some extra virgin olive oil, oregano, black pepper, salt and mix them nicely.
  2. Now take a baking tray, place the butter paper. Now take the fresh long baguette. cut it in to the slices and place them to top of the butter paper.
  3. Now spread the butter with the help of the brush. Put the grated cheese on the slices and bake them for 5 min at 400 degrees fahrenheit. (I avoided cheese so didn’t use.)
  4. Now spread the tomato mixture on the top of the baguette. Bruschetta is ready to serve now.
