Steps to make Bruschetta:
  1. Slice the tomatoes into 1 cm approx pieces (not uniformed) and fine dice the red onion
  2. Chop 1 clove of garlic, sprinkle with flaky salt and crush with the flat edge of a large knife (or use a garlic crusher) then slice or tare the basil leaves
  3. Season the tomatoes with olive oil salt and pepper and toss, leave this mixture to mature for 10 - 20 minutes in the fridge
  4. Slice the ciabatta to around 1 inch thickness and rub both sides with a half a clove of garlic
  5. Drizzle both sides of the bread with olive oil and season with salt and pepper, place croutons in a hot griddle pan. If you don't have a griddle pan you can toast the bread in a very hot oven for about 10 minutes.
  6. Griddle the bread to achieve bar marks on both sides, when well marked rub with the garlic again while its still warm.
