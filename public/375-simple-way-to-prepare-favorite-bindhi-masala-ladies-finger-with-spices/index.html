The ingredients needed to make Bindhi Masala (ladies finger with spices):
  1. Get 1/2 kg chopped ladies finger
  2. Take 7-10 green chillies
  3. Prepare 1 Small piece of grate ginger
  4. Take 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  5. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon mango powder
  6. Prepare 1 tsp coriander powder
  7. Make ready 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  8. Prepare Pinch asafoetida
  9. Get to taste Salt
  10. Take 4 tsp oil
  11. Take 3 chopped tomatoes
Instructions to make Bindhi Masala (ladies finger with spices):
  1. Take a ladies finger chopped it and chopped tomatoes, green chillies & ginger.
  2. Take a pan put oil when oil is heat put pinch of asafoetida, green chillies & ginger than add ladies finger saute for 5 mins. After that than add tomatoes, salt & turmeric powder cover the pan and cook for 10 mins on low gas. After 10 mins put the off leave for 5 mins, than add red chilli powder, coriander powder & mango powder mix well again cook for 5 mins. Ready to serve.
  3. Ready to serve with chapati, it's very light & spicy dish.
