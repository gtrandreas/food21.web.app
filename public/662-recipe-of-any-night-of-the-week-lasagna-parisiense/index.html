The ingredients needed to make Lasagna parisiense:
  1. Take 2 onions
  2. Take 400 grams ham or smoked turkey breast
  3. Prepare 400 grams of your favorite cheese slices
  4. Prepare 400 ml creamed milk
  5. Take 200 grams cream cheese
  6. Make ready 250 grams green frozen peas
  7. Make ready 200 grams champignon, mushrooms of your choice
  8. Make ready 500 grams precooked lasagna dough (about 10-12 pieces)
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp cooking oil
  10. Make ready 1 salsa, oregano, black pepper or spicy paprica according to your taste
  11. Make ready 1 olive oil as you wish

Steps to make Lasagna parisiense:
  1. Dice the onions in small bits
  2. Dice the ham or smoked turkey breast in small bits
  3. Slice the mushrooms as thin as possible
  4. Pre heat a high pan and add the cooking oil
  5. Fry the onions until they start to get brown
  6. Add the diced ham or smoked turkey breast and let it cook for about 2 minutes with the onions
  7. Add the spices, the green frozen peas and mix it evenly
  8. Add the mushrooms
  9. Add the cream cheese and mix evenly
  10. Add the creamed milk and mix it constantly so it won't boil. Use low heat in this step
  11. Let the sauce rest for 5 minutes to assemble the lasagna tray
  12. With a large tray, coat it with olive oil and set a thin layer of sauce
  13. Set a layer of dough, a layer of sliced cheese and another layer of sauce
  14. Repeat step 13 until the tray is filled and top it of with the remaining slices of cheese
  15. Add another coat of olive oil and sprinkle oregano as you'd like
  16. Pre heat the oven to 180CÂ° for 5 minutes and add the lasagna
  17. Observe and remove it when the top layer of cheese is boiling (about 10-15 minutes)
  18. Cut, serve and enjoy!
