The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin Chocolate Chip Cookies:
  1. Get 3/4 cup Sugar
  2. Get 1 stick Butter (1/2 cup)
  3. Get 1 tsp Vanilla
  4. Take 6 tbsp Pumpkin purée
  5. Get 1 1/2 cup Flour
  6. Make ready 1/4 tsp Salt
  7. Take 1/4 tsp Baking powder
  8. Get 1/4 tsp Baking soda
  9. Take 1 cup Chocolate chips
  10. Make ready 1 pinch Cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger

I did change a few things though: I used fresh not canned pumpkin that I baked and pureed. These pumpkin chocolate chip cookies are super soft and pillowy and packed with pumpkin pie flavor! Bonus: this recipe makes a BIG batch of cookies. These pillowy, cakey, pumpkin chocolate chip cookies fit that bill.

Instructions to make Pumpkin Chocolate Chip Cookies:
  1. Mix together butter, sugar, vanilla, and pumpkin.
  2. Make sure there are no sugar lumps, and add the dry ingredients.
  3. Once well blended, chill in the fridge for at least 30 minutes. Don't skip this part!!! It is part of making your cookies deliciously chewy.
  4. After chilling, roll into balls on a prepared pan. (Oven on 350)
  5. Lightly press down and bake for 8-10 minutes.
  6. Right when they come out, press them down a bit with a fork or knife. You can also add some chocolate chips on top to make they pretty and melty.
  7. Now your done!!! You can eat them now, or leave them out for awhile uncovered. They will become chewier. Enjoy! ; )
