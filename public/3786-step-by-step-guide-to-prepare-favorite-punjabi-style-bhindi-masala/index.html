Steps to make Punjabi style bhindi masala:
  1. STEP 1-Take a pan,Add 1-2 tbsp oil,add bhindi and fry for 2-3 min.keep stirring on low flame.
  2. STEP2- remove it on plate and keep it aside.
  3. STEP3- take same pan add 2tbsp oil,add 02 dry red chilli (saute for a minute)and 1/2 tsp hing.
  4. STEP4- Add chopped 5-6 garlic cloves,saute till raw aroma of garlic disappear
  5. STEP5- Add sliced onions and fry till they become translucent.
  6. STEP6- Add tomato puree and cook on medium flame.
  7. STEP7- ADD spices- 1/2 tbsp chilli powder,1/2 tbsp coriander powder,1/2 tbsp turmeric powder,1/2 tsp garam masala.
  8. STEP8- Add kasoori methi(optional) and add salt to taste. Add 01 small bowl water and Mix everything well
  9. STEP 9- cook gravy till it starts leaving oil and then add fried bhindis.
  10. STEP 10- cover on low flame 5-6 min
  11. STEP11- Open and mix it well.serve hot with chappati
