Instructions to make Kalah's fall-off-the-bone super moist roasted chicken:
  1. Preheat oven to 450°F.
  2. Mix together the oil and vinegars. Add the 1 teaspoons each of granulated garlic, onion powder, Italian seasoning, and 1/2 teaspoon of lemon pepper; set aside.
  3. Mix together the rest of the seasonings and set aside.
  4. Clean and remove the skin from the chicken; cut up if needed.
  5. Rub the chicken all over with the seasoning mixture.
  6. Begin to coat the chicken, one piece at a time, with the oil, vinegars, and seasoning mixture.
  7. Add the chicken to a large aluminum foil lined roasting pan.
  8. Roast the chicken for 1 1/2 hours.
  9. Mix together the remaining ingredients in a bowl.
  10. After 1 1/2 hours, take the chicken out of the oven, remove some of the juices; cover the chicken with the soup mixture and return to the oven.
  11. Continue cooking for another half an hour.
  12. Remove from the oven and your all done!
