The ingredients needed to make Peanut butter (Reeses) Bars:
  1. Take 2 cup Graham cracker crumbs
  2. Get 2 cup Confectioners' sugar
  3. Take 1 cup Peanut butter
  4. Get 1 1/2 cup Semisweet chocolate chips or morsels
  5. Prepare 1 cup Melted Butter

Steps to make Peanut butter (Reeses) Bars:
  1. In a bowl, combine butter, graham cracker crumbs, peanut butter, & sugar.
  2. Mix well until blended, & pour into a 9x13 pan, spread evenly.
  3. In a double-boiler or microwave, melt the chocolate chips, stir til smooth.
  4. Spread chocolate over the peanut butter mixture in the pan.
  5. Cut into squares. Refrigerate til chilled well. Serve.
