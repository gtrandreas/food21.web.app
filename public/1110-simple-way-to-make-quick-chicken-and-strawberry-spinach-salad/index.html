The ingredients needed to make Chicken and Strawberry-Spinach Salad:
  1. Take 3 T apple juice
  2. Get 2 T strawberry spreadable fruit
  3. Prepare 2 T balsamic vinegar
  4. Take 1 lb chicken breast, boneless and skinless
  5. Get 8 c spinach
  6. Take 1 c strawberries, halved
  7. Take 1/4 c crumbled Gorgonzola cheese
  8. Make ready 2 T walnuts, finely chopped

Steps to make Chicken and Strawberry-Spinach Salad:
  1. In small bowl, mix juice, spreadable fruit and vinegar.
  2. In skillet with cooking spray, cook chicken until no longer pink. Remove from skillet and set aside.
  3. To skillet, add strawberry mixture stirring any chicken drippings loose.
  4. Slice chicken and place on bed of spinach. Top with strawberries and cheese. Drizzle with strawberry dressing. Sprinkle with walnuts.
