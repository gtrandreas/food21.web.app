The ingredients needed to make Exchange Student Life Hacks: Eggplant Omelette (Torta):
  1. Prepare vegetables
  2. Take 7 Chinese eggplants
  3. Prepare 1 spring onion, finely chopped
  4. Prepare eggs and dairy
  5. Get 6 large eggs
  6. Make ready spices and base ingredients
  7. Take 2/3 cup olive oil
  8. Prepare 1 5 spice powder (optional), to taste
  9. Take 1 salt, to taste
  10. Get 1 ground black pepper, to taste
Steps to make Exchange Student Life Hacks: Eggplant Omelette (Torta):
  1. boil or grill eggplants until they turn soft.
  2. beat eggs along with salt, pepper, spring onions, and 5 spice
  3. once eggplants are soft, peel off skin and mash with fork until they reach tge desired flattened shape.
  4. soak both sides of eggplant in beaten egg mixture.
  5. heat olive oil in a frying pan and fry battered eggplants on both sides.
  6. serve with ketchup and rice. share, make merry, and have fun!
