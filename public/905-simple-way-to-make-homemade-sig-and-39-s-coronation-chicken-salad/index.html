The ingredients needed to make Sig's Coronation Chicken Salad:
  1. Prepare Chicken
  2. Make ready 1 tbsp olive oil
  3. Make ready 850 grams chicken breast filet
  4. Take 2 tbsp paprika
  5. Get 1 tbsp salt
  6. Take 1 tbsp dried parsley
  7. Make ready 1 pinch each of pepper and cayenne pepper
  8. Prepare Mayonnaise
  9. Get 1/2 cup mayonnaise homemade or good quality bought one
  10. Prepare 1/2 cup creme fraiche
  11. Prepare 1/4 cup homemade or bought mango chutney
  12. Make ready 2 tsp curry powder
  13. Get 300 grams mixed, seedless grapes
  14. Get 1 cup halved pecan nuts
  15. Make ready 1 handful snow pea sprouts or coriander
  16. Get Salad
  17. Prepare 1/2 onion sliced finely
  18. Get 1/2 head lettuce of choice that is not crispy
  19. Take 2 tbsp rasberry vinegar
  20. Get 80 ml oil
  21. Take 60 ml olive oil
Instructions to make Sig's Coronation Chicken Salad:
  1. Heat spoonful of oil in pan, rub the spices and parsley into chicken. Cook gently all the way through do not let get burned. Set aside to cool, slice into 5- 10 mm wide slices.
  2. Place all ingredients for mayonnaise into bowl mix gently, chill. Keep a few grapes and nuts for garnish.
  3. Arrange the lettuce and onions on plate. Mix vinegar with oil and olive oil, drizzle over lettuce
  4. Arrange all three on a plate garnish with nuts and grapes
