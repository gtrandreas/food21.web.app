Instructions to make Yellow chicken curry:
  1. Shallow fry pre-seasoned chicken, not touching, in metal oven-proof pan, to golden.
  2. Remove chicken and fry onion, chili and pepper in the hot fat
  3. Add tomato paste, then vinegar, sizzle
  4. Add paste plus half litre water and turn down heat
  5. Add tin chickpeas and put chicken back in
  6. Put pan in oven. 180C/ 350Â°F 45 mins
  7. Steam rice, add lemon rind on top, serve w wedges of lemon on side. drizzle of yoghurt and all the remaining coriander leaves. top w a little chopped red chili
