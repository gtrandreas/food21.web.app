The ingredients needed to make Best Brownies:
  1. Get 1 egg collect the yolk
  2. Take 4 tablespoons sugar
  3. Take Half a bag of melted chocolate 🍫
  4. Make ready 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  5. Take 1 tablespoons flour
  6. Prepare 1 tablespoons powdered sugar
  7. Get Non-melted chocolate chips
Steps to make Best Brownies:
  1. Get a egg collect the yolk add the sugar and powdered sugar
  2. Now melt your chocolate add it to your mixture
  3. Now add the rest of the ingredients except the chocolate chips
  4. Bake for about 25 minutes if not baked bake for another 10 minutes
  5. Now the best part 😊 ENJOY
  6. Add your mixture to a pan and then add your chocolate chips
