  5. Prepare 500 gm Liver washed n cubed
  6. Prepare 4 or more tablespoons Cooking cream
  7. Get to taste Salt n black pepper
  8. Prepare Ugali to serve with
  9. Get Cooking oil

Instructions to make Liver, Creamed Collard Greens (Skuma wiki) Served with Ugali:
  1. In a pot, add a little oil and 1 onion. Fry til translucent. Add Skuma and fry.
  2. Add salt and cook til almost done. 4 minutes should be enough.
  3. Add the Cooking cream. Let cook through..about a minute. Remove from heat.
  4. In another pot, put the liver to heat. Since you washed it, it'll cook in its own liquid.
  5. Once all liquid is cooked out, add oil and onions. Fry til it gets a nice brown colour.
  6. Add tomatoes and let them cook through n soften completely.
  7. Add salt n pepper. Add coriander and remove from heat. Enjoy.
