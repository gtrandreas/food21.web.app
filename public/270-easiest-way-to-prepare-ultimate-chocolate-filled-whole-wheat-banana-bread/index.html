The ingredients needed to make Chocolate filled whole wheat banana bread:
  1. Take 3 riped banana
  2. Prepare 2 eggs
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup thick curd
  4. Prepare 1/3 cup honey
  5. Prepare 4-5 drops vanilla escence
  6. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon powder(optional)
  7. Get 1 cup whole wheat flour
  8. Make ready 1 pinch salt
  9. Prepare 11/2 teaspoon baking powder
  10. Prepare 1/4 cup melted chocolate or 1/4 cup cocoa powder+3 tablespoonmilk

Steps to make Chocolate filled whole wheat banana bread:
  1. Before doing the preparations first pre heat your oven at 170 degree centigrate for 10 minutes.
  2. 1.Take a big bowl add in 3 bananas and smash them with the help of fork.
  3. 2.crack open 2 eggs and mix
  4. 3.add in thick curd and mix once again all the ingredients uniformly
  5. 4.now add in honey
  6. 5.add salt a pinch
  7. 6.cinnamon powder and vanilla escence and mix
  8. 7.add in 1 cup whole wheat flour and mix.
  9. 8.add in 11/2 teaspoon baking powder and mix with your light hands.
  10. 9.now take some part(5-6 tablespoon) of this banana bread mixture in another small bowl and add melted chocolate mixture and mix with each other.
  11. 10.if you dont have chocolate, you can also take 1/4 cup cocoa powder and make a paste with 3-4 tablespoon of milk.
  12. 11.grease one rectangle shape 4x8 inches baking dish and spread some dry whole wheat flour.
  13. 12.pour banana egg mixture(white one)into a greased baking dish.
  14. 13.now pour chocolate mixture over the white mixture in 3 batches with a gap of 2 inches with each other.
  15. Now insert a knife and make a swirl from inside to outside.
  16. 14.gently tap the baking dish against the slab so that air bubbles comes out from the batter
  17. 15.spread some chopped cashew and almonds.
  18. 16.keep it inside the preheated oven for 45 minutes at 170 degree centigrate. Or until tip of the tooth pick comes out clean.
  19. 17.let it cool for 10 minutes and cut into pieces and enjoy them with your evening coffee.
