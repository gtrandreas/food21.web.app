  5. Prepare 1 tsp baking powder
  6. Make ready 1 tablespoon flavor (use supreme flavor
  7. Take 7 eggs (whipped to stiff peaks)
  8. Prepare Half full cup of buttermilk
  9. Prepare 2 tablespoon oil
