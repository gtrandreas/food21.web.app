The ingredients needed to make Cajun Chicken with Chilli Potatoes and Cucumber Salad:
  1. Take Meat
  2. Make ready 1 Chicken Breast
  3. Get 1 A pinch of salt
  4. Make ready 1 A dash of ground white pepper(depends on how spicy you want)
  5. Make ready 1 A dash of black pepper(depends on how spicy you want it)
  6. Prepare 3 tbsp of Cajun Seasoning
  7. Get 1 A dash of dried oregano
  8. Prepare Chilli Potatoes
  9. Get 2 Potatoes
  10. Get 3 tbsp of chilli powder(depends on how spicy you want)
  11. Make ready 3 tbsp of Cajun seasoning
  12. Prepare 1 A pinch of salt
  13. Make ready Cucumber Salad
  14. Take 1 Cucumber
  15. Take 1 small onion
  16. Prepare 2 red chillies
  17. Make ready 5 tbsp yogurt
Steps to make Cajun Chicken with Chilli Potatoes and Cucumber Salad:
  1. Heat up a pan with 2 teaspoons of olive oil
  2. Marinate the chicken with all the ingredients needed for the meat
  3. Boil 2 potatoes(skin on) until your fork can go through half an inch
  4. Place chicken on the pan. Cook it for around 4 minutes each side or until cooked
  5. Cut the potatoes into wedges and marinate with the required ingredients
  6. Saut√© the potatoes in the same pan until its cooked
  7. Cut the cucumbers into small pieces
  8. Cut chillies and onions
  9. Add chillies and onions to the cucumber
  10. Mix cucumber with yogurt
  11. Set it in the fridge for about an hour
  12. Plate and serve. Chicken and Potatoes should be hot while the cucumbers should be cold
