The ingredients needed to make Tawa Veg:
  1. Prepare Baby Potato, (Aloo), half boiled (halves)
  2. Get -Ladyfinger, (Bhindi)
  3. Prepare Bitter gourd, (Karela)
  4. Make ready Colocasia (Arbi)
  5. Take Tomato, finely chopped
  6. Take Ginger + Garlic paste
  7. Get mustard oil
  8. Take leaves Coriender
  9. Make ready large onion, finely chopped
  10. Prepare cumin seeds
  11. Prepare mustard seeds
  12. Take coriender seeds
  13. Make ready fennel seeds
  14. Get black peppercorns
  15. Make ready aamchur powder
  16. Prepare red chili powder
  17. Get turmeric powder
  18. Take garam masala
  19. Prepare Salt
Steps to make Tawa Veg:
  1. Wash the Vegetables and keep aside.
  2. Take a nonstick pan heat on add, cumin, coriander, fennel seeds, mustard seeds, black peppercorns roast on low flame for 3-4 minutes and then grind it keep aside.
  3. Slit the bhindi and karela & prick baby potatoes and arbi.
  4. Take a nonstick Tawa add 1 tbsp mustard oil add onion saute for 1 minutes add tomato and green chilli & ginger + Garlic paste & roasted spices powder stir well. Sprinkle coriender leaves.
  5. Add salt, aamchur powder, garam masala, red chili powder, turmeric powder stir fry for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Fill each the vegetables with this stuffing and place on the same tawa. Potato and arbi coat nicely into the mixture, drizzle 1 tbsp oil.
  7. Cook on medium flame stir fry 10-12 minutes until crisp.
  8. Serve with chapati.
