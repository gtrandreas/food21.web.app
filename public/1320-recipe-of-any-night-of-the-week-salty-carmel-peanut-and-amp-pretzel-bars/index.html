The ingredients needed to make Salty Carmel Peanut & Pretzel Bars:
  1. Take Blondie bottom layer
  2. Take 2 tsp Baking Powder
  3. Get 1 1/2 cup AP Flour
  4. Take 2 tsp salt
  5. Get 1 1/2 cup Soft Butter
  6. Make ready 2 each Eggs @ room temp
  7. Take 1 1/2 tbsp Vanilla Extract
  8. Take 2 cup Packed Brown Sugar
  9. Take For The Carmel Layer
  10. Get 16 oz Carmels
  11. Get 2 cup Roasted Salted Peanuts
  12. Prepare 2 cup Slightly crushed tiny pretsels
  13. Take Final Drizzle
  14. Make ready 2/3 cup Milk Chocolate
  15. Get 1/2 tsp Coconut Oil
Steps to make Salty Carmel Peanut & Pretzel Bars:
  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Add flower, baking powder & salt stir well and set aside.
  3. I used a food processor. Add sugar,butter, eggs, vanilla & whip till creamy. Add flour mixture a little at a time untill it becomes smooth.
  4. Line the bottom of 13+9 cookie pan with foil and spray with nonstick spray.
  5. Pour cake mixture into lined pan and smooth out.
  6. Bake for 20-25 mins or until golden brown & tooth pick comes out clean.
  7. While your cake is cooling melt caramels in the microwave at 30 second intervals. Untill melted, if your caramel is to thick add a few tablespoons of heavy cream to thin it out.
  8. When caramel is melted and while its still warm add nuts & pretzels, stir quickly to combine.
  9. Melt your Chocolate & Coconut Oil in microwave just like you did the caramel. Put into corner of a plastic bag and pipe over the top generously.
