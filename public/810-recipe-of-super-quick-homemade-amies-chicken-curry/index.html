The ingredients needed to make AMIEs CHICKEN CURRY:
  1. Take 1 kg chicken, cut up into serving portion
  2. Make ready 1 can coconut milk
  3. Prepare 1 onion, chopped
  4. Take 1 clove garlic, minced
  5. Make ready 1 large potato, diced
  6. Prepare 1 medium carrot, sliced
  7. Prepare 1 1/2 cup warm water
  8. Get 1 tsp brown sugar
  9. Take 2 tbsp cooking oil
  10. Make ready 1 salt and pepper to taste
  11. Make ready 3 tbsp curry powder
  12. Get 1 tsp chili powder (you can add more if u want spicy)
  13. Get 3 tbsp lemon or calamansi juice

Instructions to make AMIEs CHICKEN CURRY:
  1. Saute garlic and onion in cooking oil. Drop in chicken pieces, cook covered for 5 minutes.
  2. Add the curry and chili powder and mix until equally distributed to the chicken. Pour in coconut milk and water. Let it boil for 10 minutes, stirring ocassionally.
  3. Add vegetables, calamansi or lemon juice and sugar. Mix well. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Simmer gently, stirring frequently until chicken and vegetables is tender.
  5. Serve with hot steamed rice. Share and enjoy!
