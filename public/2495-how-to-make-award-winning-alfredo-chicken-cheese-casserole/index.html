The ingredients needed to make Alfredo Chicken Cheese Casserole:
  1. Take Onion sliced
  2. Get pepper
  3. Take olive oil
  4. Get butter
  5. Take Jar of Alfredo sauce
  6. Prepare skim milk
  7. Get diced cooked chicken
  8. Make ready Salt and pepper
  9. Get Spaghetti pasta
  10. Prepare mozzarella cheese
  11. Prepare parmesan cheese
Instructions to make Alfredo Chicken Cheese Casserole:
  1. Break spaghetti pasta in half. Cook according to direction on box. Drain
  2. While pasta is cooking. Slice onions, cook onions in olive oil until caramelized. Cut peppers into stripes and add to onions. Cook until tender. Melt butter in pan with onions and peppers.
  3. Add alfredo sauce, milk and diced chicken to onions and peppers. Salt and pepper. Stir add pasta. Mix all together. Pour into greased casserole dish.
  4. Spread mozzarella and parmesan cheese on top. Bake on 350 for 15 to 20 minutes. Untill cheese is melted.
