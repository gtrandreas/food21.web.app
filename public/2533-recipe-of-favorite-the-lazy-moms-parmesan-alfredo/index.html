The ingredients needed to make THE LAZY MOMS PARMESAN ALFREDO:
  1. Take any kind of pasta
  2. Take cooked small boneless chicken breasts
  3. Get ragu alfredo sauce
  4. Get chopped broccoli
  5. Prepare diced tomatos
  6. Get garlic
  7. Prepare parmesean cheese
  8. Take lemon herb spice

Instructions to make THE LAZY MOMS PARMESAN ALFREDO:
  1. preheat oven to 350°
  2. bake boneless breast until cooked all the way. cube and set aside
  3. steam broccoli and set aside. i steamed mine in the microwave
  4. boil water for pasta
  5. cook pasta as directed and strain until most of the water runs off.
  6. pour entire can of Alfredo ssauce in bottom of a pot on the stove set on low temp. add broccoli, garlic, diced tomatoes, 1/2 of the parmesan cheese, and lemon herb spice.
  7. pour the pasta ontop of the sauce mixture and stir very well. if you need to add a little olive oil. i added in about a quarter of a cup to mine to make the sauce go farther
  8. plate it up and salt ant pepper to taste. sprinkle on a little extra parmesan cheese before serving.
