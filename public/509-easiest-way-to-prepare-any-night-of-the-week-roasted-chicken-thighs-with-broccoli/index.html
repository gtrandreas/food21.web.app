The ingredients needed to make Roasted Chicken Thighs with Broccoli ðŸ¥¦:
  1. Make ready 10 cloves minced garlic
  2. Prepare 1/8 teaspoon ground ginger
  3. Get 1/8 teaspoon crushed red pepper
  4. Take 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  5. Take 1 can (14.5 ounce) reduced sodium chicken broth
  6. Get 2 tablespoons teriyaki sauce
  7. Prepare 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  8. Make ready 4 tablespoons honey
  9. Make ready 1 large pack chicken thighs (skin on, bone in)
  10. Get 1 large pack frozen broccoli florets
  11. Prepare Parsley flakes
  12. Make ready Garlic powder
Steps to make Roasted Chicken Thighs with Broccoli ðŸ¥¦:
  1. In a bowl combine: minced garlic, ginger, crushed red pepper, black pepper, chicken broth, teriyaki sauce, soy sauce and honey, mix well and set to the side……
  2. Rinse and pat dry your chicken thighs and place into a large baking dish….
  3. Place frozen broccoli florets into baking pan with chicken thighs……
  4. Sprinkle all with parsley flakes and garlic powder….
  5. Stir your wet ingredients together and pour over chicken thighs and broccoli…..
  6. Cover and bake at 400 degrees for 1 hour…..
  7. Remove cover and increase oven temp to 450 degrees, baste chicken and broccoli, then bake uncovered for 35- 40 minutes….. Chicken should be golden on top !
  8. Serve with white rice and enjoy ðŸ˜‰!!!
