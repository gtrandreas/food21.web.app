The ingredients needed to make chicken curry:
  1. Take 1 cup onion
  2. Get 4 1/2 clove garlic
  3. Take 1 small ginger
  4. Prepare 1 large potato (if preferred)
  5. Prepare 1 large carrots
  6. Prepare 1/2 slice bell peppers (diff.colors)
  7. Take 2/3 lb chicken meat
  8. Make ready 1 1/2 tbsp boullion
  9. Take 1 can coconut milk
  10. Prepare 1 tbsp pepper
  11. Take 3 boiled eggs (if preffered)

Instructions to make chicken curry:
  1. Sautee garlic,onion, ginger
  2. Add meat then cover to tenderize the meat
  3. Add pepper then cover
  4. Add boullion
  5. Add a can of coconut milk slowly, mix then cover
  6. Let it Simmer
  7. Add curry powder
  8. Add potato and carrots, let it simmer until it is tender
  9. Add boiled eggs
  10. Add bell pepper last.
