The ingredients needed to make Stuffed lady fingers:
  1. Make ready 200 gram Lady fingers
  2. Prepare 4 tsp Peanut powder
  3. Make ready 4 tsp Desicated coconut
  4. Make ready 1 tsp Ginger garlic paste
  5. Make ready 4 tsp Oil

  7. Take 1 tsp Red chilli powder

  9. Get 1 tsp Garam masala

Instructions to make Stuffed lady fingers:
  1. Wash and dry the lady finger with cloth
  2. Make long cut to lady finger don't cut into pieces
  3. Add peanut powder,desicated coconut,red chilli powder,turmeric powder,salt,1tsp oil and mix well Stuffing is ready
  4. Now stuff the lady finger with stuffing masala
  5. Heat oil in pan add cumin seeds,when flutters add ginger garlic paste mix well saute for 1 minute add remaining Stuffing masala and saute on low flame
  6. When it looses oil add stuffed lady finger and mix well late it cook on low flame till softens. Stir it meanwhile.
  7. Stuffed lady finger is ready
