The ingredients needed to make Chicken Curry in Bangladeshi Style:
  1. Get Ingredients :
  2. Take o Chicken (500 gm)
  3. Make ready o Red Chilli powder (1.5 tsp)
  4. Get o Soya bean oil (5 tbsp)
  5. Make ready o Turmeric powder (1 tsp)
  6. Make ready o Onion (3 medium size sliced)
  7. Get o Ginger paste (1 tsp)
  8. Make ready o Garlic paste (1 tbsp)
  9. Prepare o Cumin fried powder (1 tsp)

  14. Get o Water (5 cup)
