The ingredients needed to make Arpik's apple pie:
  1. Get 3/4 cup white sugar
  2. Prepare 2 tbsp flour
  3. Prepare 7 cup thinly sliced apples
  4. Prepare 2 packages phyllo dough
  5. Prepare 1/2 tbsp cinnamon
  6. Prepare 1/4 tbsp nutmeg
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp ground cloves
  8. Make ready 1/2 tbsp lemon zest
  9. Make ready 2 tsp lemon juice
  10. Prepare 1 tbsp butter
  11. Get 1 pinch milk

Steps to make Arpik's apple pie:
  1. Peel apples, slice thinly, add lemon quickly so that they don't get brown
  2. Mix sugar, spices and flour
  3. Mix spices with apples
  4. Open 1 dough, put into the pan
  5. Add apples and small chunks of butter on top
  6. Open 2nd dough, cut into small slices and pleat them on top of apples
  7. Preheat oven to 220 C
  8. Brush with milk for glazed appearance
  9. Bake at 220 C for 40-50mins
