Instructions to make Peanut Butter Bars:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°
  2. In a mixing bowl, cream butter, sugars, and peanut butter.
  3. Add egg and vanilla; mix well
  4. Combine flour, oats, baking soda, and salt; stir into the creamed mixture
  5. Spread into a greased 13x9x2 baking pan. Sprinkle with chocolate chips.
  6. Bake for 20-25 min. Or until lightly browned
  7. Cool 10 minutes
  8. Combine icing ingredients; drizzle over bars
  9. Makes 3-4 dozen
