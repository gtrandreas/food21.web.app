The ingredients needed to make Fudge Brownies <3:
  1. Make ready 4 eggs
  2. Get 3/4 cup butyer or margarine
  3. Prepare 2 cup sugar
  4. Prepare 1 1/2 cup plaon flour
  5. Get 2 tsp vanilla essence
  6. Prepare 3/4 cup grated chocolate
  7. Prepare 5 tbsp cocoa powder
  8. Prepare 2 cup nuts (optional)
Instructions to make Fudge Brownies <3:
  1. preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Melt butter
  3. Add grated chocolate to butter on Bain Marie
  4. mix in cocoa powder
  5. stir in sugar
  6. blend in the eggs, one at a time
  7. stir in flour (and nuts)
  8. bake in greased pan for 30 min or until skewer comes out clean
  9. Let it cool on rack and cut into pieces
