The ingredients needed to make Pancit Canton:
  1. Prepare flour sticks pancit canton noodles
  2. Take pork belly cut into strips
  3. Get medium size shrimp
  4. Prepare garlic
  5. Take medium onion
  6. Prepare Chinese sausage
  7. Make ready medium carrot
  8. Make ready parsley
  9. Take cabbage
  10. Make ready pork broth or 4 cups of water with 1 pork cube
  11. Make ready oyster sauce
  12. Take fish sauce
  13. Get soy sauce
  14. Prepare olive oil
  15. Take Salt and pepper to your taste
Steps to make Pancit Canton:
  1. In a wok put one tbsp olive oil, cabbage and carrots and cook for 3 minutes and set aside
  2. In a separate wok heat rest of oil and sauté garlic and onion
  3. Add pork strips and cook until tender
  4. Add fish sauce, Chinese sausage, and soy sauce and cook for 3 minutes
  5. Add shrimp, parsley and oyster sauce and cook for 3 more minutes
  6. Add pork broth or water and pork cube and let it simmer for a few minutes then add noodles
  7. Cook until noodles are tender and some of the soup is absorbed
  8. Add the vegetables you set aside and tossed it with the noodles
  9. Add salt and pepper for taste as necessary
  10. Transfer to a serving plate and add desired calamansi or lemon and enjoy!
