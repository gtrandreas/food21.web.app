The ingredients needed to make Balsamic Bruschetta:
  1. Make ready 8 Roma tomatoes, diced
  2. Take 1/3 cup chopped fresh basil
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup shredded or grated Parmesan cheese
  4. Prepare 2 cloves garlic, minced
  5. Take 1 tbs balsamic vinegar
  6. Get 1 tbs Olive oil
  7. Get 1/4 tbs kosher salt
  8. Take 1/4 tbs ground black pepper
  9. Prepare 1 loaf French bread toasted and sliced
Steps to make Balsamic Bruschetta:
  1. In a small bowl, toss together, basil, Parmesan cheese, garlic, balsamic vinegar, olive oil, kosher salt and pepper.
  2. Serve on toasted bread and enjoy!
