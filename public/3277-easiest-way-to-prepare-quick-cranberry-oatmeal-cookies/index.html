Steps to make Cranberry oatmeal cookies:
  1. Cream butter and sugar.
  2. Add eggs, one at a time beating well. Add vanilla at this time.
  3. Combine flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt. Add to creamed mixture.
  4. Stir in oats, cranberries, and orange peel. Stir in chips. Drop by spoonfuls on cookie sheets.
  5. Bake at 375°F for 10 to 12 minutes. Cool on wire racks. Makes about 6 dozen.
