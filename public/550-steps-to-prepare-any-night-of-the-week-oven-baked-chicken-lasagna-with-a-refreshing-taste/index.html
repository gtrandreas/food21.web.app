The ingredients needed to make Oven Baked Chicken Lasagna with a Refreshing Taste:
  1. Prepare 1 package Lasagna Sheets
  2. Take 750 grams Chicken minced meat
  3. Take 1 large Carrot chopped
  4. Make ready 2 Red Bell Pepper chopped
  5. Get 1 Onion chopped (optional)
  6. Make ready 2 Garlic Cloves diced (optional)
  7. Take 5 Tomatoes grated
  8. Prepare 1/2 cup Olive Oil
  9. Prepare 3 cups Chicken broth
  10. Make ready 3 cups Parmesan Cheese
  11. Prepare Salt
  12. Make ready Sugar
  13. Take Bechamel Sauce
  14. Prepare 1 teaspoon Sugar
Instructions to make Oven Baked Chicken Lasagna with a Refreshing Taste:
  1. On medium heat add olive oil into large sauce pan, along with the chicken meat and the peppers. Sauté ingredients until meat has been cooked.
  2. Continue by adding in the tomatoes, chicken broth, salt, sugar, carrot, onion, and garlic. I haven’t added onion or garlic into this meat sauce because I wanted the sauce to have a lighter taste. Let this cook until broth has been absorbed.
  3. Once your meat sauce is ready add in 2 cups Parmesan Cheese and stir.
  4. On the bottom of a baking pan spread about 2 tablespoons of ready made tomato sauce. I usually do this so that the lasagna sheets don’t stick to the bottom of the pan, but you can also use olive oil, or butter. Start to layer the sheets as shown in picture.
  5. Continue by adding the meat sauce. Layer sheets and once more the meat sauce.
  6. Add Bechamel Sauce and top with 1 cup of Parmesan Cheese. Yesterday, I posted the recipe for the Bechamel Sauce, so you can use that dosage for this recipe. 🙂
  7. Bake for 1 hour and until Bechamel sauce is golden brown. Once baked let this sit for 1/2 hour to cool. This way it is easier to cut pieces and serve. - - Kali Orexi!! 🙂
