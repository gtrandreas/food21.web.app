The ingredients needed to make Japanese Curry Chicken:
  1. Make ready 1 tbsp olive oil
  2. Make ready 1 medium yellow sweet onion
  3. Take 2 lb boneless skinless chicken thighs
  4. Make ready 2 tsp seasoning salt
  5. Prepare 1 cup carrots
  6. Take 3 medium red potatoes
  7. Make ready 3 1/2 cup water
  8. Get 1/2 packages Japanese curry, lots of choices (suggested Vermont spicy hot).
Instructions to make Japanese Curry Chicken:
  1. Add olive oil to large deep skillet.
  2. Chop onion and cook in oil for about 5 minutes than add in cubed chicken.
  3. Once chicken is mostly cooked art seasoning salt, carrots and potatoes. All cut into bit size pieces.
  4. Add water bring to a simmer, cover and cook for 20 minutes.
  5. Uncover, mix in curry cubes and remove from heat. In that order. let it thicken for 20 minutes stirring occasionally.
  6. Serve over rice.
