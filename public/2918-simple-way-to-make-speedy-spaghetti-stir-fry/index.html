Instructions to make Spaghetti stir fry:
  1. Boil your pasta till it's aldente, drain it and run it under cold water to stop the cooking process
  2. Lightly oil your pan, add in the onions, sausages, chicken, carrots and then the garlic
  3. Sauté for a bit and then add in the pasta, green pepper and seasoning.
  4. Toss slightly till they're well incorporated.
  5. Serve!
