The ingredients needed to make Easy Lasagna Rolls Up:
  1. Get 250 gr dry lasagna noodles - unbroken
  2. Get 2 Tbsp olive oil
  3. Make ready 500 gr ground beef
  4. Take 4 cloves garlic, minced
  5. Get 4 medium tomatoes, small diced
  6. Prepare 6 Tbsp Prego Traditional Pasta Sauce
  7. Prepare 3/4 tsp salt or to taste
  8. Get 1/4 tsp freshly ground black pepper powder or to taste
  9. Get 100 gr shreded mozzarella cheese
  10. Make ready butter for greasing the lasagna dish
  11. Prepare fresh minced parsley
Instructions to make Easy Lasagna Rolls Up:
  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium high heat. SautÃ© garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add ground beef. Cook until the beef change color.
  3. Add Prego Traditional Pasta Sauce. Mix well.
  4. Add tomatoes. Cook until the tomatoes are soft.
  5. Add salt and black pepper powder. Test the taste. Remove from the heat. Set aside.
  6. Boil water in a big pot on high heat. Add some salt and oil. Cook lasagna noodles to al dente, according to the package directions. Stir often to prevent from sticking. Remove from the heat. Drain in a colander and rinse with cool water. Gently separating any noodles that maybe sticking together. 1.
