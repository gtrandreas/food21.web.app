The ingredients needed to make Lady's Finger in Mustard Paste:
  1. Prepare 250 gm lady's finger
  2. Prepare 2 tsp mustard seed paste
  3. Get 1 tsp poppy seed paste
  4. Make ready 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  6. Get As needed salt
  7. Make ready 1 pinch nigella seeds
  8. Make ready 4 tsp mustard oil
Steps to make Lady's Finger in Mustard Paste:
  1. Wash the lady's fingers and cut them into pieces
  2. Take a pan and put 3 tsp of mustard oil in it
  3. Then, put the nigella seeds and the lady's fingers and fry it for sometimes
  4. Now, put salt, turmeric powder and red chilli powder in it and saute for a while
  5. Pour little water in the pan and let it get cooked for sometimes
  6. Now, put the mustard paste and poppy seed paste in it and again let it get cooked for a while
  7. Turn off the gas oven and put 1 tsp of mustard oil in it and it's ready
