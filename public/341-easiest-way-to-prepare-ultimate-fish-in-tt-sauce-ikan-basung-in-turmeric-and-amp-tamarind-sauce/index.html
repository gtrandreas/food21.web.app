The ingredients needed to make Fish in TT Sauce (ikan basung in turmeric & tamarind sauce):
  1. Make ready 12 small scad fish(basung)-marinated in fish sauce, coded with corn flour
  2. Prepare 6 medium okra(ladies finger)-cutted half
  3. Make ready 2 stick lemon grass-crushed
  4. Make ready 2 large onion-cutted chunk
  5. Make ready 2 large tomato-cutted chunk
  6. Make ready 12 each curry leaf
  7. Make ready 2 clove garlic-sliced
  8. Take 2 clove shallot-sliced
  9. Get 3 tbsp chili powder
  10. Prepare 3 tbsp hot & sour chili dips
  11. Make ready 2 tbsp fish sauce
  12. Take 3 tbsp turmeric powder
  13. Prepare 3 cup tamarind juice

Instructions to make Fish in TT Sauce (ikan basung in turmeric & tamarind sauce):
  1. deep fried fished, till crispy & goldern Brown, set a side
  2. stir fried, garlic, shallot, lemon grass, chili & turmeric powder, curry leaf for 2-3 mins.
  3. add in large onion & tomato, continue to stir fried for 2-3 mins.
  4. add in tamarind juice, bring to boiled, add in okra cook for 5 mins., add in fish sauce.
  5. note: water could be added in order to have more sauce
  6. added TT mixture to fish before serving
