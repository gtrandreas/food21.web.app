The ingredients needed to make Fettuccine Alfredo with sausage and veggies:
  1. Prepare Pasta
  2. Prepare -8 cheese turkey or chicken sausage
  3. Get fettuccine noodles
  4. Take mixed frozen vegetables, thawed and rinsed
  5. Prepare Alfredo sauce
  6. Take chicken stock
  7. Prepare grated parmesan cheese 1/3 cup or to taste
  8. Make ready olive oil
Steps to make Fettuccine Alfredo with sausage and veggies:
  1. cook fettuccine according to package. Save some pasta water you may need later.
  2. heat oil in dutch oven, once hot, add sausage and brown.
  3. stir in vegetables, Alfredo sauce and chicken stock, bring to a simmer
  4. add cooked pasta to simmering mixture. Taste, add salt and pepper as needed.
  5. if this looks dry add some pasta water as needed. Do not add too much.
  6. shake cheese on meal as needed.
