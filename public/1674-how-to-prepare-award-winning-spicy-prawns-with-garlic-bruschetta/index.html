The ingredients needed to make Spicy prawns with garlic bruschetta:
  1. Get Enough prawns for everyone
  2. Get Garlic
  3. Make ready Fresh chilli
  4. Prepare Olive oil
  5. Take Glug of wine
  6. Get Tin chopped tomatoes
  7. Prepare Parsley
  8. Make ready to taste Salt
  9. Get Bruschetta bread or bread of your choice
