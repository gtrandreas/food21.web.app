Steps to make Alfredo bread bowl:
  1. Melt butter, add garlic, and other seasoning (to liking)
  2. Add in cream, and Parmesan cheese. Stir for 7 minutes.
  3. Boil water in separate pot to cook pasta (15-18 minutes after boil)
  4. Prepare chicken, season both sides, grill (in oil if pleases you) 5 minutes per side (depending on thickness, I did 7 minutes per side)
  5. Cut chicken into pieces, return to skillet just to make sure they are completely cooked!
  6. Add noodles, and chicken to sauce. And mix well.
  7. Then remove top from bread, and sprinkle the bottom with cheeses, add Alfredo, sprinkle cheese on the top, and then place in oven on 350F until cheese is melted.
