The ingredients needed to make Gramma Evies Banana Bread:
  1. Get 1 C Crisco - I prefer butter flavored
  2. Prepare 4 Eggs
  3. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  4. Make ready 3 C Sugar
  5. Prepare Mix the following 3 ingredients in a bowl:
  6. Get 1 C Milk
  7. Get 2 Tbs vinegar
  8. Get And 2 tsp soda - stir & it will fiz
  9. Take 4 C Flour
  10. Make ready 6 Bananas
  11. Get 2 tsp Vanilla
  12. Take Walnuts or choc chips optional
Instructions to make Gramma Evies Banana Bread:
  1. Mix the 1st four ingredients together
  2. Alternately add milk and flour
  3. Add bananas & vanilla
  4. Add Chocolate chips or walnuts if you want
  5. Grease pans (you could sprinkle cinnamon/sugar mixture in your pans to coat outside of Bread if you want). If you're doing a batch of small loaves they should be done in about 45-50 minutes. I keep an eye on since every oven is different. Two large loaves will take 45-50 minutes also. Check on & make sure toothpick comes out clean. Let cool and remove.
  6. Enjoy!
