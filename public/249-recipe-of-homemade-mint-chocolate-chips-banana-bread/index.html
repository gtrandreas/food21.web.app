The ingredients needed to make Mint chocolate chips banana bread 🍌 🍞:
  1. Take 5 1/3 TBSP unsalted butter
  2. Prepare 2/3 Cup sugar
  3. Take 1 1/3 Cup buckwheat flour
  4. Make ready 3/4 tsp salt
  5. Get 1/2 tsp baking soda
  6. Make ready 1/4 tsp baking powder
  7. Take 2 large eggs
  8. Prepare 1/2 tsp peppermint extract
  9. Get 2-3 tsp flex seed
  10. Take 1 cup mashed, ripe bananas (2-3)
  11. Take 1/2 cup mini chocolate chips
  12. Take For glaze on the topping
  13. Prepare 2-3 tbsp honey
  14. Prepare 2-3 tbsp chocchip
  15. Get 1 banana thin slice

  2. You can glaze with honey and add some banana slices if you wish
