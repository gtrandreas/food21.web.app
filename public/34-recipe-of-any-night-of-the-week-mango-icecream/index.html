Steps to make Mango icecream:
  1. Put a steel or tin bowl on freezer f1 hour.
  2. Add mango chunks in choper make smooth paste or puree of mango
  3. Add a packet of cream in chilled bowl i put in freezer
  4. Beat the cream for 5 minute then add sugar 1 smpoon then beat.after adding every spoon of sugar beat 2 minute.add 4 or 5 tbsp sugar according to your taste
  5. Add mango puree beat for 6 to 8 minutes.put some ice cubes below the bowl to make it cool.
  6. Now add 1/2 tsp yellow food colour
  7. Beat for 3 to 4 minutes
  8. Add some mango chuks in the base before adding ice cream mixture
  9. Add ice cream mixture in mould
  10. Put in freezer for 5 to 6 hours
  11. After 5 hours serve chill.
