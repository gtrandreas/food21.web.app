Instructions to make Spaghetti Alfredo:
  1. Cook spaghetti on boiling water with 1 tbs olive oil and a pinch of salt, cook until all dente
  2. After spaghetti cooked, take a little bit of the cooking water and drain the rest of water
  3. Heat a pan with 2 tbs olive oil on medium heat
  4. Add onion cook until soft and add garlic, spinach and chili cook for 1 minute
  5. Then reduce the heat to medium low and add cooking water, full cream milk and cheese
  6. Mix all around until the cheese melted, add salt and pepper to taste
  7. Turn off the heat and add cooked spaghetti to the pan and mix well
