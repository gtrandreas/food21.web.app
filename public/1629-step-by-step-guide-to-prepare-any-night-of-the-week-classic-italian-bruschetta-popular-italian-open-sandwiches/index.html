The ingredients needed to make Classic Italian Bruschetta Popular Italian Open Sandwiches:
  1. Take as needed French Loaf (Any)- I’ve used Multigrain Baguette
  2. Get 2-3 tbsp EVOO (Extra Virgin Olive Oil)
  3. Make ready 1 tbsp Balsamic Vinegar or Apple Cedar Vinegar
  4. Make ready 2 Garlic Cloves
  5. Take as needed garlic butter
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp Oregano
  7. Take 4-5 Basil Leaves
  8. Get 1/2 tsp Black Peppercorns (Freshly Cracked)
  9. Take 2 Tomatoes (Finely Chopped)
  10. Make ready 2 Green Chillies (Finely Chopped)
  11. Make ready 1/2 tsp Paprika or Red Chilli Flakes
  12. Get To Taste Salt
  13. Prepare as needed Cheese Slices (Any)
  14. Prepare 1/2 Cup Grated Cheese (Cheddar & Mozzarella or Parmesan)
  15. Take 1/4 cup Any Processed Cheese (Cut into small Cubes)
Steps to make Classic Italian Bruschetta Popular Italian Open Sandwiches:
  1. For the Filling- In a mixing bowl : Add in all the ingredients mentioned above except the Baguette (Cut into slices) & add into it Salt & Vinegar & EVOO
  2. Give, everything a nice toss to blend & well combine with each other
  3. Take the Baguette Slices & Smear Garlic Butter to it on one side & on the other EVOO & grill that in preheated the oven at 200C for about a couple of mins on it's each sides
  4. Take out off the Oven & Place a little cheese slices at it's base & then, with it's already prepared Tomato Fillings/Toppings & finally put the cheese cubicles & a bit more shredded cheese & drizzle with a little more EVOO & put it in the preheated oven @200C, for about 12-15 mins time
  5. Our CLASSIC ITALIAN BRUSCHETTA’s now ready to be grabbed & relished with the most choicest DRINKS of yours
