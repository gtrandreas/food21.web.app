The ingredients needed to make Penne and Meatball Casserole:
  1. Get penne pasta
  2. Prepare hamburger
  3. Get garlic salt
  4. Get minced onion
  5. Make ready jars of spaghetti sauce (26oz. each)
  6. Make ready shredded mozzarella cheese
  7. Prepare shredded cheddar cheese
Instructions to make Penne and Meatball Casserole:
  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Mix hamburger, garlic salt and onion thouroughly. Form into 1 inch meatballs and place in skillet. Cook, turning frequently, until cooked through. Approx. 20-25 minutes. You can use store bought meatballs if you want.
  3. Meanwhile, cook pasta according to package directions.
  4. In a large bowl mix meatballs, pasta, sauce and 1 cup of mozzarella. Pour into lightly greased 13x9 baking dish. Cover.
  5. Bake 30 minutes or until bubbly and hot. Uncover and top with remaining cheese. Cook until cheese is melted.
