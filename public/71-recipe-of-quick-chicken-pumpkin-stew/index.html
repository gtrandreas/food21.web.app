The ingredients needed to make Chicken Pumpkin Stew:
  1. Make ready 1 tbsp oil
  2. Make ready 1 1/2 lbs skinless, boneless chicken thighs, fat trimmed and cut into 1 inch pieces
  3. Take 1 medium onion, chopped
  4. Make ready 1 red bell pepper, cut in 1 inch pieces
  5. Get 1 tsp minced garlic
  6. Make ready 1 (15 oz) can chicken broth
  7. Get 1 (15 oz) can diced tomatoes
  8. Take 1 tbsp smoked paprika
  9. Take 1/2 tsp salt
  10. Prepare 3 c peeled sugar pumpkin or butternut squash (about 1 lb), cut into 1 inch cubes
  11. Make ready 8 oz fresh green beans, cut in half
  12. Take 1 tbsp cornstarch
  13. Get 1/4 c creamy peanut butter
Instructions to make Chicken Pumpkin Stew:
  1. Heat the oil in a large saucepan over med- high heat.
  2. Add the chicken and sauté for 4 minutes or until browned.
  3. Remove to a plate and cover to stay warm.
  4. Reduce heat to medium, then add onion, bell pepper, and garlic and sauté 4 minutes until softened.
  5. Add the 1 1/2 cups broth, the tomatoes, paprika, and salt and bring to a boil.
  6. Add the chicken back in, the pumpkin, and beans.
  7. Reduce heat, cover and simmer 12 minutes or until chicken and pumpkin are tender.
  8. Meanwhile, stir remaining broth, cornstarch, and peanut butter in a bowl until smooth.
  9. Add peanut butter mixture to pot and stir until blended.
  10. Cook for 2 minutes or until thickened and serve.
