The ingredients needed to make Mandys stuffed chicken and mash potatoes:
  1. Make ready 5 large chicken breast
  2. Prepare 125 grams cheese
  3. Prepare 5 slice cooked ham (deli)
  4. Take 750 grams potatoes
  5. Take 2 tbsp butter
  6. Prepare 150 ml milk
  7. Get 1 pinch salt and pepper
  8. Take 1 packages frozen veg mix
  9. Take any gravy (your choice )
  10. Get parsley to garnish

Instructions to make Mandys stuffed chicken and mash potatoes:
  1. Preheat oven 400°F/200°C. Meanwhile peel potatoes and cut into even sized pieces, wash and put into a pot of cold water. Add a good pinch of salt and put onto cook. Once boiling reduce to a simmer until fork tender
  2. Cut a pocket hole in each chicken breast and put cheese inside it, wrap with ham and secure with a toothpick, place in oven for 20-25 minutes until chicken is cooked and cheese is melted (if oven isn't preheated add another 5-10 minutes cooking time for the chicken to be totally done)
  3. When potatoes are softened drain off water and mash, add butter, milk salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Boil veg as per direction, make the gravy as per direction
  5. Serve on warmed plates and enjoy
