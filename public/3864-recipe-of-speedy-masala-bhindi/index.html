Steps to make Masala bhindi:
  1. Chopped onions, tomatoes and gralic cloves.. cut lady fingers
  2. Heat oil in a wok add cumin seeds when it's crackly add chopped garlic and onions, saute till onion turns brown
  3. Now add ginger garlic paste saute well., now add tomatoes
  4. Mix and mashed all the tomatoes now add turmeric, red chilli, salt and coriander powder.. mix and saute till oil releases from sides
  5. Now add bhindi mix well now add lemon juice.. cover and cooked till bhindi becomes soft.. once it done off the flame
  6. Serve hot with chapati
