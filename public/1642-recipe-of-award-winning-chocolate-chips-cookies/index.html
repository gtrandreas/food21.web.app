The ingredients needed to make Chocolate chips cookies:
  1. Prepare 115 g soften butter
  2. Make ready 50 white sugar
  3. Take 120 g lite brown sugar
  4. Get 1 large egg room temperature
  5. Make ready Tsp vanilla extract
  6. Prepare 185 g all purpose flour
  7. Get 1/2 tsp baking soda
  8. Take 1/4 tsp baking powder
  9. Take 1/4 tsp salt
  10. Make ready 200 gram chocolate chips

Steps to make Chocolate chips cookies:
  1. Preheat your oven to 350 Fahrenheit or 175-180 degree celcius, then mix the butter, white sugar and sugar with the hand mixer
  2. Add the egg and vanila and continue mixing with tthe hand mixer
  3. Add the flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt and mix well with spatula
  4. Add the chocolate chips and mix well again with spatula
