The ingredients needed to make Noom friendly microwave cake:
  1. Prepare 1/2 banana
  2. Make ready 1/4 cup organic apple sauce
  3. Make ready 1/2 tsp vanilla
  4. Make ready 1 tbsp molasses
  5. Make ready 2 eggs
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp cocoa powder
  7. Make ready 1 tsp ground ginger
  8. Prepare 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  9. Prepare 1/2 tsp baking powder
  10. Get 3 tbsp whole wheat flour

Instructions to make Noom friendly microwave cake:
  1. In a tall microwave safe bowl or large mug, mash the banana.
  2. Stir in apple sauce, vanilla, molasses, and eggs.
  3. Mix in cocoa powder, ginger, cinnamon, and baking powder.
  4. Stir in flour.
  5. Microwave for 2.5 minutes.
  6. Gentle shake the bowl/mug to loosen the cake. Place a dish on top of it and flip. Garnish with fruit and berries.
