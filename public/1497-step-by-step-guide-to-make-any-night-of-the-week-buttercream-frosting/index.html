The ingredients needed to make Buttercream Frosting:
  1. Make ready 4 Cups Powdered/ Confectioners Sugar
  2. Make ready 8 tbsp Unsalted Butter, room temperature
  3. Make ready 1/4 cup Heavy Whipping Cream or Milk
  4. Get 2 tsp Vanilla Extract

Instructions to make Buttercream Frosting:
  1. Add the Powdered/ Confectioners Sugar into a bowl with the butter, then beat together.
  2. Add the Cream/ Milk and Vanilla Extract to the bowl. Use a mixer to blend together until fluffy and light.
  3. You can add more cream/ milk until the consistency is to your liking.
  4. Add to your cake/cupcakes or just eat it as is. It is that good. No judging here. :-)
  5. Enjoy!
