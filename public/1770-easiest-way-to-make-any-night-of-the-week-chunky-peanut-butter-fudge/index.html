The ingredients needed to make Chunky peanut butter fudge:

  2. Make ready 2 1/4 cups brown sugar
  3. Get 1/2 cup milk
  4. Make ready 1 cup Honey Roasted chunky peanut butter
  5. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla extract
  6. Take 3 1/2 cups powdered sugar

Instructions to make Chunky peanut butter fudge:
  1. Line a 8x8 dish with aluminum foil, and measure out the powdered sugar into a large mixing bowl.
  2. Melt butter in medium sauce pan over medium-low heat. Then add the brown sugar and milk, stirring until dissolved. Bring to a boil for 2 minutes, stirring frequently. Remove from heat, and stir in peanut butter and vanilla extract. Pour over powdered sugar in large mixing bowl. Beat until no clumps remain.
  3. Scrape into lined dish and refrigerate until firm, around 2 hours. Remove fudge with foil and cut into squares.
