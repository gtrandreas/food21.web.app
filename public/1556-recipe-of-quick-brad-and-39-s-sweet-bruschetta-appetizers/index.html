The ingredients needed to make Brad's sweet bruschetta appetizers:
  1. Take 1 loaf sweet bruchetta rolled with oats
  2. Make ready 1 pkg pork mousse with port wine
  3. Prepare Olive oil
  4. Prepare 4 Oz procuitto di parma
  5. Take Artisan cheese
  6. Get leaves Fresh mint and basil
  7. Prepare Portuguese sauvignon blanc

Steps to make Brad's sweet bruschetta appetizers:
  1. Cut bruchetta loaf on the bias. Place on a LG baking sheet. Drizzle with olive oil. Preheat broiler on high. Place on center rack. Broil 5-6 minutes.
  2. Spread mousse evenly on bruchetta. Top with small pieces of procuitto.
  3. Split the bruchetta pieces in half. On half top with the creamy earthy cheese and basil. The other half top with the sweeter cheese and mint.
  4. Serve with the wine. Clean the palate between the different flavors. Enjoy
