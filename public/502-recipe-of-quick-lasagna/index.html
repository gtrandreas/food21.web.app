Instructions to make Lasagna:
  1. Mix mozzarella cheese, cottage cheese, and eggs set aside for later
  2. Brown and chop ground beef and sausage.
  3. Add diced tomatoes, seasoning’s, and tomatoes paste to browned meat.
  4. Layer from bottom to top. Meat mix, noodles, cheese mix, meat mix, noodles, cheese mix, meat mix.
  5. Bake at 400 degrees for 55 minutes. At minute 50 sprinkle shredded cheese on top.
