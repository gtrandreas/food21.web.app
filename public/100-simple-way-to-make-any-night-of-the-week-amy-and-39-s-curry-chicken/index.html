The ingredients needed to make Amy's Curry Chicken:
  1. Get 3 lb Boneless, Skinless chicken thighs (cut into bite size pieces)
  2. Get 2 tbsp olive oil
  3. Make ready 3 medium onions (sliced)
  4. Make ready 2 medium apples (chopped)
  5. Make ready 4 cup chicken broth
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp curry powder
  7. Make ready 1/2 tbsp turmeric powder
  8. Prepare 1 tsp fresh ginger (pureed)
  9. Take 1 salt & pepper to taste
  10. Prepare 1 tbsp flour or cornstarch

Steps to make Amy's Curry Chicken:
  1. Heat olive oil in skillet over med-high heat. Once oil is hot add chicken.
  2. Brown chicken on both sides and add the onions and apples. Saute until tender.
  3. Combine broth, curry, ginger, turmeric and salt & pepper. Pour over chicken and bring to a boil.
  4. Simmer over med-low heat for 45 minutes.
  5. Add flour/cornstarch and stir well. Once the sauce thickens it is ready to serve. Goes well over plain rice with a piece of Naan.
