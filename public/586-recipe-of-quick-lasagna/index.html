The ingredients needed to make Lasagna:
  1. Prepare 1 lb ground beef
  2. Get 2-1/2 cups shredded Mozzarella cheese
  3. Prepare 1 container (15 oz) part skim ricotta cheese
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese, divided
  5. Prepare 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  6. Take 1 egg beaten
  7. Prepare 1 jar (24 oz) pasta sauce
  8. Make ready 1 cup water
  9. Make ready 12 lasagna noodles

Instructions to make Lasagna:
  1. Heat oven to 350°f
  2. Brown meat in large skillet on med-high heat.
  3. In separate bowl mix 1-1/4 cups Motz, ricotta, 1/4 cup Parmesan, parsley and egg until blended
  4. Drain meat and return to skillet. Stir in Pasta sauce and add the 1 cup of water to the jar, shake and add to meat.
  5. Spread 1 cup of meat mixture to bottom of pan
  6. Top with noodles. Add 1/3 of the ricotta cheese mixture and 1 cup meat sauce. Repeat.
  7. Bake 1 hour or until heated through, removing foil after 45 minutes. Let stand 15min before severing.
