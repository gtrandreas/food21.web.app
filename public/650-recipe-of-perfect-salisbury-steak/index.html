The ingredients needed to make Salisbury steak:
  1. Get 2 lb ground meat
  2. Take 1/4 cup bread crumbs
  3. Get 1 egg (beaten)
  4. Get 1 packet onion soup mix
  5. Make ready 1 T minced garlic
  6. Take 1 T worchestshire sauce
  7. Get 1 t salt
  8. Make ready 1 t pepper
  9. Prepare 1 t butter
  10. Prepare 2 cups beef gravy

Instructions to make Salisbury steak:
  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Mix together meat, crumbs, egg, soup mix, garlic, worchestshire, salt, and pepper.
  3. Form into 8- 10 flat patties.
  4. Heat butter in oven proof skillet. Brown patties in batches. (They'll finish in oven)
  5. Drain off most of the fat in the skillet. Put all patties in. Pour gravy over patties. (If they don't fit transfer to casserole dish)
  6. Bake 20 minutes. Serve with mashed potatoes.
