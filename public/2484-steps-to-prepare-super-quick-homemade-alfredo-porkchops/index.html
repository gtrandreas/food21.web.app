Steps to make Alfredo porkchops:
  1. Add all ingredients, except porkchops.
  2. Mix with a whisk until thoroughly blended.
  3. Add porkchops, making sure they are submerged in sauce.
  4. Set crockpot on high for 5 hours.
  5. Serve with mash potatoes.
