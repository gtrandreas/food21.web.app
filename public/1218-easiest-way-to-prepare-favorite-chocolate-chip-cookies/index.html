The ingredients needed to make Chocolate chip cookies:
  1. Take 1 stick butter
  2. Take 1/2 cup sugar
  3. Get 1/2 brown sugar
  4. Get 1 egg at room temp
  5. Prepare 1 1/4 cups flour
  6. Prepare 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  7. Get 1 teaspoon baking powder
  8. Make ready Pinch salt
  9. Get Splash vanilla
  10. Take 1 cup chocolate chips

Steps to make Chocolate chip cookies:
  1. Cream the room temperature butter
  2. Add in the sugars and beat
  3. Add in 1 egg
  4. A splash of vanilla
  5. Add all the dry ingredients together
  6. Add in the chocolate chips
  7. Scoop and roll the dough into balls and refrigerate for 30 mins
  8. After thawing, bake at 325 for 15-18mins. Enjoy!
