The ingredients needed to make White Chocolate Chip Brownies:
  1. Get For The White Chicolate Brownies
  2. Take 4 ounces (1 stick) unsalted butter
  3. Prepare 12 ounces good quality white chocolate, not chips chopped
  4. Prepare 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  5. Get 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

  11. Take 3 tablespoons butter, salted at room temperature

  13. Get 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

  15. Prepare For Decoration
  16. Get Colored chocolate transfer sheet and melted white chocolate

Steps to make White Chocolate Chip Brownies:
  1. Make White Chocolate Brownies
  2. Preheat the oven to 350. Spray a 9 by 13 inch baking pan with bakers spray. Line pan with foil with ends extending over pan for easy removing if cooled brownies
  3. In a microwave safe bowl melt butter and white chocolate until just melted, stir until smooth. Cool to room temperature
  4. In a large bowl stir theflour, sugar, vanilla and eggs until well combined
  5. Stir in the butter/ white chocolaye mixture and mini M&M's

  9. Make Glaze
  10. In a large bowl combine all glaze ingredients and beat until smooth, adding more drops of cream if it's to thick
  11. Glaze Brownies
  12. Remove cooled brownies from pan by lifting fool and placind entire browne on parcment paper. Spread glaze evenly over brownies. Refrigerate to set glaze quckly.
