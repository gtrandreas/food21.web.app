The ingredients needed to make Apple crisp pie:
  1. Make ready Crisp
  2. Prepare 1 1/2 cup oats
  3. Make ready 3/4 cup flour
  4. Get 2 stick real butter ( it tastes best)
  5. Prepare 1 cinnamon to taste
  6. Prepare 1 cup brown sugar
  7. Make ready filling
  8. Make ready 4 large apples
  9. Take 3/4 cup juice (any)
  10. Get 3 tbsp cornstarch
  11. Get 1 cinnamon to taste
  12. Take 4 tbsp brown sugar
