The ingredients needed to make Fried Lady finger:
  1. Prepare 1 kg Lady finger
  2. Make ready 3 onion
  3. Get 3 tomato
  4. Take 1/2 tsp ginger paste
  5. Take 1/2 tsp garlic paste
  6. Make ready 2 green chilli
  7. Take To taste salt
  8. Get To taste red chilli powder
  9. Get To taste turmeric powder
  10. Prepare To taste bindi masala
  11. Make ready as needed Oil for fry
  12. Prepare as needed Green coriander for decoration
Steps to make Fried Lady finger:
  1. First, cut Lady finger in a long shape.Now put Lady finger in oil and fry them.Put fried lady finger in a separate plate.
  2. Now prepare onion green chilli and tomato paste.Now take a pan and add little oil.Now put onion tomato ginger garlic paste in this oil.Now fry this mixture.Now add salt red chilli turmeric powder and lady finger masala.Cook it for 15 minutes.Now add fried lady finger in this mixture. Cook for 5 to 7 minutes. Add Green coriander for decoration
  3. Lady finger is ready to serve.
