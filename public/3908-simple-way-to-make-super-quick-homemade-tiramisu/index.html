The ingredients needed to make Tiramisu 💖:
  1. Make ready ) 6 oz boiling water
  2. Make ready ) 2 tsp instant espresso coffee
  3. Take ) 6 egg yolks
  4. Prepare ) 1 cup sugar
  5. Get ) 2 tbsp milk
  6. Get ) 1.5 cups whipping cream
  7. Make ready ) 1 tsp vanilla essence
  8. Prepare ) 8 oz mascarpone cheese
  9. Make ready ) 2 packs Italian ladyfinger cookies
  10. Prepare ) Unsweetened cocoa powder
Steps to make Tiramisu 💖:
  1. Prepare your espresso by adding 2 tsp instant espresso coffee to 6 oz boiling water
  2. Assemble a double boiler by placing a heat proof bowl over a pan of boiling water
  3. Whisk 6 egg yolks and 1 cup sugar together on the double boiler
  4. It will first look like this
  5. And then turn into a custard. Add 2 tbsp milk and keep whisking for 8-10 mins.
  6. Final result. Turn off the boiler and set this bowl aside to cool.
  7. Take 1.5 cups whipping cream
  8. And whip it up with 1 tsp vanilla essence until it acquires this texture
  9. To your egg and sugar custard, add 8 oz mascarpone cheese and fold using a rubber spatula.
  10. To that mixture, add the whipped cream and fold it up till it becomes smooth.
  11. Final result
  12. This is what Italian ladyfinger cookies look like
  13. One by one, dip each cookie in the espresso, for not more than 3 seconds (or the cookie will collapse), and place it in a dish
  14. Like this
  15. Add half of your egg-cheese-cream mixture and form a layer on top. Thickness doesn't matter. It will be divine no matter what.
  16. On top of that place another layer of cookies dipped in espresso
  17. And finally the rest of the egg-cheese-cream mixture
  18. Refrigerate for 6-8h before serving. Resist the urge to cut through it before that. Tadaaaa! its ready to serve 💖 I hope you will like my efforts to share this long recipe steo by step. Hope to be a luckies winner of Cookpad 🙈💕
