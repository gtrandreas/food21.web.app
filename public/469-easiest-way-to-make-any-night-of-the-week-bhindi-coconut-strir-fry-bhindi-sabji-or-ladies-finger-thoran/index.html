The ingredients needed to make Bhindi coconut strir fry (bhindi sabji or ladies finger thoran):
  1. Prepare 1/4 kg ladies finger
  2. Get 1 chopped onion
  3. Take 3 cloves garlic
  4. Take 2 green chilles
  5. Prepare 1-2 dry red chilli
  6. Take 1 teaspoon red chilli powder
  7. Take 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  8. Get 1/2 teaspoon mustard
  9. Get 1 teaspoon cumin
  10. Make ready leaves Curry
  11. Take As needed oil Oil
  12. Prepare to taste Salt

Steps to make Bhindi coconut strir fry (bhindi sabji or ladies finger thoran):
  1. Cut the ladies finger in to four pieces and chopped finely into small pieces.
  2. Chopped the onion finely
  3. Heat in a oil splutter the mustard, add dry red chilli and few curry leaves
  4. Then add the chopped onions and saute it
  5. Once it became brown in colour, add green chillies and mixed it. Then add the chopped ladies finger and saute it, In this time you can add little salt.
  6. Cover and cook till bhindi get cooked, don't add water.
  7. Then grind the grated coconut, cumin, garlic, turmeric powder, red chilli powder. Just slowly crushed once in blender.
  8. Open the lid and pour the mixture of coconut and saute it well, again cover the lid 2-3mins.
  9. Remove the lid saute it well, now the ladies finger and coconut mixed together, also it looks like very crispy and dried form.
  10. Stir fry is ready to serve
