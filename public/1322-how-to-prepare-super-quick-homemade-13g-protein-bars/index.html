  3. Prepare 1 cup Whole wheat flour

  5. Get 1 cup Sunflower seeds
  6. Make ready 1/3 cup Flax seeds
  7. Take 2 each Whey powder scoop

Instructions to make 13g Protein Bars:
  1. Mix dry ingredients
  2. Add peanut butter and milk last
  3. Mix well
  4. For Pastry like bars, cook at 350 for 10 min. Final product is awesome with peanut/almond butter!
  5. For chewy bars, place in pan and refrigerate over night. They will have the texture if cliff bars and be slightly more flavorful than the baked version.
