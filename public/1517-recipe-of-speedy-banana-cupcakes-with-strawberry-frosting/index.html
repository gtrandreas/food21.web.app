The ingredients needed to make Banana Cupcakes with Strawberry Frosting:
  1. Prepare CUPCAKE
  2. Take 2 1/2 cup flour
  3. Take 1 tbsp baking soda
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup butter
  5. Prepare 1 cup sugar
  6. Make ready 3/4 cup light brown sugar
  7. Get 2 eggs
  8. Take 4 ripe bananas, mashed
  9. Prepare 2/3 cup sour cream
  10. Prepare FROSTING
  11. Get 1 (3.4oz.) vanilla instant pudding
  12. Make ready 1/4 cup milk
  13. Take 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  14. Prepare 1 (8oz.) tub of cool whip
  15. Prepare 1/2 cup strawberries, chopped small
  16. Take 1 tbsp each of sugar and water
  17. Prepare 6 drops if red food coloring
Steps to make Banana Cupcakes with Strawberry Frosting:
  1. The night before I want to make this I cut up the strawberries, add 1 tablespoon of sugar and a tablespoon of water. Mix and put in the refrigerator. The next day smash them up with a fork. If you don't want to do this step, you could use strawberry ham instead.
  2. CUPCAKE:
  3. Preheat oven to 350. Place 24 cupcake liners in cupcake pans
  4. In small bowl combine flour, baking soda, set aside.
  5. In large bowl , cream butter, sugar and brown sugar until light and fluffy. Beat eggs in one at a time.
  6. Mix in bananas
  7. Add flour mixture alternating with the sour cream
  8. Fill each cup 3/4 of the way full
  9. Bake for 25 to 30 mins
  10. FROSTING:
  11. In large bowl mix pudding, milk, and powdered sugar until combined.
  12. Fold in whip topping. Add strawberry mixture or jam and 6 drops red food coloring. Fold everything until combined. Place in refrigerator until ready to use
  13. An easy way to frost them is to put the frosting in a zip lock bag, cut of the end and swirl onto the cupcake. It is much faster too!
