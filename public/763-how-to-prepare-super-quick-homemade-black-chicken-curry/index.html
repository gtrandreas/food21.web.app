The ingredients needed to make Black chicken curry:
  1. Prepare 500 g chicken
  2. Make ready 2 tbsp chilli powder
  3. Make ready 1 tsp turmeric powder
  4. Prepare 2 tbsp black masala powder(kalu kudu)
  5. Make ready Salt for the taste

  7. Get 5 pcs garlic

  11. Prepare 1/2 pcs red onion
  12. Make ready 3 pcs green chilli
  13. Prepare leaves Curry
  14. Prepare Oil

Instructions to make Black chicken curry:
  1. First wash your chicken nicly and cut it in to cubes
  2. Drain the water properly
  3. Add chilli powder and turmeric powder, garcinia(goraka) in to the drained chicken and mix it properly
  4. Now add salt and keep that marinated chicken for a 10 minutes
  5. Take your crush machine (podi wangediya) to crush the other ingredients,you can even chopped it.
  6. Add ginger,garlic, cardamom, cineman stick, in to the pastle and crush it properly
  7. Now take a pot or cooking pan and swich on the gas cooker
  8. Add some oil in to the pan
  9. When the oil is getting hot add your black curry powder(kalu kudu) in to the pan
  10. After sometime add cut red onions, curry leaves,green chilli, in to that
  11. And now add crushed paste in to that
  12. After some time add marinated chicken in to that and add one cup of water close for a while.
