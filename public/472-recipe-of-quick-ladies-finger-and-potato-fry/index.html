The ingredients needed to make Ladies finger and potato fry:
  1. Take 1 potato
  2. Make ready 150 g ladies finger
  3. Prepare to taste Salt and sugar
  4. Take As needed oil
  5. Get 1 table spoon turmeric powder
  6. Get 2 green chillies
  7. Make ready 2-4 curry leaves
