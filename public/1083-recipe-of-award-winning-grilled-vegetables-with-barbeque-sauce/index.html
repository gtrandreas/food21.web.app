Steps to make Grilled vegetables with barbeque sauce:
  1. Sare vegetable Ko Apne pasand ke anusar cut kar lege.
  2. Ab unhe 5 minutes ke liye Garma panni me salt ke sath ubal lege(Blanch).
  3. Ab ek bade bowl me curd, ginger garlic paste,sare sauce, pepper,salt,food colour,3 spoon oil dal kar acche se batter Bana lege.
  4. Ab is batter me blanch ki Hui vegetable Ko dal kar carefully hoke sare masala acche se coat kar dege.
  5. Aur isse cover karke 3-4 hours ke liye fridge me rakh dege.
  6. Ab 4 hours ke baad hm marinate Kiya hua vegetable Ko Nikal lege.
  7. Isme Thora sa dry methi leaf uper se dal dege.aur room temperature pe Anne ke baad ise grill kar lege.
  8. Grill pan me oil dalege ab ek ek vegetable Ko uspe acche se fella dege
  9. Dono side se acche trah se low flame pe grill kar lege.
  10. Ab hamare grilled vegetables ready ho Gaya
