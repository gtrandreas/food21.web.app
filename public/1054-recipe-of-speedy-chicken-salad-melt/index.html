The ingredients needed to make Chicken Salad Melt:
  1. Get 1 pound cooked chicken 1/4 - 1/2 inch dice
  2. Prepare 1 stalk celery – washed and fine dice
  3. Make ready 1/4 of onion – fine dice
  4. Get 1/4 of red bell pepper – fine dice
  5. Get 2 tablespoons parsley – no stems and finely chopped
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  7. Make ready 1/2 cup ranch salad dressing
  8. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon salt
  9. Get 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  10. Make ready 2 slices bread- per sandwich
  11. Prepare 2 slices Pepper Jack cheese- per sandwich
Instructions to make Chicken Salad Melt:
  1. Dice chicken ( I like to use the Rotisserie Chicken or left over baked.
  2. Mix all of the salad ingredients together and let sit.
  3. Place a slice of cheese on each side of the bread add chicken salad in between.
  4. Spray with pan spray or use 1 teaspoon of melted butter for each side of the sandwich on a griddle pan over a moderate heat and cook until GBD about 3-4 minutes each side.
  5. Cheese should be melty, melty
