The ingredients needed to make Chicken salad:
  1. Prepare 1 large bunch of lettuce
  2. Make ready 1 small cabbage
  3. Make ready 4 ripe tomatoes
  4. Get 1 medium size cucumber
  5. Get 2 boiled eggs
  6. Prepare Small onion
  7. Prepare 2 big carrots
  8. Prepare Leftover chicken kebab (you can use any chicken meat available)
  9. Take Dressing
  10. Get Half cup of mayonaise
  11. Get 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  12. Take Half spoon of condensed milk

Instructions to make Chicken salad:
  1. Wash all your vegetables with vinegar and cut into desired shapes,I like to cut mine into small cubes
  2. Wash your carrots,peel and grate it,boil your eggs and cut.
  3. For the kebab:cut chicken breast into cubes,marinate with seasoning and spices and grill(I have a complete recipe in my previous posts) shred the kebab into small cubes
  4. Place the lettuce on a clean plate and use it as a base to arrange all the vegetables according to your style
  5. Whisk together the mayonnaise,lemon juice,condensed milk and a pinch of salt
  6. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and serve
