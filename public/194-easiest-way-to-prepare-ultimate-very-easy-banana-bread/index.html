The ingredients needed to make Very easy banana bread:
  1. Prepare 250 grams Plain flour
  2. Make ready 1 tsp Bicarbonate of soda
  3. Make ready 1 pinch Salt
  4. Make ready 115 grams Fine Brown sugar
  5. Prepare 115 grams butter
  6. Get 2 medium Eggs beaten
  7. Get 500 grams Over ripe banana's

Easy to make, no need for a mixer! Ripe bananas, butter, sugar, egg, vanilla, baking soda, and flour. Thousands of people make it every day. Banana bread falls into the baking category called "Quick Breads" meaning that it is leavened with chemical leavening and can be baked very quickly.
