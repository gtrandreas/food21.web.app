The ingredients needed to make Rase Dessert:
  1. Take Milk - 1 lt
  2. Take 2 tsp Lemon juice -
  3. Take 2 tsp Sugar -
  4. Get 2 tsp Fresh malai -
  5. Make ready drops Rose essence - Few
  6. Make ready 2 tbsp Rose syrup
Instructions to make Rase Dessert:
  1. First boil the milk in a heavy bottom vessel and add lemon juice for torn the milk.
  2. Now strain half water.
  3. Began to cook it. Mix sugar.
  4. Cook it until all water get dry.
  5. When all water dry transfer it in a kadhai and add malai
  6. Mix well and stir it regularly.
  7. Now add rose syrup and rose essence.
  8. Mix well and transfer in the serving bowl. Garnish with Chopped pista and dry fruits
  9. Your yummy dessert is ready to eat. Eat hot or cold…..it's your choice.
