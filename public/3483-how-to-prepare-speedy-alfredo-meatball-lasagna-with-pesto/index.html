Instructions to make Alfredo Meatball Lasagna with Pesto:
  1. Preheat oven 350 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Cook the meatballs frying in oil, and cook Alfredo sauce.
  3. Move meatballs to a paper towel
  4. Cook the second batch
  5. Layer the bottom of the pan with half the meatballs sprinkle with smoked paprika.
  6. Mix the parmesan cheese with the cottage cheese.
  7. Add half the Alfredo sauce
  8. Add a layer of pasta, and then cottage cheese.
  9. Now add the Pesto to the top of cottage cheese
  10. Next add a layer pasta
  11. Add rest of meatballs I cut these meatballs in half for better coverage
  12. Add the rest of Alfredo sauce, and sprinkle with smoked paprika.
  13. Add the shredded cheese
  14. Bake in oven for 60 minutes
  15. Let rest 15 minutes
  16. Serve, I hope you enjoy!
