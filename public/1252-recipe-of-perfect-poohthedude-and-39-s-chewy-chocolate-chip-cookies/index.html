The ingredients needed to make PoohTheDude's Chewy Chocolate Chip Cookies:
  1. Take 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  2. Prepare 1 tsp baking soda
  3. Make ready 1 tsp salt
  4. Make ready 1 cup butter, softened
  5. Prepare 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  6. Prepare 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
  7. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla extract
  8. Get 2 eggs
  9. Prepare 2 cups (12 oz)bag of semi-sweet chocolate chips
Instructions to make PoohTheDude's Chewy Chocolate Chip Cookies:
  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. Combine flour, baking soda and salt in small bowl.
  3. In separate large bowl, beat butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar and vanilla extract.
  4. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition; gradually beat in flour mixture.
  5. Stir in chocolate chips.
  6. Drop by rounded tablespoon onto ungreased baking sheets.
  7. Bake for 9 to 11 minutes or until golden brown. I let them sit on the baking sheet for the 11 minutes I'm baking my next set of cookies.
  8. Get some nice milk and eat em up.
