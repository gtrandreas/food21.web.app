The ingredients needed to make Chocolate cake!:
  1. Get 1 cup milk
  2. Take 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  3. Get 1 and half cup of sugar
  4. Take Vanilla extract
  5. Take 1 and half cup of all purpose flour
  6. Prepare 1 teaspoon baking powder
  7. Take 1/4 of teaspoon of baking soda
  8. Make ready 3 tablespoon coco powder
  9. Take Chocolate ganache
  10. Get Dark chocolate
  11. Get Whipping cream
  12. Prepare 1 teaspoon butter

Instructions to make Chocolate cake!:
  1. In a bowl, add milk, oil, vanilla extract and sugar… mix these ingredients well and keep aside for 5 minutes.
  2. Then add dry ingredients, all purpose flour, baking powder, baking soda, coco powder. Mix and make a smooth batter, then bake the cake for 30 mins on 180 degree C.
  3. To make chocolate ganache, boil whipping cream and add it in a bowl of grated dark chocolate. Keep aside for 1/2 minutes, then mix chocolate n whipping cream together n then add butter to it. Let it cool…
  4. Once ur cake and ganache is cooled, pour the ganache on cake, to the side add any grated chocolate (I grated KitKat chocolate, it gave an amazing flavor), keep to bars of the chocolate on cake.
