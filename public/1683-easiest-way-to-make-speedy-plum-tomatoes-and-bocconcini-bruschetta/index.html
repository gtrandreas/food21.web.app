The ingredients needed to make Plum tomatoes and Bocconcini Bruschetta:
  1. Get 2 Plum tomatoes blanched
  2. Get 120 gms Bocconcini cheese.
  3. Get 10 gms Basil leaves.
  4. Prepare to taste Salt
  5. Take 4 Garlic
  6. Get 10 ml Balsamic reduction
  7. Get 10 ml Basil pesto
  8. Make ready to taste Pepper
Instructions to make Plum tomatoes and Bocconcini Bruschetta:
  1. Toast all the four garlic croutons
  2. Slice all the tomatoes and cheese separately
  3. Now arrange alternate layers of cheese and tomato on the croutons
  4. Arrange basil leaves as shown
  5. Season with salt and pepper
  6. Drizzle with balsamic dressing
  7. Smear basil pesto as shown
