The ingredients needed to make Apple pie:
  1. Take mass
  2. Prepare 300 grams all-purpose flour
  3. Prepare 180 grams butter
  4. Take 1/2 tsp salt
  5. Make ready 80 ml very cold water
  6. Make ready stuffing
  7. Prepare 6 granny smith apples
  8. Prepare 1 egg
  9. Get 100 grams brown sugar
  10. Prepare 100 grams granulated sugar
  11. Prepare 45 grams all-purpose flour
  12. Take 1 lemon
  13. Make ready 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  14. Get 3 tbsp butter
  15. Make ready 1/2 tsp (optional) pink pepper

Steps to make Apple pie:
  1. Put the salt and the flour in a bowl. Add the butter cut in diced and the water.
  2. Knead it with the fingertips and make 2 balls about the same size and reserve 30 minutes in the fridge.
  3. In a separate bowl mix the flour, the 2 kinds of sugar, the cinnamon, the pepper (if used), 2 tablespoons of fresh lemon juice and the egg. Beat until you have a fluffy cream. Preheat oven to 220Â°C.
  4. Peel the apples, remove the seeds and cut in even pieces. Add to the sugar cream and mix.
  5. Roll 1 of the balls to cover the mold. Rub some butter on said bowl and then extend the mass on it.
  6. Stuff the insides with apples and the sugary cream.
  7. Roll the other ball and cover the stuffing. You may paint it with egg if desired. Bake for st least 1 hour.
