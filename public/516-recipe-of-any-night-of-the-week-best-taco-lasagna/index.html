The ingredients needed to make Best taco lasagna:
  1. Prepare soft 6 inch tortillas,
  2. Make ready ground beef or turkey or even ground chicken,
  3. Take season of your choice (taco seasoning or season salt n pepper),
  4. Prepare tomatoes, red onion, scallions, cilantro,
  5. Take heavy cream,
  6. Take mild cheddar Colby jack and Monterey Jack cheese, butter, Cajun season (for cheese sauce) and sour cream to top

Instructions to make Best taco lasagna:
  1. Brown meat and drain grease, season meat to taste.to make cheese sauce bring heavy cream and butter to a boil and slowly add cheese until thickens. Add Cajun season to spice. With red onion green pepper tomatoes and cilantro dice up to make pico and season to taste.
  2. In aluminum pan layer tortilla, meat, cheese, pico, and continue for 3 4 layers on the top finish with the last of the meat pico cheese and bake in oven for 20 mins on 400 once finish let cool down and top with sour cream and diced scallions
  3. Notes- I used one lbs of hamburger meat, and I added about one cup of salsa because I didn’t have the other ingredients. I made just the taco meat and added the salsa.
