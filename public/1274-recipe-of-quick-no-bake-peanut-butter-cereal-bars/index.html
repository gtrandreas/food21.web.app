Steps to make No bake peanut butter cereal bars:
  1. In large bowl, pour cereal and set aside
  2. In sauce pan over medium low heat, dissolve sugar in the corn syrup
  3. Add peanut butter and vanilla extract and stir until warm and creamy but not bubbly
  4. Pour over cereal and stir until all cereal is covered
  5. Press into 8 x 5 pan and allow to cool before cutting into bars
