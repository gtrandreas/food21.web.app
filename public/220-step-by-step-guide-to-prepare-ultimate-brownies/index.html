The ingredients needed to make Brownies:
  1. Prepare 3/4 cup flour
  2. Get 1 cup granulated sugar or brown sugar
  3. Make ready 1/4 tsp salt
  4. Take 1/3 cup Cadbury unsweetened cocoa powder
  5. Take 1/2 cup margarine, softened
  6. Prepare 3 tbsp water
  7. Take 1 tsp vanilla extract
  8. Get 2 eggs
  9. Prepare 1/2 cup chopped nuts of choice

Instructions to make Brownies:
  1. In a large bowl combine flour sugar, salt and cocoa powder
  2. Add margarine, water, vanilla extract, eggs and beat for 2 minutes at medium speed
  3. Add nuts and pour in an 8" greased square pan.
  4. Bake at 350.° F for 25 to 30 mins
  5. Cool In pan and cut in squares
