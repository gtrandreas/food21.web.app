The ingredients needed to make Roasted Butternut Salad:
  1. Make ready medium butternut halved and seeded
  2. Get heaped Tblsp garlic butter
  3. Take Cumin seeds
  4. Prepare red Chilli flakes
  5. Prepare vegetable spice
  6. Make ready crushed black pepper
  7. Get cooked beetroot diced (I used the sliced bottled kind)
  8. Take Danish Feta
  9. Make ready Rocket
  10. Get sliced almonds toasted
  11. Prepare slides coconut toasted
  12. Make ready olive oil
  13. Get Salt and Pepper
  14. Prepare Red Chilli flakes
  15. Take spring onions
  16. Make ready torn basil leaves
Instructions to make Roasted Butternut Salad:
  1. Season Butternut with garlic butter, Cumin, red Chilli flakes, vegetable spice and crushed black pepper. Roast in oven at 180°C until tender.
  2. Cool and cut into bite sized cubes.
  3. Place rocket in bottom of serving dish followed by cubed butternut, beetroot and feta.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt, freshly ground black pepper and some red chilli flakes.
  5. Top with toasted almonds, coconut, torn basil leaves and chopped spring onions.
  6. Serve and enjoy
