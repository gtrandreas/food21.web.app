The ingredients needed to make Chicken curry:
  1. Make ready 1 tbsp olive oil, extra virgin
  2. Take 1 onion, chopped
  3. Take 1 bay leaf
  4. Prepare 2 clove garlic
  5. Take 1 can tomatoes, peeled
  6. Take 2 tbsp curry powder (hot or mild)
  7. Get 600 grams peanuts
  8. Prepare 2 liter water
  9. Take 1 a pinch of salt
  10. Get 1 whole chicken
  11. Make ready 1 can coconut milk
  12. Get 1 lemon, juiced

Instructions to make Chicken curry:
  1. Heat olive oil in a large saucepan. Stir in the onions, garlic crushed and bay leaf and saut√© the onion until it is lightly browned.
  2. Mix tomatoes, curry powder and salt and continue cooking for 5 minutes.
  3. In the meantime, place the peanuts in a food processor and grind to a flour. Mix the peanut flour with the 2 liter water
  4. Turn into the saucepan and boil, starring occasionally. As soon as it boils, add to your chicken pieces and simmer. Cover and cook gently for approximately 40 minutes or until tender.
  5. Mix in the lemon juice just before serving.
  6. Serve hot with porridge or dumpling ( check my recipes for dumpling recipe). Enjoy.
