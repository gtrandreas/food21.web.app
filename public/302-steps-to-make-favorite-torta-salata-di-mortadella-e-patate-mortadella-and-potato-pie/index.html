The ingredients needed to make Torta salata di mortadella e patate mortadella and potato pie:
  1. Make ready Round of puff pastry
  2. Prepare Packet mortadella
  3. Get 2 medium size potatoes
  4. Take 2-3 tablespoons Parmesan cheese
  5. Get 1 egg
  6. Make ready Black pepper optional

Stendete la pasta brisè in una teglia rivestita con la carta forno e mettete sul fondo i dadini di mortadella. Al posto della mortadella potete usare del prosciutto cotto, o dei formaggi a scelta. Lessate le fette di patate in acqua salata per alcuni minuti. Ungete con dell'olio evo una pirofila.

Instructions to make Torta salata di mortadella e patate mortadella and potato pie:
  1. Peel, chop into cubes and boil potatoes until just cooked. Leave to cool a bit. Preheat oven to 180. Chop up the mortadella and add to potatoes
  2. Add Parmesan, pepper and egg, save a tiny bit of egg for glazing. Mix up well, trying not to break up the potatoes
  3. Roll out pastry into small round baking tray. Prick with a fork. Add mixture inside. Spread evenly. Fold edges down as shown. Glaze with the bit of egg
  4. Bake for about 30 mins. Leave to cool on a wire rack before serving 😀
