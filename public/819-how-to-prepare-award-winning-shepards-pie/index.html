The ingredients needed to make Shepards Pie:
  1. Take 3 cup mash potatoes
  2. Take 2 tbsp canola oil
  3. Take 1 cup chopped onion
  4. Take 2 carrots diced small
  5. Make ready 2 garlic cloves minced
  6. Get 1 1/2 lb ground meat
  7. Take 1 tsp salt
  8. Take 1/2 tsp black pepper
  9. Take 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  10. Get 2 tsp tomato paste
  11. Take 1 cup chicken broth
  12. Prepare 1 tsp worcestershire sauce
  13. Make ready 1/2 tsp thyme
  14. Get 1/2 cup frozen corn
  15. Prepare 1/2 cup frozen peas
  16. Prepare 1/2 cup cheddar cheese
  17. Make ready pinch paprika

Steps to make Shepards Pie:
  1. You can make your own mash potatoes or instant are fine, I use whatever I have time for.
  2. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  3. Place canola oil in 12 inch saute pan set over medium high heat. Add onion and carrots and saute for 3-4 minutes. Add garlic and stir.
  4. Add the meat, salt and pepper and cook until browned about 3 minutes. Then drain the meat if needed.
  5. Sprinkle the meat with flour toss to coat and cook another minute. Then add the tomato paste, chicken broth, Worcestershire, thyme, and stir.
  6. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, cover and simmer for 10 minutes until sauce is thickened.
  7. Add corn and peas to meat mixture and spread evenly into a 11 by 7 baking dish. Top with mash potatoes, starting around the edges to create a seal to prevent bubbling over. Sprinkle with cheese and paprika. Place on cookie sheet in middle of oven for 25 minutes. Remove and let cool for 15 minutes before serving
