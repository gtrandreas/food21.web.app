The ingredients needed to make Yogurt, Granola, Fruit & Fudge Christmas Delight:
  1. Prepare 2 (11 oz) bags Honey Bunches of Oats
  2. Make ready 1 (32 oz) your favorite yogurt (I used Dannon vanilla)
  3. Get 1 (16 oz) fresh strawberries
  4. Take 1 (6 oz) fresh blueberries
  5. Prepare 1 (6 oz) fresh blackberries
  6. Make ready 1 tsp sugar

Steps to make Yogurt, Granola, Fruit & Fudge Christmas Delight:
  1. Slice strawberries and in a medium bowl mix all the fruit with a tsp of sugar and set aside.
  2. Pour a layer of granola, next yogurt, then fudge. Next put a layer of fruit.
  3. Pour a layer of granola then yogurt and next fruit. Drizzle fudge on top. Place in refrigerator till ready to serve. Enjoy!
