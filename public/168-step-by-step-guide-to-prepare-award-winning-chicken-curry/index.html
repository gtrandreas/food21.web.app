The ingredients needed to make Chicken Curry:
  1. Make ready 6 pcs chicken drumsticks (or any part available)
  2. Make ready Diced potatoes, carrots, bell pepper
  3. Prepare 2 cups coconut milk
  4. Get 1 cup water
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp curry powder
  6. Prepare Minced garlic, onion & ginger
  7. Prepare 2 pcs bay leaves
  8. Prepare 2 tbsp cooking oil
  9. Get 1 tsp cayenne pepper (optional)
  10. Make ready 1 tbsp fish sauce
  11. Take 1 tbsp butter
  12. Get Salt & pepper
  13. Get (You can adjust ingredients according to your liking)
Steps to make Chicken Curry:
  1. Set a pot over medium heat. Saute garlic, onions and ginger for about 1 minute. Then add the chicken, cook it for about 5 mins.
  2. Add the curry powder, bay leaves, fish sauce, water and coconut milk. Mix well. Cook for 1 hour. Add a little water if it gets too dry.
  3. Add the potatoes, carrots and bell pepper. Mix well, cook for another 5 mins.
  4. Add the butter, salt & pepper to taste plus the cayenne pepper (optional). Simmer for 5 mins.
