The ingredients needed to make Meat and Red Curry:
  1. Get .5 lb chicken
  2. Make ready .5 lb beef
  3. Prepare .5 lb shrimp
  4. Make ready 1 cup fresh broccoli cuts
  5. Make ready 4 green onions thinly chopped
  6. Make ready 3 cloves fresh minced garlic
  7. Get 1 tbs s&b oriental curry powder
  8. Take 2 tsp Thai kitchen red curry paste
  9. Get 2 tbs fresh chopped cilantro
  10. Prepare Tsp ginger powder
  11. Take Dashes Salt and pepper
  12. Get to taste Thai red chile oil
  13. Prepare 3/4 cup water
  14. Take Tbs olive oil
Steps to make Meat and Red Curry:
  1. Thinly slice chicken and beef.
  2. Heat oil on a large skillet or wok.
  3. Add garlic and the lower, whiter ends of the green onion (save top of onion for serving) stir for about 30 seconds.
  4. Add meat and seasonings and cook until meat is done.
  5. Add fresh broccoli and continue to simmer in curry until tender.
  6. Serve on steamed jasmine rice and top with fresh cilantro and green onion.
  7. Optional: Serve with fresh lime wedges. For extra spice, add fresh jalapeno slices to the curry or as a garnish.
