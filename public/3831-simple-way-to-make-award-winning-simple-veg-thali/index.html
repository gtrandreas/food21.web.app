Steps to make Simple Veg Thali:
  1. For Rice - wash, drain and cook rice with water, then discard the water.
  2. For Dal - wash and cook arhar dal with salt, turmeric, oil and water for 3 whistle in cooker.
  3. For Masala Bhindi - see the link. https://cookpad.com/in/recipes/12351500-masala-bhindi?invite_token=KWnvz4rxPc6X7dxUyXuguRVV&amp;shared_at=1590861431
  4. For Aloo bhujiya - Peel and cut potato in long, thin stripes.
  5. Fry in oil with adding turmeric, chilli, garlic paste and salt.
  6. For Raw Mango Chutney - peel and cut mango, then grind in mixer with garlic cloves and dry red chilli.
  7. Add salt and mustard oil.
  8. Enjoy Dal, Rice, masala bhindi, lahsuni aloo bhujiya, raw mango chutney and fryums as lunch platter.
