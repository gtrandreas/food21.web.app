Steps to make mmm mash potatoes:
  1. fill pot with water and bring to boil
  2. peel potatoes and cut them into inch pieces
  3. once water is boiling add potatoes. and put a little salt and pepper into the water.
  4. let it boil for 30 min. or until it tender. and turn burner to low.
  5. strain the potatoes. and add all over ingrediants to the pot and place it on stove top.
  6. add strained potatoes back to the pot and start to mash them. once butter is melted turn burner off.
  7. season to taste and serve.
