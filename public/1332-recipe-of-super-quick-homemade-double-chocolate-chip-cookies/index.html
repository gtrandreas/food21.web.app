The ingredients needed to make Double Chocolate Chip Cookies:
  1. Prepare 3/4 cup cocoa
  2. Prepare 2 cup all-purpose flour
  3. Get 1/2 cup white sugar
  4. Prepare 1 cup brown sugar
  5. Prepare 1 cup unsalted butter
  6. Prepare 2 eggs
  7. Take 1 tsp baking soda
  8. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla extract
  9. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  10. Make ready 1 cup chocolate chips
Steps to make Double Chocolate Chip Cookies:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Mix all the dry ingredients. Don't forget to sift them.
  3. On a separate bowl, mix the melted butter, eggs, and vanilla extract. Combine with the dry ingredients.
  4. Fold until mixture is blended well. Add in the chocolate chips and fold again.
  5. Grease your pans and put in about 1/2 to 1tsp of batter.
  6. Bake for 5-10mins or until the edges of the cookies turn slightly brown.
  7. Allow to cook before serving.
