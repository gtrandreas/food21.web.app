The ingredients needed to make Double Chocolate Brownies:
  1. Make ready 2 stick Salted Butter (room temperature)
  2. Get 1 cup Granulated Sugar
  3. Get 4 Eggs
  4. Get 1 tbsp Vanilla Extract
  5. Prepare 8 oz Bittersweet Chocolate Chips (melted)
  6. Get 4 1/2 tbsp Cocoa Powder
  7. Make ready 1 cup All-Purpose Flour

Steps to make Double Chocolate Brownies:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Cream together butter and sugar.
  3. Slowly mix in eggs, vanilla, melted chocolate, and cocoa powder until fully incorporated.
  4. Slowly mix in flour 1/4 cup at a time.
  5. Pour batter into greased baking dish. Bake at 350°F for 35-40 minutes.
  6. Cut into squares and enjoy!
