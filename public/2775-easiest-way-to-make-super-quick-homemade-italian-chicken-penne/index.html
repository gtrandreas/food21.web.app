Steps to make italian chicken penne:
  1. fry bacon. drain on papertowel.
  2. saute chicken pieces in bacon grease until cooked through and slightly browned
  3. stir in flour and italian seasoning until chicken pieces are coated.
  4. stir in milk and cook until thickened and bubbly.
  5. stir in cheese until creamy and melted.
  6. crumble bacon. mix some in and sprinkle some on top
  7. serve over pasta.
