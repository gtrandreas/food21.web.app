The ingredients needed to make Mash potato cakes:
  1. Prepare 4 medium sized potatoes
  2. Prepare 75 ml milk
  3. Take 3 tablespoons butter
  4. Prepare To taste salt

Instructions to make Mash potato cakes:
  1. Pre heat an oven to 220 degrees Celsius
  2. Peel potatoes, dice and boil until cooked
  3. Mash the potato add butter, milk and salt to taste
  4. Put into a piping bag or a zip lock packet and cut the end so you can squeeze some out
  5. Grease an oven proof pan and pipe in whatever shape onto the pan
  6. Add into oven for 40-55 minutes minutes until the edges and top start to brown. Remove from oven and serve
