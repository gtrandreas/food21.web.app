Steps to make Primavera Penne Pasta Salad:
  1. Cook pasta according to package directions. Meanwhile in a large serving bowl, combine tomatoes, green pepper and onions, and grapes.
  2. Drain pasta and rinse in cold water; add to vegetables and grapes. Combine in bacon bits and Queso fresco.
  3. Then add seasoning and salad dressing. Top with more bacon and Queso fresco.
  4. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours. Yields 8-10 servings.
