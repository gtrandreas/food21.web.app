The ingredients needed to make Stuff lady's fingers masala:
  1. Take 100 grams small size lady fingers
  2. Get 1/4 cup roasted besan
  3. Make ready 2 tablespoons cumin_coriender powder
  4. Prepare 1 teaspoon salt
  5. Prepare 1 teaspoon red chilli powder
  6. Take 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  7. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon garam masala
  8. Get 3 tablespoons oil

Steps to make Stuff lady's fingers masala:
  1. In a mixing bowl add besan, red chilli powder, turmeric powder, cumin_coriender powder, garam masala, salt, and 2 tablespoons oil, mix well
  2. Give straight cut into all lady fingers, stuff the masala in all
  3. In a pan add all stuffed lady fingers and pore 1 tablespoons oil on it cook on low flame for 5 min. Then turn all lady fingers and cook for 5 min.
