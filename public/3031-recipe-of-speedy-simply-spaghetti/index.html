The ingredients needed to make Simply Spaghetti!:
  1. Make ready Sauce
  2. Make ready onion, chopped
  3. Get garlic clove, minced
  4. Prepare ground beef, lean
  5. Get montreal steak seasoning
  6. Make ready garlic powder
  7. Take salt & pepper
  8. Get pasta sauce
  9. Get Noodles
  10. Take olive oil, extra virgin
  11. Take salt
  12. Make ready spaghetti noodles
Steps to make Simply Spaghetti!:
  1. In a medium skillet add: ground beef, onion, garlic clove, Montreal Seasoning, garlic powder, salt & pepper.
  2. Heat on medium high until beef is browned and thoroughly cooked.
  3. Meanwhile, in a large pot, fill half way with water and add the olive oil and dash of salt. Bring to a boil.
  4. Once beef is cooked, drain grease and put back in skillet.
  5. Mix in the sauce and simmer on low for around 8 minutes.
  6. Once water is boiling, add the spaghetti noodles and cook al dente.
  7. Drain water from the noodles.
  8. Remove the sauce and noodles from heat.
  9. Plate noodles and then top with sauce. If you please, garnish with Parmesan and parsley flakes. Enjoy!
