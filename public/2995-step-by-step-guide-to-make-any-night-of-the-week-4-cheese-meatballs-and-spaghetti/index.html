The ingredients needed to make 4 Cheese Meatballs And Spaghetti:
  1. Make ready Ground beef
  2. Make ready Spaghetti noodles
  3. Make ready 4 cheese tomato sauce
  4. Prepare Colby jack shredded cheese
  5. Take Mozzarella shredded cheese
  6. Take Italian style shredded cheese
  7. Prepare Parmesan shredded cheese
  8. Get Eggs
  9. Get Green onions
  10. Make ready Parsley flakes
  11. Take Oil
Steps to make 4 Cheese Meatballs And Spaghetti:
  1. Boil the water once boiled add a pinch of salt and box of noodles
  2. While noodles cook add 2 eggs with ground beef and parsley mash all together well
  3. Add all bags of cheese to ground beef mash together form shape of meat balls
  4. Put thin layer of oil in pan let it Heat
  5. Add meat balls to pan constantly rotate them so they don't flatten
  6. When cook drain oil from pan
  7. Noodles should be for drain then put back in pan
  8. Add can of 4 cheese tomato sauce
  9. Stir add meet balls
  10. Cut green onions very fine add in pot stir with parsley flakes
