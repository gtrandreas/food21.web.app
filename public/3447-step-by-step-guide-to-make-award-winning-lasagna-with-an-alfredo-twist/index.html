Steps to make Lasagna with an Alfredo twist:
  1. Preheat oven to 375°.
  2. Brown sausage in medium skillet while you mix the egg, ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheeses in a separate bowl.
  3. In a small loaf baking dish, spray cooking spray on all sides. Layer bottom with marinara sauce.
  4. Start with one pasta layer, then add a layer of the cheese mixture, sausage, equal amounts of sauce, and separated amounts of spices. (if you love cheese, you can add a little on each layer as you go)
  5. Repeat at least three times and save enough cheese mixture and meat to add on top before baking.
  6. Bake at 375° for 40 minutes covered. Remove cover and bake an additional 10 minutes or until the cheese is slightly browned.
