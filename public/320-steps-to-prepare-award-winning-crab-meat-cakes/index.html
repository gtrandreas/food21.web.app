The ingredients needed to make Crab meat cakes:
  1. Make ready 1 pound crab meat
  2. Make ready 1/4 cup cream sauce
  3. Prepare 2 eggs
  4. Prepare 8 ounces mushrooms
  5. Make ready 1/2 cup butter
  6. Take 4 tablespoons flour
  7. Get 2 cups cream
  8. Prepare to taste Salt,pepper paprika
  9. Prepare 1/2 cup cooking sherry
