The ingredients needed to make Banana custard dessert:
  1. Prepare 2 Bananas
  2. Get 500 ml Milk
  3. Get As required Condensed milk for creamy texture & sweetness
  4. Prepare 2 tablespoons Vanilla custard
  5. Prepare Few drop rose essence
  6. Take 1 cup leftover cake crumbs for two layers
  7. Prepare As required roohafza for garnishing it or any other sweet syrup
