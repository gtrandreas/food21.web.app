Instructions to make green authentic enchiladas:
  1. add the tomatillos and jalapenos covered with water
  2. let them boil for 4 minutes
  3. once they are done save 1 cup of the water and throw the rest awaay
  4. add to blender the water and tha jalapenos and tomatillos
  5. add to blender the garlic salt and both buillon powders and blend well
  6. in a pan add oil and the salsa and turn to low med heat
  7. let it boil and the turn off
  8. add 2 tablespoons of oil and turrn up to med heat
  9. fry the tortillas for about 4 seconds on each side
  10. once all tortillas are done,,in a plate add some cheese and roll them up
  11. add the salsa and ur done,,goes best with my mexican rice from my recipes
