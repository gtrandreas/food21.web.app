Instructions to make Quick and dirty Alfredo:
  1. Heat olive oil, herbs, garlic and onion in a small pot until at a simmer.
  2. In a cup mix milk and cold milk. Once thoroughly mixed, add milk to pot with oil.
  3. Bring to boil stirring frequently until boiling. Let simmer 1 minute and remove from heat. Stir often until cool and creamy. Add cheese as sauce cools.
  4. Once sauce cools, put it on something tasty. Anything from pasta to steamed veggies or quinoa.
