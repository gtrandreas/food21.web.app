The ingredients needed to make Failed Fudge Cheesecake:
  1. Take Crust
  2. Make ready 5.5 ounce. sugar free shortbread cookies, I used Murrays Shortbread with pecans and chocolate chips
  3. Get butter, melted
  4. Get Cheesecake
  5. Take 8 ounce each. cream cheese
  6. Take sour cream
  7. Prepare milk
  8. Get eggs, lighly beaten
  9. Take vanilla extract
  10. Make ready butter
  11. Make ready sweetener, like Splenda or Stevia
  12. Get package. sugar free chocolate chips
  13. Take chopped nuts, pecans
Instructions to make Failed Fudge Cheesecake:
  1. Preheat oven to 300°F
  2. FOR THE CRUST
  3. Pulverize cookies to crumbs
  4. Pour melted butter over crumbs and blend well
  5. Press in greased 8 inch spring form pan
  6. Bake at 300°F for 10 minutes
  7. FOR THE FILLING
  8. Melt chocolate chips and butter, set aside
  9. Blend sour cream ,milk, vanilla, eggs, creamcheese, and Splenda until smooth
  10. Stir in chocolate mixture and nuts until blended
  11. Pour over crust
  12. Bake at 300°F for 45 minutes or until center is almost set.
  13. Turn off oven
  14. Leave cheesecake in oven for 1 hour, remove
  15. Let cool for 4 hours
  16. Serve and enjoy
