The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Fudge & white choc chip cookie crumble:
  1. Make ready 300 ml milk
  2. Take 800 grams sugar
  3. Prepare 1 pinch salt
  4. Make ready 40 grams cocoa
  5. Prepare 75 ml golden syrup
  6. Prepare 60 grams butter
  7. Take 10 ml vanilla essence
Instructions to make Chocolate Fudge & white choc chip cookie crumble:
  1. Heat stove top and have a saucepan
  2. add milk, sugar, salt, cocoa and syrup
  3. warm up until sugar dissolves
  4. rinse out the sugar stones
  5. bake until soft on 115Â°F
  6. mix ingeredients slowly
  7. leave in sauce pan until semi-warm
  8. add butter and vanilla essence
  9. mix until fudge thickens
  10. put in pan (200 x 250mm)
  11. let it stand until cooled off
  12. finish off with white choc chip cookie crumble
  13. cut in blocks
  14. if wanted. put in fridge over night
