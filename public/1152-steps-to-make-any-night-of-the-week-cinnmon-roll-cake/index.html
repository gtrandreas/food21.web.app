The ingredients needed to make Cinnmon Roll Cake:
  1. Prepare 6 cups flour
  2. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  3. Make ready 2 cups sugar
  4. Take 8 tsp baking powder
  5. Prepare 3 cups milk (I used non fat)
  6. Get 4 eggs
  7. Take 5 tsp vanilla
  8. Take 1 cup melted butter
  9. Get For the cinnamon brown sugar mixture:
  10. Prepare 2 cups butter softened
  11. Make ready 2 cups brown sugar
  12. Take 4 Tbs flour
  13. Make ready 3 Tbs cinnamon
  14. Make ready For the frosting:
  15. Make ready 24 oz cream cheese
  16. Take 5 cups powdered sugar
  17. Prepare 1 cup milk (I used non fat)
Steps to make Cinnmon Roll Cake:
  1. In a mixing bowl, mix the flour, salt, sugar and baking powder together.
  2. Stir in the milk, eggs and vanilla.
  3. Stir in the melted butter.
  4. Spray non stick cooking oil in baking dish. This recipe was good for two 9x13 dishes. Do not fill baking dish to the top. This cake rises!
  5. Now prep the brown sugar and cinnamon mixture.
  6. Cream all ingredients together.
  7. Reserve 1/4 of the brown sugar mixture and put on the side. You will use this later when the cake is done.
  8. Drop the cinnamon mixture by the spoon full into the cake batter.
  9. Use a knife to swirl it around in the batter.
  10. Bake cake at 350 degrees for 25-30 minutes.
  11. When cake is done, use the reserved brown sugar mixture to frost the top of cake.
  12. Set cake aside and make the cream cheese frosting.
  13. Combine all ingredients for the cream cheese frosting, using an electric mixer, blend until smooth.
  14. Top cake with cream cheese frosting.
