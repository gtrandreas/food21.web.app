Steps to make Cream Cheese asparagus Rolls:
  1. Preheat oven to 375°F
  2. boil about 3 cups of water
  3. Cut the ends off of the asparagus stalks.
  4. Boil the asparagus stalks until limp
  5. dry the asparagus very well
  6. Combine cream cheese, garlic powder and paprika
  7. unroll the unbaked crescents
  8. spread the cream cheese mixture at the end part of the crescent.
  9. place the asparagus on the cream cheese coverd end of the crescent roll and roll it up
  10. place the rolls on an ungreased baking sheet
  11. bake until rolls are golden brown, let cool then enjoy!
