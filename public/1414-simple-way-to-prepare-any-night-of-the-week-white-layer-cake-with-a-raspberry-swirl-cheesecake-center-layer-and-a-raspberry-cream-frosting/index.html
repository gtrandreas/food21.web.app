The ingredients needed to make White Layer Cake with a Raspberry Swirl Cheesecake center layer and a Raspberry Cream Frosting:
  1. Take For Raspberry Swirl Cheesecake
  2. Get 16 ounces cream cheese, at room temperature
  3. Make ready 2/3 cup granulated sugat
  4. Make ready 2 large eggs at room temperature
  5. Make ready 1/8 teaspoon salt
  6. Prepare 2/3 cup sour cream, at room temperature
  7. Get 1/2 cup seedless raspberry jam
  8. Get 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  9. Take 3/4 cup fresh raspberries, cut in half
  10. Take For The White Cake
  11. Make ready 1 cup whole milk
  12. Take 6 large egg whites
  13. Prepare 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  14. Get 2 1/4 cups cake flour
  15. Take 4 teaspoons baking powder
  16. Make ready 1 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  17. Prepare 1 teaspoon salt
  18. Prepare 3/4 cup room temperature butter (1 1/2 sticks)
  19. Take For The Raspberry Cream Frosting
  20. Make ready 2 cups heavy whipping cream
  21. Make ready 8 ounces room temperature cream cheese
  22. Prepare 1 1/2 cups confectioner's sugar
  23. Make ready 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  24. Prepare 1/3 cup seedless raspberry jam
  25. Take For Garnish
  26. Take as needed fresh raspberries
  27. Make ready as needed dark and white chocolate shavings
Instructions to make White Layer Cake with a Raspberry Swirl Cheesecake center layer and a Raspberry Cream Frosting:
  1. Make the Raspberry Cheesecake
  2. Preheat the oven to 325. Spray a 9 inch springform pan with bakers spray. Line a baking sheet with foil.
  3. Beat the cream cheese and sugar nail smooth
  4. Add eggs one at a time and beat in, beat in salt and vanilla
  5. Fold in sour cream until well blended
  6. Gently fold in fresh raspberry halfs
  7. Pour into prepared springform pan. Drop 3 mounds of raspberry jam on top of cheesecake. Using a butter knife or skewer swirl jam through batter
  9. Place cheesecake on foil lined baking sheet and bake 50 to 60 minutes until just slightly jiggly in center. Cool completely on rack until room temperature then wrap entire pan in plastic wrap and frees until hard at least 4 hours or overnight
  10. Make Cake
  11. Preheat oven to 350. Spray 2 - 9 inch cake pans well with bakers spray
  12. In a large bowl whisk together milk, egg whites and vanilla
  13. In another large bowl whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder and salt
  14. Add butter to flour mixture and beat on low until its a crumbly mixture
  15. Add 1/2 of the milk mixture and beat until smooth
  16. Add remaining ingredients milk mixture and beat until well blended
  17. Pour evenly into prepared pans and bake 25 to 30 minutes until a toothpick comes out just clean. Cool on racks 20 minutes. Then remove and cool completely
  19. Make Filling and Frosting
  20. Beat cream until it holds soft peaks in a large bowl
  21. In another bowl beat cream cheese, sugar, vanilla and jam until smooth
  22. Add 1/3 of cream cheese mixture to whipped cream and beat in until its holding its shape
  23. Fold on remaining Icream cheese mixture in two additions until well blended
  24. Assemble Cake
  25. Place one cake layer bottom up on serving plate.
  26. Apply a thin layer of filling
  27. Remove cheesecake from freezer and then pan by running a thin knife around pan and releasing sides. Carefully put a spatula under cheesecake to remove springform bottom plate and place on cake layer, bottom flat side facing up.
  29. Top cheesecake with a thin layer of filling
  30. Place second cake layer on cheesecake layer
  31. Frost entire cake
  32. Decorate with fresh raspberries and dark and white chocolate shavings. Keep cake refrigerated but be sure the cheesecake layer has defrosted before serving. About 4 hours should set the frosting and thaw the cheesecake. Let slices stand at room temperature for about 10 minutes for best flavor.
