The ingredients needed to make Teri's No Bake Chocolate and Oatmeal Cookies:
  1. Prepare sugar
  2. Take Butter
  3. Prepare Milk
  4. Make ready Cocoa
  5. Prepare Peanut Butter (creamy or crunchy)
  6. Get Vanilla
  7. Take Oatmeal
Instructions to make Teri's No Bake Chocolate and Oatmeal Cookies:
  1. Makes 36 cookies
  2. In LG saucepan mix sugar, butter, milk, & cocoa. Stir continually
  3. Bring to a boil. Cook @ boil for 2 minutes
  4. Remove from heat and add peanut butter. Stir till melted
  5. Mix in vanilla
  6. Add Oatmeal
  7. Spoon cookies onto wax paper
  8. Let cool
  9. Will harden.
  10. May need to mix between 1/2 to 1 cup extra oatmeal to stiffen up before putting on wax paper
