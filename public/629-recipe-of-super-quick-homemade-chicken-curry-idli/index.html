The ingredients needed to make Chicken curry idli:
  1. Make ready 4 idli
  2. Take 1/4 tsp mustard seeds
  3. Take 1 onion
  4. Get 1 tomato
  5. Prepare 1 green chilly
  6. Make ready 1/4 tsp pepper powder
  7. Get 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  8. Prepare 1/4 tsp cumin powder
  9. Take 3/4 cup chicken gravy with some chicken pieces
  10. Prepare 1 tbsp oil
  11. Make ready Salt if required

Steps to make Chicken curry idli:
  1. Cut idlis into small pieces
  2. Heat a pan with oil. Add mustard seeds and let it splutter.
  3. Add onion and sauté well. Add tomato and sauté until it becomes soft.
  4. Add cumin powder, pepper powder, turmeric powder and idli pieces.
  5. Stir it and add chicken gravy and chicken pieces. Add salt if it is required
  6. Stir it and leave it in low flame for 10 mins
  7. Serve hot.
