The ingredients needed to make pasta chicken and carrots:
  1. Prepare 3 quart water
  2. Get 5 oz achiotina (pasta de achiote)
  3. Get 1 tbsp celery salt
  4. Make ready 1 tbsp salt
  5. Get 2 tbsp granulated garlic salt
  6. Prepare 16 oz elbow pasta
  7. Take 5 cup carrots cut on a bias
  8. Make ready 6 large breast boiled cut into chunks
  9. Prepare 16 oz penne
  10. Take 2 tbsp margarine
  11. Make ready 1 cup sr flour
  12. Prepare 3 tbsp olive oil
  13. Take 1 pinch ground black pepper on top of your serving

Steps to make pasta chicken and carrots:
  1. add all your spices in water with achiotina add chicken and boil
  2. peel carrots cut on bias put into broth
  3. when it come back to a boil add pasta
  4. cook for 10 minutes on a boil
  5. mix olive oil and flour pour half into broth bring to boil stirring constantly it will thicken add more for thicker consistency let boil stirring constantly so flour will cook as it thickens taste adjust seasoning if needed
