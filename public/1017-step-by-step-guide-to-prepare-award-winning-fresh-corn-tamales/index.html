Instructions to make FRESH CORN TAMALES:
  1. start off by peeling corn, after peeling the corn remove the curnels.
  2. blend the corn with a cup of water.
  3. meanwhile, beat the butter,sugar and baking powder together.
  4. Add the mixture of the corn a cup at a time,then add the corn meal.
  5. Then mix all ingredients together until you see a masa mixture.
  6. meanwhile soak the corn husks in hot water so that you could remove one by one.
  7. Open corn husk an spoon in the mixture in the middle,close the longest side up (if u can tie the edges).
  8. place in a pot open side up all around. (If u can used a evapotdora pot)
  9. cook for one hour and a half til form a tamale
