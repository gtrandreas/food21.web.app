Instructions to make Chicken salad:
  1. In a small bowl combine all the spices, coat the chicken breast with the spices
  2. Cut all the vegetables and add them into your salad bowl
  3. Heat up some oil in a pan about 1 tbsp,add the well coated chicken breast, cook for couple of minutes and flip the other side and cook,until cooked through
  4. Place the chicken breast on a chopping board and cut it into slices
  5. Then place it on top of your salad, serve your chicken salad with any dressing of your choice
