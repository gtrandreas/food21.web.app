##### The ingredients needed to make Stuffed Okra recipe (Lady’s finger/bhindi sabzi):-:

1. Prepare 250 grm Okra  (cut long and slit in between)
1. Get 1/2 tsp Cumin seeds
1. Make ready 1/4 tsp Asafoetida
1. Get as needed Coriander leaves for garnishing
1. Make ready  For the stuffing
1. Get 3 tsp chilli powder
1. Get 4 tsp cumin coriander powder
1. Get 1/2 tsp turmuric powder
1. Make ready 1/4 tsp garam masala powder
1. Take 1/2 tsp garlic paste
1. Prepare as needed oil
1. Make ready to taste Salt Oil




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Stuffed Okra recipe (Lady’s finger/bhindi sabzi):-:

1. Take the masala’s for stuffing in a plate/bowl and add 2 Tsps of oil to it and mix it well.
1. Cut the ends of the okra. Cut into medium size long pieces with a slit in between.
1. Fill the stuffing masala in the okra.
1. Heat the remaining oil in a Kadhai and add cumin seeds, asafoetida then add the stuffed okra.
1. Now mix it well cover the Kadhai with a lid and let it cook till it turns soft.
1. Once done garnish with Coriander leaves/cashewnut and serve hot.
1. Suggested accompaniments include:- - Phulka, roti, Romali roti, Naan, Paratha
