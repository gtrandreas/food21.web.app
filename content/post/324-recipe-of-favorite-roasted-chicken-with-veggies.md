##### The ingredients needed to make Roasted Chicken with Veggies:

1. Make ready 6 chicken thighs skin on bone in
1. Get 3 large carrots
1. Make ready 1/2 large onion chopped
1. Get 1 pound broccoli chopped
1. Prepare 3 teaspoon Herbs De Provence
1. Take 3 teaspoon salt divided
1. Get 8 teaspoon butter




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Roasted Chicken with Veggies:

1. Preheat oven 400 degrees Fahrenheit
1. Heat the skillet with the chicken. When the chicken sizzles turn off the heat add the carrots
1. Chop the broccoli and add.
1. Add 1/3 of salt to the broccoli and herbs De Provence to the chicken and sprinkle. Add onions on top of the chicken, add some of the Herbs De Provence over everything else
1. Add butter over each piece of chicken, and in the middle of dish. Put into oven for 35-40 minutes.
