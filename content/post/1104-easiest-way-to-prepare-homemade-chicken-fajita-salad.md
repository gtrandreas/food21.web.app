##### The ingredients needed to make Chicken Fajita Salad:

1. Get 3 tbsp olive oil
1. Prepare 100 ml freshly squeezed lime juice
1. Make ready 2 tbsp chopped cilantro
1. Get 2 cloves garlic, crushed
1. Get 1 tsp brown sugar
1. Make ready 1/2 tsp cumin
1. Make ready 1 tsp salt
1. Take pinch chili flakes
1. Take 4 chicken thighs skinless boneless
1. Prepare 1/2 yellow pepper
1. Make ready 1/2 red pepper
1. Take 1/2 onion
1. Take 5 cups romaine lettuce
1. Make ready 2 avocados
1. Prepare  sour cream for garnish




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Chicken Fajita Salad:

1. Whisk marinade ingredients together to combine. Pour half the marinade into a shallow dish to marinade the chicken fillets for two hours if time allows. Refrigerate the reserved untouched marinade to use as a dressing.
1. Heat about one teaspoon of oil in a grill pan or skillet over medium-high heat and grill chicken fillets on each side until golden, crispy and cooked through. (Grill in batches to prevent excess water being released.) Once chicken is cooked, set aside and allow to rest.
1. Wipe pan over with paper towel; drizzle with another teaspoon of oil and fry pepper and onion strips until cooked to your liking.
1. Slice chicken into strips and prepare salad with leaves, avocado slices, peppers, onion strips and chicken.
1. Drizzle with remaining marinade/dressing and serve with (optional) extra cilantro leaves and sour cream.
