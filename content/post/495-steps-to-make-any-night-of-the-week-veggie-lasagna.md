##### The ingredients needed to make Veggie Lasagna 🍝 🌱:

1. Make ready 300 g Spinach
1. Make ready 350 g Mushrooms
1. Take 500 g Mozarella (or other cheese of choice)
1. Get 100 g Cottage cheese
1. Get 250 g Lasagna pasta
1. Prepare 800 g Canned diced tomatos (or fresh tomatoes)
1. Make ready 2 Cloves garlic
1. Make ready  Basil (fresh or dried)
1. Make ready  Olive oil
1. Make ready  Salt
1. Take  Pepper




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Veggie Lasagna 🍝 🌱:

1. Slice the mushrooms, mince the garlic and moderately fry them in olive oil and some basil.
1. Combine the diced tomatos and spinach with some basil and fry them until the spinash softens. Add salt and pepper according to choice.
1. Grate half the mozarella and slice the other half. If you prefer, you can use/combine with yellow cheese of vegan cheese. I like using mozzarella because of the creamy structure it gives.
1. Preheat the oven on 180°C.
1. Layer the ingredients in the following order: 1. Lasagna pasta, 2.Tomato&amp;spinach mixture, 3. Lasagna pasta, 4. Mushrooms mixture, 5. Lasagna pasta, 6. Tomato&amp;spinach mixture, 7. Grated mozzarella and cottage cheese, 8. Lasagna pasta, 9. Sliced mozzarella. You can sprinkle some basil on top of the mozzarella.
1. Bake the lasagna for ~40 minutes. If you like the cheese on top crispier, like I do, you can bake it for 50 minutes.
1. Enjoy!
