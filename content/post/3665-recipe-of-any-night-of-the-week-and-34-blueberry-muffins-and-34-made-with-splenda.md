##### The ingredients needed to make &#34; Blueberry Muffins &#34; ( made with splenda ):

1. Take 2 cup flour

1. Prepare 3/4 tsp salt
1. Get 1/2 cup light margarine - softened
1. Prepare 1 cup Splenda
1. Take 1/4 cup honey
