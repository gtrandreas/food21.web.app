##### Instructions to make Chicken and pumpkin sweet curry:

1. Dice and saute the onion, apple, ginger and garlic. Add the spices once onions are translucent.
1. Cut the chicken thighs in to quarters and brown with onions. Add the tomato paste and pumpkin once the chicken is cooked.
1. Fry ingredients for 5 minutes before adding the coconut milk. Cook until the pumpkin is soft, add additional water when required.
1. Add fruit chutney and lemon juice 5 minutes before cooking is complete.
1. Serve with some bas math rice.
