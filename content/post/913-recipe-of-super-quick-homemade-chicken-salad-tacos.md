##### The ingredients needed to make Chicken Salad Tacos:

1. Take 6 small skinless chicken breasts
1. Take 3 tbsp worshershire
1. Make ready 1 1/2 tsp white wine
1. Make ready 1/3 cup seasoned salt
1. Take 1/2 cup house seasoning
1. Take 1/3 onion powder
1. Prepare 1/4 cup curry powder
1. Take 1/4 cup lemon juice
1. Get 1 dash mustard
1. Make ready 1 cup brown rice with italian seasoning cooked in
1. Make ready 1/3 cup carrots
1. Make ready 1 lettuce
1. Get 1 whole grain taco shells (soft)
1. Take 1/2 chopped bell pepper
1. Make ready 1/2 minced onion




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Chicken Salad Tacos:

1. Prep: remove excess fat from chicken
1. slice breasts into desired strips and place in bowl
1. place minced onions and chopped bell pepper in with chicken and mix
1. place all wet seasonings in chicken and mix
1. add each dry seasoning in one at a time, stirring chicken after each ingredient
1. place about 2 tablespoons of e.v.o.o. in a pan on the stove and raise to medium-high heat
1. place chicken strips in pan and constantly stir and flip chicken until chicken is fully cooked (do not brown the chicken)
1. drain excess oil out of pan unless a broth is made by the chicken. repeat step 7 and 8 until all chicken is cooked.
1. prep: rice,   place rice in small pot and add water and italian seasoning
1. once rice is done, fluff and place on cookie sheet to cool (this keeps the grains from sticking)
1. chop carrots to desired size
1. on taco shell: place rice, chicken, lettuce, and carrots in that order
1. enjoy
